# m720_off_laptop_zero_certificates

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

For the `h=7..20` calibration cells used by `midlarge_h7_20`, complete
zero-certificate payloads supply cell parameters, `complete=true` flags, and
zero unpaid non-toral active-core counts.

## Falsifier

A complete calibration cell with nonzero unpaid non-toral active cores, or
missing/false completeness metadata.
