# ef_full_orbit_pole_forcing

- **status:** CONDITIONAL
- **closure:** conditional

## Statement

A genuinely full-field Galois orbit of horizontal extension-fiber components,
neither base-descended nor confined to a proper intermediate subfield, must
meet the extension-pole divisor. Hence the orbit is paid by the existing
extension-pole ledger and cannot form an unpaid hidden-fiber leakage class.

This is now reduced to the proved descent of any pole-free full orbit to a
`B`-defined horizontal cycle and the remaining exclusion of such pole-free
cycles after base/tower/noncontainment removals.

## Falsifier

A full Galois orbit of horizontal components over a non-generating row that is
not base-descended, not subfield-confined, avoids the pole divisor, and
survives noncontainment.
