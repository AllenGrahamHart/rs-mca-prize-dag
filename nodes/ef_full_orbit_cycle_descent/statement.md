# ef_full_orbit_cycle_descent

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

In the `B`-defined extension-fiber alignment incidence, with the
extension-pole divisor also defined over `B`, any full Galois orbit of
horizontal components avoiding the pole divisor has a `B`-defined reduced
cycle as its union. That reduced cycle is still disjoint from the pole
divisor.

## Falsifier

A Galois orbit of components disjoint from a `B`-defined divisor whose reduced
union is not `B`-defined, or whose union meets the divisor despite every
component being disjoint from it.
