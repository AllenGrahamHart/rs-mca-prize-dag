# dli_odd_phase_noncollapse_conductor

- **status:** CONDITIONAL
- **closure:** conditional

## Statement

For every central profile, nonzero frequency, and harmonic `m` in the DLI
ranges, the actual odd-evaluation phase

```text
P_lambda(sigma(y))
```

on the square-root section is geometrically nonconstant/non-Artin-Schreier on
every relevant component, with conductor bounds whose harmonic total is
`o(t)`.

This is now reduced to the standard Artin-Schreier/conductor criterion and
the remaining reduced-pole budget for the actual odd-evaluation phases.

## Falsifier

A central profile and nonzero frequency where the odd-evaluation phase is
geometrically trivial on a component, or where the conductor budget is too
large for the DLI harmonic summation.
