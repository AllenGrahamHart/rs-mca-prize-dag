## Statement

For every official small-t row (Row-C class; the exact list from piece A's window arithmetic): an exact machine-checkable certificate that no primitive t-null block of size b <= C log n exists in that row's domain — by elimination, the resultant route, or MITM (two methods cross-checked on one cell). Certificates are Reading-B determination inputs. In Codex's queue.

## Falsifier

a certificate run FINDING a primitive block at an official row (then the column is charged, not excluded — a major but survivable event)
