# sov_hminus1_coefficient_distribution

- **status:** CONDITIONAL
- **closure:** conditional

## Statement

For official-shape rows and `h in (20,A]`, the coefficient
`[X^{h-1}]L` of anchored-core locators has fibers small enough, after
conditioning on the forced-root higher coefficients, to meet the active-core
budget once transported by first-obstruction sensitivity.

This is now reduced to finite Fourier fiber duality plus the remaining
anchored-core character-sum bound.

## Falsifier

A calibration or official-shape row where the `h-1` locator coefficient has a
fiber above budget after the forced-root conditioning.
