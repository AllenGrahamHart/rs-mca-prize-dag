- **status:** PROVABLE

## Statement

For a fixed post-strip pair, bound the aligned-support family by the exhaustive
per-scale split:

- close pairs, where exchange distance is within the ledger reach;
- far-only sets, where distance independence is controlled by anticode bounds;
- structured/link-dense mass, where globalness identifies the mass as paid.

The three regions overlap exactly in the range certified by
`xr_radius_arithmetic`.
