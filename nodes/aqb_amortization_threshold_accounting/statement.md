# aqb_amortization_threshold_accounting

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

Any `c=2` averaged quotient-box family whose certified shared entropy gain
after all box charges and normalizations is at least `429,645,547` bits clears
the finite AQB deficit at `sigma*`, because `aqb_deficit_arithmetic` proves
the worst-case deficit is strictly below that threshold by more than `0.226`
bits.

## Falsifier

A certified recomputation where the AQB deficit reaches or exceeds
`429,645,547` bits.
