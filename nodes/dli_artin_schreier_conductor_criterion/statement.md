# dli_artin_schreier_conductor_criterion

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

Let `f` be a rational additive phase on a smooth curve component in
characteristic `p`. The associated Artin-Schreier trace sheaf is
geometrically trivial exactly when

```text
f = g^p - g + c
```

for some rational function `g` and constant `c` over the geometric field.
If `f` is not of this form, then after Artin-Schreier reduction its conductor
is bounded by the reduced polar divisor of `f`.

## Falsifier

A non-Artin-Schreier rational phase whose additive trace sheaf is
geometrically trivial, or whose conductor exceeds the reduced polar-divisor
bound.
