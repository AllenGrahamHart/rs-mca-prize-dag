# e22_cofactor_divisor_quotient_gluing

- **status:** CONDITIONAL
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

For E22 mixed/full-petal challengers, the petal-locator divisibility
constraints

```text
L_{T_i}(X) | U(X)L_{Z\C}(X) - a_i L_{C\Z}(X)
```

together with the degree and scalar data of the sunflower model force all
non-tail roots to be saturated on full fibers of a quotient map
`x -> x^M` with `M > t`.

This includes the mixed-petal completion problem and the common-modulus
gluing problem. The common-modulus part is now reduced to the proved dyadic
local-to-common saturation lemma, so this node is conditional on extracting a
single bounded tail plus local dyadic quotient-fiber blocks from the E22
divisor constraints.

## Falsifier

A family satisfying all petal divisor constraints but whose non-tail roots are
not full fibers for any quotient modulus `M > t`.
