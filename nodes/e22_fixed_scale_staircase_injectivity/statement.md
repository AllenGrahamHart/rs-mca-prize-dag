# e22_fixed_scale_staircase_injectivity

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

For a fixed quotient modulus `M`, the locator `L_B(X)G(X^M)` of a tail
`|B| < M` plus full quotient fibers determines the selected fibers and the
tail uniquely. Equivalently, there are no hidden multiplicities inside one
fixed `M` staircase scale.

## Falsifier

Two distinct tails or selected quotient-fiber sets at the same `M` with the
same locator.
