# e22_agreement_cofactor_equations

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

For any listed E22 sunflower codeword `f` and its zero-agreement root set
`Z` inside `C union B0`, writing

```text
f = U L_Z
```

converts every petal agreement on petal `i` into the cofactor equation

```text
U(x) L_{Z\C}(x) = a_i L_{C\Z}(x)
```

after cancelling the common core factor. Thus the mixed/full-petal
support-forcing problem may be studied entirely through these cofactor
equations on petal agreement points.

## Falsifier

A listed codeword and petal agreement point for which the stated cofactor
equation fails after factoring zero agreements.
