# Claim contract

- **Claim:** recovered-line MCA-bad slopes inject into discrepancy
  coordinates.
- **Scope:** finite slopes, arbitrary linear code, arbitrary witness supports
  of size at least `A`.
- **Hypothesis:** the witness codeword is exactly `c_0+z c_1`.
- **Bound:** at most `|T|<=n-A`, stronger than the consumer's `n-A+1` slot.
- **Nonclaim:** no payment of a witness explained by another codeword.
- **Falsifier:** two distinct finite slopes charged to the same nonzero error
  coordinate.
