# e1_exceptional_set_reduction

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

The `e1_fullness` problem reduces to control of an explicit exceptional set of
row primes: every non-quotient `e_1` collision outside the paid antipodal
class forces the admissible row prime to divide one of the nonzero bounded
cyclotomic norms supplied by `collision_norm_criterion`; the same condition is
equivalently a sparse folded kernel vector in the lattice model. Small
swap-distance pairs are already excluded by the proved radius threshold.

Thus, after the proved reduction, the only remaining E1 content is to show
that the admissible prize row primes have negligible incidence with this
exceptional norm-divisor set, or to certify the pinned rows directly.
