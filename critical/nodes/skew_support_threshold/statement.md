# skew_support_threshold

- **status:** PROVED

## Statement

Vandermonde rigidity: a nonzero level-j skew under L_j odd constraints needs active support >= L_j + 1; at prize t = 2^33 the thresholds are 2^{32-j} + 1 per level. In the small-support regime the branch factor is profile-only ({0,1} by parity). (Pro; replayed.)
