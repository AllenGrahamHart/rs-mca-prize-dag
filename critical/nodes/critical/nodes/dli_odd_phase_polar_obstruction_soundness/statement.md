# dli_odd_phase_polar_obstruction_soundness

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

If, for each actual DLI odd-evaluation phase on each relevant square-root
component, a certificate identifies a pole of the Artin-Schreier-reduced phase
with positive order not divisible by `p`, then the phase is not of
Artin-Schreier form

```text
g^p - g + c.
```

## Falsifier

A phase of Artin-Schreier form whose Artin-Schreier-reduced representative has
a positive pole order prime to `p`.
