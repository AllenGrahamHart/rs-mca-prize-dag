- **claim id:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
- **scope:** the six `(RowC, prize) x (1/4,1/8,1/16)` candidate rows
- **quantifier:** per generic received pair `(u,v)`
- **currency:** distinct post-strip live slopes
- **budget:** `8n^3`
- **consumer:** `xr_smallcore_spread_count`, exact `8+8=16` split
- **inside:** dihedral-symmetric and extension-type slopes
- **outside:** same-slope, quotient, tangent, and other explicitly stripped
  columns
- **falsifier:** one sustained transported generic-branch count above `8n^3`
- **nonclaim:** neither raw W-collision moments nor per-chart counts are the
  target
- **paid atlas:** canonical collision components partition the target slopes
  and carry one residual-union chart each

  error rank at most three fits the full `8n^3` allocation

  collision lines into `(s-1)`-subsets; basis-poor lines put the whole selector
  in a small GRK chart
- **paid cogirth branch:** kernel-slice information sets pay full-domain
  selector ranks `4,4,3,11,11,10` on the six rows
- **paid uniform higher-rank compiler:** every `u=v=0` selector cell reduces
  to a bounded `GRS_a` Maxwell core and dual-product-code trade; rank-two
  trades use at most `2(s-1)` active coordinates
- **paid minimum-face classification:** rank-two union `a+2` is an exact
  Plucker face-syzygy space or a common `k+2` near-tangent core; no mixed
  singular branch exists
- **rank-four close:** unconditional on five rows (full-domain cogirth); at
  RowC 1/16 complete for min-rank selector hulls admitting a collision line
  with core-nonloop count m <= 49 (equivalently b <= 47; localized cogirth,
  covering-free) or whose high-core members are all line-covered (CLB3,
  b_* >= 7); the line-free/line-uncovered rank-4 sub-case at RowC 1/16
  remains open (catch #158)
- **next theorem:** bound the component count/union-excess ledger by `8n^3`,

  including the explicit `h-2` near-tangent deficit, and extend comparable
  control to the nonuniform residual
