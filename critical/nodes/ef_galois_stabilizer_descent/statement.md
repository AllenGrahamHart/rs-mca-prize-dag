# ef_galois_stabilizer_descent

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

For the B-defined extension-fiber alignment incidence over an admissible row
`B <= E`, every horizontal irreducible component is classified by its
stabilizer under `Gal(E/B)`: full stabilizer gives base descent; a proper
nontrivial stabilizer gives an intermediate-subfield pullback/tower component;
only trivial stabilizer/full Galois orbit remains as the genuinely full-field
case.
