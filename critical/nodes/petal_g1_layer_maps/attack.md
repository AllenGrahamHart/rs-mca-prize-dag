# Attack plan

## Construct the atlas independently of the list

The atlas index must be recoverable from bounded decomposition data attached
to `U`, not by creating one singleton chart per listed codeword. Candidate
data are a canonical core, quotient scale, retained residual profile, and the
union-injective recovery certificates already proved for distinct-label
residual-free charts.

## Required checks

1. coverage of every top-band full-petal contributor;
2. exact transport to `def:capf-sunflower-layer`;
3. coset-petal compatibility with the scale used downstream;
4. a total chart census `<=n^b1`, jointly over missed cores;
5. the exact exponent fit `b1+b4<=B`.

The F4 scans begin inside supplied local charts and therefore test chart
arithmetic, not arbitrary-word atlas coverage.

## Falsifiers

- an arbitrary-word contributor admitted by the top-band definition but by
  no chart;
- a family whose number of necessary nonempty charts is super-polynomial;
- a chart whose auxiliary map or coset scale fails a downstream hypothesis;
- any proposed `b1` that does not fit the primitive exponent budget.
