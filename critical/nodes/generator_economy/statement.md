## Statement

Construct at least `B*/2^33` pairwise `e_1`-distinct cluster centers whose
nonzero pairwise differences lie in height-budget units times the
multiplicative semigroup generated by `poly(N')` base elements. The proved
germ is the root-difference factorization
`zeta^a-zeta^b=zeta^b(zeta^(a-b)-1)`. The open problem is to lift this to
half-size subset sums without counting exact `e_1` collisions as distinct
centers. The construction must not consume prefix-map fiber counts.
