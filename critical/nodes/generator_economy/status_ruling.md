# Status ruling

`TARGET`. The single-root cyclotomic factorization and the older
antipodal-padding construction are valid germs, but neither supplies the
required number of pairwise distinct `e_1` centers at `N'=128`.

The later signed-8-core proposal is refuted by
`generator_size_budget_check`: its `binom(56,28)` padding factor consists of
exact `e_1` collisions. The remaining obligation is a different
fiber-count-independent construction, or an obstruction that reroutes the
consumer.
