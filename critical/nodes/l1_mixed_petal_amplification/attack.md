- **First bound attempt:** price mixed-petal supports against the
  COLUMN (the Lemma-COL pattern that closed the periodic branch) rather
  than n^6 — the diffuse bucket may be column-small even where it is
  cell-large.
- **Structure:** a mixed support = full petals + defective petals +
  core; the defective-petal positions inject into a shortened code —
  check whether the K4/GRK chart machinery prices the defective part.
- **Falsification first:** measure the mixed-petal census growth across
  scales (extend catch #176's single cell to a grid; the wz/cp Modal
  harness patterns apply directly). A super-poly trend kills; a poly
  trend calibrates the target constant.
