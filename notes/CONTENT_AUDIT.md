# Content audit ledger (pre-submission verification pass)

Adversarial audit of every PROVED critical node: does the artifact establish
the FULL statement, or a weaker cousin? One line per node. FLAGS ONLY — the
maintainer triages every flag and performs any status change on replay.

Legend: OK = artifact establishes the full statement | MINOR = hygiene/wording
| MAJOR = a referee would bounce it (gap, but likely fixable) | BLOCKER =
the artifact does NOT establish the statement (status is wrong).
