# sov_first_obstruction_value_distribution

- **status:** CONDITIONAL
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

For official-shape rows and `h in (20,A]`, the coefficient-sensitive first
obstruction `O_{h-1}` has fibers small enough on anchored cores to meet the
active-core budget.

## Conditional decomposition

This node is conditional on:

- `sov_first_obstruction_sensitivity`;
- `sov_hminus1_coefficient_distribution`.

## Falsifier

A calibration or official-shape row where a fiber of `O_{h-1}` is above
budget, or a structural constant-family for `O_{h-1}`.
