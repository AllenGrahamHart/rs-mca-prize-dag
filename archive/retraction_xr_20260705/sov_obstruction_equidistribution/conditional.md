# conditional: sov_obstruction_equidistribution

## Predicate nodes

- `sov_first_obstruction_sensitivity`
- `sov_first_obstruction_value_distribution`

## Claim

Conditional on the value-distribution theorem for the first obstruction, the
SOV equidistribution predicate holds.

## Proof

The proved sensitivity predicate identifies `O_{h-1}` as the correct
coefficient-sensitive first obstruction: changing the `X^{h-1}` coefficient
of the locator changes `O_{h-1}` by exactly the opposite amount while leaving
the forced root fixed.

The remaining value-distribution predicate supplies the actual small-fiber
bound for this obstruction on anchored cores at official-shape rows and
`h in (20,A]`.

Together these predicates say that the obstruction used by SOV is genuinely
sensitive in the intended coefficient direction and has fibers below the
active-core budget. That is the equidistribution statement required here.
