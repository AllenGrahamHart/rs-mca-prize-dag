# dli_odd_eval_exponential_sum_bound

- **status:** CONDITIONAL
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

For every central profile and every nonzero frequency, the normalized odd
low-degree evaluations on the square-root section have Weyl sums small
enough, uniformly over the harmonic ranges required by
`dli_et_peak_mass_reduction`, with total DLI error `sum_j eps_j=o(t)`.

## Conditional decomposition

This node is conditional on:

- `dli_deligne_weyl_transfer`;
- `dli_odd_phase_noncollapse_conductor`.

## Falsifier

A central profile, nonzero frequency, and harmonic range whose Weyl sum is too
large to feed the peak-mass reduction.
