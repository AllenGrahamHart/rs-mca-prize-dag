# sov_active_core_obstruction_vanishing

- **status:** TARGET
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

Every active `h`-core in the intended `h > 20` band corresponds to a finite
`h`-trade whose locator `L` satisfies the forced-root obstruction equations,
so all obstruction coordinates from `sov_forced_root_recursion_algebra`
vanish.

## Falsifier

An active `h`-core whose locator is not in the square-shift trade model or has
a nonzero forced-root obstruction coordinate.
