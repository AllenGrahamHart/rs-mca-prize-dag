# Claim contract

- **claim:** the pendant, `K_4-e`, `K_4`, and triangle-plus-singleton
  branches have the rational transversals and exact split-member counts in
  `(RTA1)--(RTA7)`; together with the path theorem, all thirteen printed
  deficit-free pencils have exactly three fully domain-split members at the
  official scale.
- **scope:** every field and distinct `4d`-point evaluation domain supporting
  the canonical split-pencil normal form; the printed thresholds range from
  `d>=4` to `d>=13` and include `d=2^39`.
- **requirements:** `rate_half_list_budget_three_split_fiber_atlas`,
  `rate_half_list_budget_three_plucker_edge_gate`, and
  `rate_half_list_budget_three_path_mobius_transversal`.
- **consumer effect:** removes every residual hidden-fourth-member attack and
  replaces four unnormalized incidence types by explicit low-degree graph
  fibers; no status promotion.
- **nonclaim:** no incidence type is excluded, no simultaneous pencil
  coupling or subgroup-factorization theorem is proved, and no list crossing
  is located.
- **falsifier:** a canonical branch violating a displayed fiber formula, a
  constant claimed-nonconstant transversal with distinct edge roots, or an
  additional fully domain-split member above the printed threshold.
