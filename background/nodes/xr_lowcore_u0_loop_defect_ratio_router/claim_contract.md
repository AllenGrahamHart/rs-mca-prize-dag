- **scope:** RowC rank-five P-B cells with `u=0` and `v>=1`
- **currency:** distinct retained slopes after at most `v` loop exceptions
- **input:** `xr_rank_five_extension_list_reduction`
- **output:** exact ratio identity, denominator-zero cap, reused-core line
  cap, and multiplicity-sensitive rich-core router
- **load-bearing hypotheses:** P-B actual-agreement intersection cap `3+v`,
  distinct slopes, nonpersistent kernel loops, and the `GRS_4` conclusion at
  `u=0`
- **consumer:** `xr_lowcore_spread_heart`
- **nonclaim:** no current residual `(u,v)` cell or aggregate P-B target is
  paid by this router alone
- **failure certificate:** two retained members through one four-core with
  at least `v` additional zero-denominator agreement points, or an
  over-budget block family violating the rich-core peeling conclusion
