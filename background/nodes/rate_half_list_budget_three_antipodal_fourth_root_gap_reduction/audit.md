# Audit

The uniqueness statement is limited to residual degree at most three. Above
that threshold the difference polynomial can have low enough degree to remain
inside the allowed residual range.

The formal fourth root is normalized by constant term one. Choosing another
fourth root would break the monic normalization `B(0)=1`.

The gap in `(FRG5)` is exact: `a_(qh)` is nonzero. This uses both exact
reverse contact from the dependency and the fact that four is invertible.

The `eta_j` in recurrence `(FRG4)` are coefficients of the reversed deleted
divisor `E`; the `e_j` used to define `q` are the centered outer coefficients.
