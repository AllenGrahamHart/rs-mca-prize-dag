# Claim contract - L1 full-pullback divisibility/Johnson closure

## Inputs

- an official dyadic row with `k>1` and `2<=ell=sigma+1<=n-k+1`;
- a nontrivial dyadic scale `s|n` and `s|ell`;
- a monic degree-`s` map partitioning the complete domain;
- exact agreement supports that are unions of its complete fibers.

## Output

The exact source-threshold shell is empty in this class. Under
`h_0^2>bd`, each fixed map contributes at most the integer in `(FJ5)`, and
all tame fixed-source map classes contribute at most `n^3`.

## Falsifier

A fully fiberwise support of exact size `k+ell-1`; a pair of distinct quotient
tuples agreeing on more than `d` labels; a list violating `(FJ5)`; or failure
of the dyadic simplification `(FJ6)`.

## Nonclaims

The sub-Johnson, partial-fiber, sparse-coverage, wild, unanchored, and
arbitrary-locator branches remain open.
