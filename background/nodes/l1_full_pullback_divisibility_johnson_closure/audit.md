# Audit - L1 full-pullback divisibility/Johnson closure

1. Exact agreement support, not merely one source petal, is required to be a
   union of complete fibers.
2. The official `k>1` guard excludes the only `k congruent 1 mod s` endpoint.
3. Both dyadic orderings `s<=k` and `s>k` are handled.
4. The threshold is `k+ell-1`; the next allowed multiple is not rounded down.
5. Full domain partition makes the pullback evaluation injective.
6. Distinct interleaved tuples have common agreement at most `d`.
7. Agreement sets are trimmed to exactly `h_0` before Cauchy--Schwarz.
8. Strict Johnson positivity is retained in the denominator.
9. The list proof is independent of label-domain smoothness and field size.
10. The tame map count is union-bounded only after the per-map theorem.

The live full-partition residual is exactly the nonpositive Johnson gate;
partial and sparse-coverage branches remain separate.
