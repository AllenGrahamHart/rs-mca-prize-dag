# Claim contract - L1 raw support-ledger exponential route fence

## Inputs

- the printed right-hand sides of `pma_petal_pattern_root_pinning_ledger` and
  `l1_maximal_background_anchor_injection`;
- only the elementary maximal-source, list-threshold, and per-petal-cap
  inequalities.

## Output

One infinite arithmetic family of balanced formal profiles on which both raw
ledger strategies assign an exponential allowance.

## Consumer rule

Do not propose a large run that merely enumerates support patterns or sums
either printed local allowance. A future run must test an additional
algebraic feasibility predicate, collective injection, or canonical paid
owner theorem.

## Nonclaims

No formal profile is claimed realizable. No lower bound on an RS list is
proved. The two local ledgers remain valid and useful after stronger algebraic
deletions.

## Falsifier

An arithmetic failure in `(RF1)--(RF7)`, or a proof that raw evaluation of
either displayed expression is polynomial on the stated family.
