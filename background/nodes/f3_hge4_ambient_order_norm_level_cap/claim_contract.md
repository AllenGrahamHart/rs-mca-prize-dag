# Claim contract

- **binding claim:** `(AON1)--(AON4)` in `statement.md`.
- **scope:** official `n=2^s`, `13<=s<=41`, official prime `p`, exact
  dyadic level `m=2^a|n`, and primitive ordered top shift pairs.
- **dependencies:** the exact-level decomposition and the proved
  cyclotomic-norm/odd-Parseval argument.
- **falsifier:** one primitive pair in scope violating `(AON1)`, or one pair
  above the cap `(AON3)` or inside the parity deletion `(AON4)`.
- **nonclaim:** no count is supplied below the cap, and no HGE4 status is
  changed.

The ambient exponent `s` is load-bearing. Replacing it by the exact-level
exponent `a` recovers only the old quarter cap and discards the theorem's
proper-level gain.
