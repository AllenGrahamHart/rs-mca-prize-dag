# Claim contract

- **claim:** on a two-antipodal `c=2` denominator, six selected pairs collapse
  to three explicit trace classes and the whole chamber shares the existing
  CR-002 Jacobi torsion norm pair.
- **scope:** the maximal official generic `c=2` parity subbranch.
- **dependencies:** exact parity gap reduction, mismatch trace coupling,
  doubled Jacobi norm transfer, and pure fourth-root exclusion.
- **falsifier:** a selected pair outside the three trace classes, a wrong
  coupling norm, or a parity primary/torsion survivor not screened by
  `R_-,R_+`.
- **nonclaims:** parity is not forced for a general denominator and neither
  shared norm is evaluated.
