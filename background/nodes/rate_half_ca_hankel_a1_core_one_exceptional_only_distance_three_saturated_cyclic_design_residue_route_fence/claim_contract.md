# Claim contract

- **Claim:** exact biregularity, quadratic rank `3e+1`, and complement span
  `e+4` coexist in one explicit split-divisor design; its residue rank is at
  least four for every admissible degree-`e` internal locator.
- **Scope:** an abstract `e=5`, `F_151`, order-15 cyclic design matching all
  three printed numerical interfaces. It is not an official Hankel packet.
- **Dependencies:** the quadratic locator and complement-residue gates whose
  weaker interfaces are being tested.
- **Consumer:** the saturated generic attack and CR-003 classifier design.
- **Falsifier:** a failed block/replication count, a quadratic rank other than
  16, a complement span other than nine, or a degree-five `I` with nonzero
  constant term and residue rank at most three.
- **Nonclaim:** no pair-Lagrange generator, boundary packet, official support,
  or resultant-power survivor is constructed.
