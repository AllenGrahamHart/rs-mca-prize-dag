# Claim contract

## Proved content

Every tangent exact-shell member contributes an explicit number of confluent
value/derivative packets, and the packet families of distinct codewords are
disjoint.  In the balanced band, role-labelled tangent/complement packets are
also disjoint. Equations `(HP2)--(HP8)` are exact integer inequalities over
every field and every distinct evaluation domain. Equations `(HP9)--(HP10)`
give the exact unimodal optimizer of the mixed family.

## Required hypotheses

- `P` has degree below `k`.
- `S_P` is the complete agreement set of common size `a`.
- `D_P=gcd(L_P,Q_P)` is computed from the polynomial interpolant of `U`.
- Evaluation points are distinct, so the linear factors and Hasse
  multiplicities are well defined.

## Not proved

No claim is made that the packet ceiling fits an official finite reserve or
is subexponential for every linear profile.  Primitive-section flatness,
quotient absorption, and puncture stability remain open.
