# Result

The low-core cap gives a unique `c`-subset certificate for every residual in
an oriented difference of width `t=K-c`. This yields the exact fiber bound

```text
floor(binom(N-2K+2c,c)/binom(H+c-1,c))
```

and pays several near-`K` widths together in the P-B difference-energy
ledger. The paid codimension prefixes are `2,1,1,6,5,4` on the six clean-rate
rows. Cross-difference slope counting and the deeper width range remain open.
