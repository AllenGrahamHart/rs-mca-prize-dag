   boundary value depends on all other `theta_i` and is not part of the
   theta-free interpolation.
2. The denominator `(xi_l-xi_k)` occurs twice: once in `(QTK3)` and once in
   `W_lk(xi_l)`.
3. The matrix is independent of `lambda_i` only after extracting
   `q_e(a)=lambda_kr_k(a)` pairwise. Its kernel vector is `lambda_i^3`, not
   `lambda_i` or the later `theta_i`.
4. At `e=3`, a quartic is not determined by its residue modulo the
   degree-four polynomial `A^(l)`. No internal-slice equation is available.
5. Full rank or a coloop is an exact exclusion. A deletion-stable deficient
   kernel is only a survivor: it still owes a coordinatewise cube vector and
   every later perfect-power, rank, and source gate.
6. The deterministic full-rank controls do not realize external split
   designs and do not prove uniform full rank.
