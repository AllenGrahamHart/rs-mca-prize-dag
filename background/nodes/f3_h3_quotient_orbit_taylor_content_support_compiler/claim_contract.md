# Claim contract

- **claim:** the positive odd coefficient content of the orbitwise
  cutoff-35 Taylor resultant has exactly the odd prime support of the exact
  orbit scalar annihilator.
- **scope:** every dyadic order and every odd prime; the C36 interpretation
  is at official split primes.
- **dependencies:** canonical monic orbit polynomials and the exact
  quotient-algebra common-root support theorem.
- **falsifier:** an odd prime for which the Taylor-resultant content and the
  orbit scalar annihilator have different divisibility, or a zero
  characteristic-zero Taylor resultant.
- **nonclaims:** no equality of valuations or principal ideals, official
  computation, coefficient bound, factorization, resource estimate, moment
  bound, or C36' close is supplied.
