# Audit

The following details are load-bearing.

1. The parameter table uses half-orders `N=256,512`; `omega` has order
   `2N`.
2. The normalization inverses are `205 mod 512` and `439 mod 1024`.
3. The surviving odd elementary coefficient contributes `-bX^2`.
4. The descended polynomial is
   `G=Y A^2-(bY+1)^2`, including the cross term `-2bY`.
5. The converse chooses `rho=(by+1)/A(y)`; squarefreeness proves its
   denominator cannot vanish.
6. A unit ideal over `Q` gives only a finite bad-characteristic certificate,
   not an official exclusion before `Delta_ell` is checked and factored.

Raw support fleets and finite prime windows are no longer the preferred
proof endpoints for these two slots.
