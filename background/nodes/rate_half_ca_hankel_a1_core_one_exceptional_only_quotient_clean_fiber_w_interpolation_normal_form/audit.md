# Audit

1. The value denominator in `(QWI2)` is nonzero by first-jet transversality.
2. Exactly `r` distinct selected roots determine a degree-at-most-`r-1`
   polynomial in `Y`.
3. Exactly `d` distinct clean slopes determine every coefficient of `W_0`
   at parameter degree less than `d`.
4. The two residual polynomials come from the sharp bound
   `deg_t W_vee<=d+1`; no hidden higher-degree correction is retained.
5. This is coefficient interpolation only. It does not assert that arbitrary
   clean-fiber data satisfy the exceptional, Hankel, or unit equations.
