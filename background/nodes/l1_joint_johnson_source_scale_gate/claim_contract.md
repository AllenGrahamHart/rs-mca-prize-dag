# Claim contract - L1 joint-Johnson source-scale gate

## Inputs

- the joint core/background Johnson payment;
- the separate core-defect Johnson payment for zero background;
- the official maximal-sunflower source equation;
- fixed petal polarity and the L1 lower cutoff.

## Output

Every bounded-polarity live cell satisfies `(SG4)`. All smaller official
source scales are polynomial per source chart with bound `(SG3)`.

## Consumer rule

On the bounded-polarity branch, consumers may impose the four rate-specific
lower bounds in `(SG5)`. They may not apply this gate to growing petal
polarity or treat the arithmetic family `(SG6)` as an existence witness.

## Falsifier

A bounded-polarity nonpositive-Johnson cell with `N+b<4ell`, or an official
source satisfying `M<3(r-1)` and `N+b>=4ell`.
