# Audit

1. The even moments are indexed by `k=2^(a+1)b`, not `2^a b`; the latter
   would double-count the odd norm.
2. The exact Haar relation is shared across scales. Replacing it by one copy
   of `L` per norm loses the endpoint.
3. `Delta_a=0` is structural divisibility by `Phi_(N_a)`, not permission to
   discard that moment without its exact power-of-two factor.
4. The row-prime exponents count distinct odd Galois conjugates only.
5. The `h=4` margin uses the support-union Taylor multiplicity modulo two.
   Merely applying continuous AM--GM misses the smallest official row.
6. The DP enumerates aggregate states of all `3^4` local sign assignments at
   each quarter orbit; it is not a sample of supports.
7. The endpoint theorem closes one complete exact level. It does not turn
   zero width debit into an estimate for any higher level.
