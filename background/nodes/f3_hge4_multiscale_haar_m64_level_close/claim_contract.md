# Claim contract

- **claim:** the multiscale norm product `(MHN6)`, uniform gate `(MHN8)`,
  sharp endpoint inequality `(MHN9)`, and complete zero-cost `m=64` level
  payment `(MHN10)`
- **scope:** official ambient rows, primitive exact-ratio HGE4 pairs, and the
  lower-quarter norm interface
- **dependencies:** exact-ratio orbit compiler and ambient norm-level
  contraction, including its quarter/complement dependencies
- **machine closure:** exhaustive 28,171-state quarter-orbit DP, exact
  rational subset gates, and exact ambient endpoint arithmetic
- **nonclaims:** no orbit estimate at `m>=128`, no full HGE4 aggregate, and no
  claim that every cyclotomic-zero support is itself imprimitive
- **falsifier:** a support state omitted by the DP, a failed subset gate at
  `h=5`, or an official primitive pair at exact level `m=64`
