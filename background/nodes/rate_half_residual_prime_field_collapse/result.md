# Result

Both unresolved rate-half budgets admit prime fields only. LTE first reduces
the extension degree to `1,2,3,4`; exact root-class intersection leaves `46`
quadratic candidates and no cubic or quartic candidates, and a compact
divisor ledger proves every quadratic candidate composite.

All remaining rate-half Hankel, split-pencil, and distance-three work may
therefore assume `F=F_p` with `p>2^167>2^41`. No extension-field or
generated/base-field normalization shard remains in these two budgets.
