# Result

The exact ideal-star candidate sieve can be executed by finite-field fiber
grouping at one primitive root. Explicit ideal normal forms and separate
enumeration of all degree-one primes above each rational prime are unnecessary.

Complete bounded evidence at `n=32,64` compresses the principal-norm prime
lists `103 -> 18` and `2,127 -> 162`. Those counts are evidence for route
selection, not part of the proof or an official-order result.
