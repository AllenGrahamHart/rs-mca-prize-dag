# ATTACK - m720_off_laptop_zero_certificates

The WSL-safe complete cells are proved by
`m720_wsl_complete_zero_subcertificates`. Use off-laptop execution or completed
Modal logs for the residual cells. Do not run the residual full scan on the
limited WSL laptop.

The certificate must include, for every claimed zero cell:

- row and h parameters;
- `complete=true`;
- unpaid non-toral active-core count equal to zero;
- enough metadata to distinguish complete cells from window slices.
