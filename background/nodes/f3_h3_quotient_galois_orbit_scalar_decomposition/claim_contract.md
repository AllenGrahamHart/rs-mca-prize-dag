# Claim contract

- **claim:** ordered nonidentity quotient lifts at order `2^s` form exactly
  `3(2^s-s-1)` odd-Galois orbits with the printed degree histogram, and the
  radical support of the cutoff-35 scalar annihilator is exactly the union of
  the orbitwise scalar-annihilator supports. The top-`r` workload counts and
  degree masses are the exact sums printed in `(QOD4a)`.
- **scope:** every dyadic order, with the candidate interpretation restricted
  to official split primes as in the quotient-algebra compiler.
- **dependencies:** characteristic-zero quotient Sidonicity, the general
  finite quotient-algebra support theorem, and the cutoff-35 C36 bridge.
- **falsifier:** an orbit of the wrong size, a histogram mismatch, two
  quotient pairs in different declared orbits with equal characteristic-zero
  quotient, a top-class workload mismatch, or an odd prime appearing on only
  one side of `(QOD8)`.
- **nonclaims:** total algebraic degree is not reduced; no orbit polynomial,
  annihilator, factorization, valuation, moment bound, or C36' close is
  computed.
