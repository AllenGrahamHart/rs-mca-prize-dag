# Result

`PROVED`. The cutoff-35 quotient algebra splits for support purposes into
`3(n-s-1)` Galois-orbit blocks with degree histogram
`3(2^j-1)` at degree `2^j`. At `n=8192` this gives `24,534` independently
checkable blocks of degree at most `4,096`. The maximum-degree class contains
`12,285` blocks and `75.009%` of total degree; the top two classes contain
`93.757%`. Contributor pilots must therefore include the maximum-degree
class and production shards should be balanced by degree, not block count.
