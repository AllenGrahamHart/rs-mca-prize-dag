# Claim contract

- **claim:** the official exactly-one-antipodal `c=2` primary/torsion packet
  is equivalently the coupled product/ratio trace circuit `(PRT6)`, and its
  trace variable always descends to the prime field.
- **scope:** the maximal official normalized `c=2` chamber with one known
  antipodal denominator pair.
- **dependencies:** the sign-free one-antipodal reducer and official
  torsion-field router.
- **falsifier:** a valid pair that fails `(PRT3)--(PRT8)`, or a passing
  `(P,Z)` circuit that does not reconstruct a distinct torsion pair.
- **nonclaims:** the circuit is not proved empty; no secondary-gap,
  canonical-span, completion-coupling, or antipodal-free exclusion is made.
