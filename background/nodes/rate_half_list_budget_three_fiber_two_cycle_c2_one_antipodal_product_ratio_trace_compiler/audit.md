# Audit

1. Product torsion and ratio torsion are not sufficient separately. Their
   half-order signs must agree through `Z_39=2P_39`.
2. The trace tower has 39 updates because it stops at exponent `N/2`.
3. The two choices of a square root of `Pt` are the common-sign symmetry, not
   two new packets.
4. Trace inversion swaps `c,d`; it does not create a labelled branch.
5. In the reciprocal field chamber, `Z` is prime-field fixed but `P` need
   not be. Replacing `P^p=P^(-1)` by `P^p=P` is invalid.
6. The compiler removes the old `X`-square gate but proves no emptiness.
