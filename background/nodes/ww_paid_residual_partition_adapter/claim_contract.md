# Claim contract

- **universe:** all list words for one exact row, word, and threshold.
- **input:** ordered paid predicates, sound upper allocations, and a disjoint
  residual-cell partition with sound upper allocations.
- **output:** an exhaustive disjoint paid/residual ledger and the exact
  additive budget implication.
- **fail-closed property:** an unrecognized word enters the residual catch-all;
  it is never discarded.
- **nonclaim:** no paid formula, residual grammar, generated-field provenance,
  or residual upper bound is supplied.
- **consumer disposition:** `ww_row_envelope_clause` is an open but retired
  `TARGET`; this abstract partition theorem remains valid as support.
