# Audit

The old W3 frontier listed a full-scope paid/residual ownership theorem as
missing. That overstates the obligation. Once paid predicates are explicitly
defined, first-match guards plus a residual complement are automatically
total and disjoint by the existing general theorem.

The repair does not assume that the paid predicates are mathematically useful
or correctly priced. Their first-match upper allocations must still be proved,
and every word not captured by them remains in the residual whose W3 upper
bound is open. Thus the adapter removes bookkeeping risk without hiding the
counting problem.
