# Audit

1. The right side `E Y^N_sq` has no coefficient below `N_sq`; the
   reconstruction only uses `k<r<N_sq`.
2. `l_0P_cl` is invertible modulo `f_0` by two separately proved gcds.
3. The term containing the new `w_k` occurs only as `l_0w_k`; every other
   convolution term uses a lower `W_vee` coefficient.
4. The affine representative is unique because `deg f_0=e>2`.
5. Exact division in `(QTA6)` is part of the certificate; a modular equality
   alone is insufficient.
6. Coefficients of `S` at and above `r` are not allocated. They come from
   the final exact quotient `(QTA7)` and must satisfy its degree box.
