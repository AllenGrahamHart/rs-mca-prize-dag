# Claim contract - PMA petal-pattern root-pinning ledger

## Inputs

- maximal sunflower source and exact core-defect reduction;
- exact-defect saturation and selected-support maximal rank;
- the L1 lower cutoff for the polynomial aggregate corollary.

## Output

A field-independent `(d,t,h)` ledger with no background-support multiplier,
and a polynomial closure for every fixed `u+e` region.

## Promotion evidence

1. proof that maximality forces `h>d`;
2. selected-petal-support application of maximal rank and core-root pinning;
3. injectivity of `(petal pattern,F)` into codewords;
4. exact pattern aggregation and lower-cutoff arithmetic;
5. exhaustive small-field verifier with failed-hypothesis controls;
6. evidence edges only, with no promotion of the consumer.

## Nonclaims

This theorem does not control unbounded `u+e`, provide a profile owner, prove
the primitive image-scale theorem, or close `imgfib`.
