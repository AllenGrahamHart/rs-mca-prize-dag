field, evaluation subgroup, spending-cell parameters, layout construction,
six distinct labels, and a factored non-planted polynomial.  Its list has at
least seven members while `B*=6`. The proof is algebraic; no exhaustive field
or list enumeration is used.

placed inside the allowed petals. The cell is, however, the globally unsafe
spending cell. It therefore refutes an all-cell extension of W3, not W3's
literal safe-side quantifier.
