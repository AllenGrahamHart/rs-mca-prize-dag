# Claim contract

- **claim:** every retained genuine tangent to an `A`-agreement lifted pair
  is already a bad slope for the original received pair at agreement `A+1`;
  the union over all terminal pairs is charged once to that stricter set.
- **scope:** every root or lifted explanation in a canonical XR mismatch
  descent for an original length-`n` instance.
- **currency:** distinct terminal genuine-tangent slopes.
- **consumer effect:** replace explanation-list breadth by a one-step
  agreement carry, with at most `n-A` possible raises.
- **nonclaims:** no bound on the carried set, no profile preservation, no
  generic-chart aggregate, and no bridge closure.
- **falsifier:** a retained terminal tangent slope whose tangent codeword has
  fewer than `A+1` agreements with the original line word.
