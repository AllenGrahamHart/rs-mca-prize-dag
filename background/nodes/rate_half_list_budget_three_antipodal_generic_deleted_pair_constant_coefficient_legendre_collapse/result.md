# Result

The first deleted-pair scalar rejection no longer requires reconstructing any
coefficient of `S`. Its input is the single classical coefficient

```text
H_(4M-1)(t)=[z^(4M-1)]((1-z)(1-tz))^(-1/2).
```

The three exact necessary equations are `(LCC6)`. This turns CR-002 into a
torsion-restricted Legendre nonvanishing problem before any full Euclidean,
gcd, or fourth-power calculation.

The deleted-pair sublane remains open. The useful next result is a uniform
nonvanishing proof, a coverage-proved fast holonomic evaluator with compact
certificates, or a factorization/resultant argument specialized to
`4M-1=2^37-1` and the official torsion family. A direct recurrence of that
length is not the requested endpoint.
