# Audit

- The characteristic exclusions are harmless on official rows, where
  `p>=n^2` and `n>=8192`.
- The curve fixes trace and product, hence `(e1,e3)`. Przemek's rational
  rich-curve chart fixes `(e1,e2)` and is not an interchangeable theorem.
- There are at most three singular trace values, but they are not deleted;
  their nodal genus-zero fibers still require the original predicates.
- The factor four in `(EC5)` orders only the two residual roots in each
  decorated triple. Endpoint order is already present in `K`, and the
  quotient representation is already ordered.
- A Weil bound for all field points on one `C_sigma` does not bound its
  subgroup points or the quotient-weighted pair correlation.
