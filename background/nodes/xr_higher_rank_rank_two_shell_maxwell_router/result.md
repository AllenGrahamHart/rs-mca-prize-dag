# Result

Every uniform higher-rank rank-two trade now lies on an explicit
degree-versus-arity ladder, and its active block family has a closed-form
Maxwell deficit certificate. Positive deficit rules out a primitive
full-core rank-two anomaly.

This removes primitive full-core rank two on the first residual shell through
kernel ranks `2^34-3` or `2^33-3` at the prize rows, and on the second shell
through `2^33-3` or `2^32-3`. Proper local circuits are not removed; their
first-match ownership and aggregate count are now the precise remaining
low-shell issue.
