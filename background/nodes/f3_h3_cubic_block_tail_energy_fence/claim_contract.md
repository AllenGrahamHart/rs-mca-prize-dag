# Claim contract

## Inputs tested

- the exact quotient-block mass identity;
- the identity contribution `(n-1)^2` to quotient energy;
- a cubic tail on the number of rich quotient blocks, with explicit constant
  four.

## Conclusion

Those inputs alone do not imply the proved `145/4` clean-energy endpoint on
the full official order range.

## Nonclaims

The constructed profiles need not be subgroup-realizable. The node does not
falsify C36', the energy premise, Mit'kin's theorem, or any proved tail. It
only fences a marginal deduction and requires future work to retain more
structure than block sizes and their cubic tail.
