# Result

Every coarse p-free collision is encoded injectively by its exchanged anchor
subset and a Wronskian polynomial. Tail excess `j` gives certificate degree
`2j` in even prefix depth and `2j+1` in odd depth, with an exact full-support
certificate census. This proves the max-fiber neighbor bound `(WNC5)`. The
only potentially exponential axis left in that bound is the choice of the
exchanged subset `X`. The tame-tail upgrade removes every formal stratum
below `tau_p`; official checkpoint runs therefore begin at width at least
`p>n/24`.
