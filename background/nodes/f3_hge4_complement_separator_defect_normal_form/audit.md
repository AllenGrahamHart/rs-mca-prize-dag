# Audit

1. `G` is the difference of the separator cofactors, not the original
   constant locator difference `d` and not the complement locator `R`.
2. Exact degree uses cancellation in `PG+dB=2`; a mere upper bound would lose
   the near-third defect parameter.
3. The leading coefficient contains `d^2`, including its sign. Dropping one
   factor of `d` breaks the differential identity on exact fixtures.
4. `(SDN4)` is necessary, not sufficient. A formal polynomial solution must
   still reconstruct monic `P,Q`, disjoint simple roots in `mu_m`, a nonempty
   complement, and the declared exact ratio and primitive orbit.
5. Bounded `e` is a classification opportunity only. Widths farther below
   one third have growing `e`, and no uniform count for them is proved.
6. The `e=1,2` statement refers to the closest arithmetically possible width,
   not to the existence of a shift pair at that width.
