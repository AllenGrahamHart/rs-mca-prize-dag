# Audit

- The six Mobius values represent permutations of one root multiset. They are
  not six independent DSP8 records; any canonicalization must restore the
  exact decoration and repeated-root multiplicities.
- The four target-zero factors are only four of the six positive-overlap
  factors. The remaining orbit relations are still forbidden by signed
  disjointness even when the displayed target numerator is nonzero.
- Positive disjointness does not imply signed disjointness. All nine negative
  root-collision tests remain load-bearing.
- `(NDP6)` removes only the forbidden target `t=1`. It is not a bound for the
  live fibers and no uniform irreducibility claim is made.
- The subgroup branch tests `(NTP2)`, the richness cutoff `P(t)>=25`, the
  antipodal class, and `R(t)` must all be retained after this pruning.
