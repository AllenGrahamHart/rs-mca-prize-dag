# Audit

1. For `e>0`, the leading coefficient of `Theta` is `-e bc`; official
   characteristic is larger than `e`, so it cannot vanish accidentally.
2. The parity correction in `d_e` is necessary because `J=A(z)+A(-z)`.
   Odd top degree cancels; even top degree doubles in odd characteristic.
3. Both `d_e` and the ordinary subgroup-root count are even, so the residual
   support defect `eta` is even.
4. The Euler divisibility is global, but the cube conclusion uses
   `deg C=H-3`, which is divisible by three.
5. The infinity quartet uses maximal canonical degree, not minimum support.
   Without `deg C=H-3`, all four top cell coefficients need not have the
   displayed affine dependence on `w_i`.
6. The affine and Stepanov gates are globalized only after both maximal
   degree and the selected-antipodal collision specialization.
7. The split-divisor condition for `J` and endpoint `Xi` require zero support
   defect and are deliberately not globalized.
