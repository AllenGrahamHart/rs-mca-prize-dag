# Claim contract

- **claim:** the canonical-degree defect gives the exact support ledger
  `(DGR1)--(DGR4)`, and every maximal-degree packet satisfies the global
  Euler and infinity gates; selected-antipodal collision packets also satisfy
  the affine intersection and official Stepanov cap.
- **scope:** complete one-antipodal `c=2` canonical packets at the maximal
  rate-half row, at arbitrary barycentric support.
- **dependencies:** the pointwise support compiler, global collision router,
  secondary differential identity, official field bound, and in-house
  Stepanov theorem.
- **consumer:** `rate_half_list_adjacent_crossing`.
- **falsifier:** a covered packet violating the defect ledger, or a
  maximal-degree packet failing one of `(DGR5)--(DGR10)`.
- **nonclaims:** no degree branch is excluded; the support split-divisor and
  endpoint `Xi` gate remain minimum-support statements.
