# Claim contract - L1 partial-pullback Johnson router

## Inputs

- one monic degree-`s` map and all its complete domain fibers;
- the exact component caps and kernel exponent in `(PJ2)`;
- a partial-agreement cap `z(f)<=Z`;
- the strict Johnson gate `(PJ5)`.

## Output

The exact per-map bound `(PJ6)`. Under a tame whole-petal anchor, fixed
`kappa<=K`, and polynomial generated field, the map aggregate is `(PJ7)`.

## Falsifier

A wrong identity `(1)`, a quotient tuple list violating `(3)`, an evaluation
fiber larger than `q^kappa`, or an aggregate exceeding `(PJ7)` under all its
printed guards.

## Nonclaims

No boundedness of `z` or `kappa`, no sub-Johnson estimate, and no wild or
unanchored map count is claimed.
