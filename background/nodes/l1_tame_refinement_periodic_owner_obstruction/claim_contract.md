# Claim contract - L1 tame-refinement periodic-owner obstruction

## Claim

There is a tame degree-two fixed-petal refinement on `F_17^*` and an exact
listed codeword at agreement `k+sigma` whose complete agreement support has
trivial multiplicative stabilizer.

## Verification payload

- `n=16`, `k=8`, `sigma=5`, `ell=6`;
- `P=X^2+3X` and the three fibers in `(PO2)`;
- exact sets `C,T,R,A` and received word `(PO3)`;
- exhaustive multiplicative stabilizer check.

## Falsifier

A failed fiber identity, a non-exact agreement set, a nontrivial stabilizer
of `A`, or a violation of the printed degree and cardinality ledger.

## Nonclaims

The witness is not an official row, does not refute L1, and does not exclude a
broader general-pullback owner that is not based on multiplicative support
periodicity.
