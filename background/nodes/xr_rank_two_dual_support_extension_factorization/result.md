# Result

Every rank-two row now has an exact support-extension certificate. Its
active-union and selected-block dual-GRS numerators share one unique cofactor
`R_i`; the only support sets are the active zero fiber `Z_i` and an inactive
block extension `T_i` of forced size `h-d-1+|Z_i|`.

This bridges the complete coefficient atlases to a finite dual-codeword
support representation. Actual received-pair realization, first-core
ownership, extension-family counting, and cross-core payment remain open.
