# Claim contract

- **Claim:** the one-dimensional trigonal defect classified on the generic
  pair-locator branch is impossible on the official order-`2^41`
  multiplicative subgroup; hence the official generic quadratic rank is
  exactly `3e+1`.
- **Scope:** the official prime-field `A=1`, core-one, distance-three packet
  with `e=2^38-1` and all cubic and pair roots in the smooth domain.
- **Dependencies:** exact generic Schur-defect classification and official
  prime-field collapse.
- **Published input:** the explicit subgroup-curve estimate in `source.md`.
- **Consumer:** `rate_half_band_closure` and the CR-003 generic classifier.
- **Falsifier:** a rank-three official pair packet with nonzero recovery
  kernel, or a failure of one of the exact subgroup-bound inequalities.
- **Nonclaim:** the saturated rank-three branch is not excluded, and no
  external split design is constructed or classified.
