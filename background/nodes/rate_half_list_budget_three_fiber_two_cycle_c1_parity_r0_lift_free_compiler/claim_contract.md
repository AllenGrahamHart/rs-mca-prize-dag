# Claim contract

- **claim:** the residual `R0` source lift can be eliminated exactly from the
  outer-trace and scalar gates, and every survivor obeys the printed
  lift-free constant equation.
- **scope:** the official generic two-antipodal nonharmonic `c=1` parity
  chamber, source role `R0`.
- **dependencies:** the nonharmonic Euclidean/scalar compiler and the exact
  Frobenius routing that leaves only `R0` anti-invariant.
- **falsifier:** an `R0` lift trace outside `K_t`, a spurious or missing
  scalar branch in the resultant identity, a trace/scalar selector mismatch,
  or failure of the constant specialization.
- **nonclaims:** the fourth-power and gcd gates remain, no branch is excluded,
  and no official-degree Legendre implementation is supplied.
