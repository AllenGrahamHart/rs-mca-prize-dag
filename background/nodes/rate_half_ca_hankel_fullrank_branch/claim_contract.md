# Claim contract

- **Claim:** at most `r+1` split-supported slopes for a tall syndrome Hankel
  pencil of full generic column rank.
- **Quantifiers:** every RS syndrome pencil with `r<R/2` satisfying `(HF1)`.
- **Dependency:** `rate_half_ca_hankel_split_pencil_equivalence` for the CA
  interpretation; the determinant root count itself is elementary.
- **Consumer:** the first post-quadratic CA-only rate-half range.
- **Nonclaims:** no bound for generically rank-deficient pencils and no claim
  at the forced-deficiency endpoint `r=R/2`.
- **Falsifier:** a full-generic-rank pencil with more than `r+1` finite slopes
  carrying a nonzero kernel.
