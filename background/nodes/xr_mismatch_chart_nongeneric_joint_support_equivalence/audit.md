# Audit

The equivalence uses a support of the exact form `Z union R`; merely knowing
that another joint support contains `Z` is insufficient if too many of its
other points lie outside `T`. This scope is explicit in the statement.

`verify.py` exhausts full external sets of sizes zero through two, all chart
codeword pairs, and all common error supports on a four-point punctured
domain over `F_7`. It checks multiplication, division, both agreement maps,
and the degree bounds.
