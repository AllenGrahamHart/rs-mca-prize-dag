# Claim contract

- **Claim:** fixed-chart genericity failure is equivalent to a second joint
  `A`-support extending the chart's full external zero set.
- **Forward map:** multiply the chart codeword pair by `P_Z` and add the
  recovered codeword pair.
- **Reverse map:** divide the difference of two codeword pairs by `P_Z`.
- **Consequence:** distinct joint explanation supports intersect in at most
  `K-1` coordinates.
- **Consumer:** the XR tangent/support-mismatch bridge.
- **Nonclaim:** no bound on slopes mapping to one joint explanation and no
  aggregate low-core theorem.
- **Falsifier:** a nongeneric chart without the stated joint support, or a
  stated joint support whose divided endpoints remain generic.
