# Claim contract

- **Claim:** the abstract degree/divisibility/four-space/quadric data admit a
  uniform family of `6e+1` split degree-`e` divisors.
- **Scope:** route selection for the dihedral pair-complement trace.
- **Dependency:** pair-complement quadratic trace.
- **Consumer:** `rate_half_band_closure` roadmap.
- **Falsifier:** a swap polynomial not dividing `P`, dependent four-space,
  a swap class off the cone, or fewer than `6e+1` distinct classes.
- **Nonclaim:** the construction does not satisfy the calibrated
  pair-Lagrange interpolation formula or define a prize support packet.
