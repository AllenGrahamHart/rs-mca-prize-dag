# Claim contract

- **universe:** one planted-sunflower receiver and its exact list at agreement
  `k+sigma`.
- **paid class:** distinct printed planted polynomials only.
- **residual:** every non-planted list polynomial, with no structural
  exclusions.
- **output:** safety is equivalent to
  `N_nonplant(U)<=floor(q/2^128)-p(U)`.
- **unsafe branch:** `p(U)>B*` is immediately unsafe.
- **nonclaim:** no non-planted upper bound is proved. W3 remains an open but
  retired safe-side target; the exact identity diagnoses the strict failure
  of extending its inequality to the unsafe spending cell.
