- **claim:** every actual `c=2` pair selected by the joint invariant/torsion
  gcd admits one exact normalized `(t,S,P)` representation, modulo the stated
  orientation involution, with forty scalar squarings compiling all quotient
  torsion conditions.
- **scope:** the generic canonical `c=2` denominator-mismatch chamber.
- **dependencies:** canonical subgroup-scaling covariance and the exact joint
  pair-torsion selector.
- **falsifier:** a selected packet lost or spuriously added by normalization,
  an incorrect orientation transform, or terminal equations not equivalent
  to `t,c,d in mu_(2^40)`.
- **nonclaims:** the normalized chamber is not excluded and the retained gap,
  canonical-span, splitting, square-class, and cycle conditions remain.
