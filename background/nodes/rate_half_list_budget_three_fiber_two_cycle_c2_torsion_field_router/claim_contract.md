# Claim contract

- **claim:** the official terminal torsion recurrence already forces splitting
  and square class, and every packet lies in either a prime-field or reciprocal
  Frobenius chamber.
- **scope:** the maximal official normalized `c=2` denominator-mismatch row
  with `N=2^40` and `2N | q-1`.
- **dependencies:** the exact normalized torsion compiler and maximal-row
  field-degree collapse.
- **falsifier:** a terminal packet whose complementary quadratic does not
  split in `F_q`, whose roots are not squares, or whose Frobenius action
  violates `(TFR3)--(TFR5)`.
- **nonclaims:** no packet is Frobenius-fixed; parity, the two coefficient gaps,
  canonical span, and `c=2` exclusion are not proved.
