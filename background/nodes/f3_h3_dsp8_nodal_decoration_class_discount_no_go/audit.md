# Audit

1. The subgroup is generated explicitly; no primitive-root library is used.
2. Exact order 256 is checked through the power-of-two order test.
3. Signed disjointness includes both positive and negative coordinates.
4. `P` and `R` are independently enumerated from the complete shifted set.
5. All nine targets pass richness and quotient support before class counting.
6. Antipodality uses `H^2`, not mere membership in `H`.
7. The failed caps include `4/9`, `5/9`, and `2/3`; seven of nine is larger
   than each.
8. The nonofficial inequality `p<n^2` is load-bearing and explicit.
