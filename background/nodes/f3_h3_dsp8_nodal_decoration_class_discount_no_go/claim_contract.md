# Claim contract

## Claim

The DSP8 singular-trace decoration packet, even with signed disjointness,
`P>=25`, `R>0`, and nonidentity targets, does not imply that at most six of
nine decorations are antipodal.

## Scope

- Exact field: `F_769`.
- Exact subgroup order: `n=256`.
- Exact conclusion: seven retained antipodal decorations from one pair.

## Dependencies

- The unit-product trace normal form identifies the nine decorations.
- The nodal target formula identifies their targets and singular scope.

## Falsifier

Failure of subgroup order, trace, product, signed-disjointness, any printed
`P` or `R` value, or the seven-entry square-membership matrix falsifies this
node.

## Nonclaims

The fixture is outside `p>=n^2`. It is not an official-row counterexample and
does not rule out a field-size-dependent class estimate.
