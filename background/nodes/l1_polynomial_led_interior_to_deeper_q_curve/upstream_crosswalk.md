# Upstream crosswalk - polynomial-led interior to deeper Q

## L4 instance

At `e=1`, `(DQ3)` is upstream's exact L4 curve decomposition

```text
sum_(s in B) |Fib_(w+1)(theta(s))|,
```

with the additional L1 gcd guard retaining only complete level-`m` members.

## General interior dictionary

For every `1<=e<=k`, a polynomial-led profile
`d_1=w+e+1` is a `B^e` curve of depth-`w+e` Q fibers.  The realized
base-field floor is the subfamily where `R_s` contributes extra domain roots;
the exact guard routes those codewords to their higher complete shell.

## Remaining input

to avoid the raw `|B|^e` additive cost.  Non-planted/general-line cells with
nonconstant minimal vector and the above-cap range remain separate BC inputs.
