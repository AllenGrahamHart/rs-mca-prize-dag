# Result

`PROVED`. On every sufficiently rich DSP8 product fiber, the cubic nodal
degree cap and the disjoint-six density lower bound imply targetwise
`D_nod<=D_sm`: the thresholds are `P>=33` off the antipodal class and `P>=35`
on it. Hence all nodal high-tail quotient weight can be charged one-for-one
to smooth edges, leaving only two bounded product-multiplicity bands. DSP8
still needs a smooth-moment estimate and a payment for those bands.
