# Claim contract

- **claim:** nodal signed-disjoint distance-six edges are no more numerous
  than smooth ones on antipodal-free fibers with `P>=33` and antipodal fibers
  with `P>=35`, with the sharper formulas `(NSD2)` and `(NSD3)`.
- **scope:** every retained DSP8 product target in an official dyadic H3 row.
- **dependencies:** the fixed-fiber maximum nodal degree three and the exact
  disjoint-six multiplicity lower bounds.
- **consumer effect:** replace the full high-tail disjoint moment by twice its
  smooth part, leaving only the printed bounded product-multiplicity bands.
- **nonclaims:** no smooth-trace estimate, no quotient-weight estimate, no
  official-order survivor exclusion, and no closure of DSP8.
- **falsifier:** one retained target above the relevant threshold with more
  nodal than smooth signed-disjoint distance-six edges, or a violation of an
  exact lower bound in `(NSD2)` or `(NSD3)`.
