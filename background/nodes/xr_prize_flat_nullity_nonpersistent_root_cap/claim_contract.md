# Claim contract

- **claim:** at each first-open prize affine rank, every over-budget P-A cell
  has nonpersistent global-root count `v` below a printed ceiling
- **scope:** prize P-A flat-nullity cells at affine ranks `16,16,14`
- **output:** `v<=1526176110,2902067939,1962285106`, together with the prior
  effective-core lower bounds
- **currency:** convex endpoint reduction of the exact `(FN3)` real envelope
- **nonclaim:** no count of the remaining cells, no P-B result, and no
  cross-cell aggregate
- **falsifier:** an in-scope over-budget cell at or above a printed `V`, or a
  failure of convexity/endpoint arithmetic under the stated prize parameters
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
