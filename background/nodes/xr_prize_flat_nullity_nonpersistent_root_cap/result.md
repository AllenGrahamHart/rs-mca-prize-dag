# Result

At the first open prize affine ranks, every P-A counterexample cell lies in
the explicit wedge

```text
a+u+v>=11,243,370; 9,629,972; 2,241,633,
v<=1,526,176,110; 2,902,067,939; 1,962,285,106.
```

The two-dimensional `(u,v)` basis-charge problem is controlled by its two
`u` endpoint chambers. Future analysis and any donated registry should start
from this wedge rather than sweep the full flat-nullity triangle.
