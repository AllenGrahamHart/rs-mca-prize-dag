# Claim contract

- **claim:** proper four/five-block rank-two circuits and full-support cores
  occupy disjoint adjacent ranges of the exact defect charge
- **scope:** an actual row-scaling circuit that is a nonempty proper subset of
  one official uniform minimal Maxwell core
- **output:** the lower defect charge `(PC2)`, host extra-block floor `(PC5)`,
  and baseline-positive shell cutoffs `(PC6)`
- **currency:** pointed support/packing records, separated by proper versus
  full-core ownership
- **nonclaim:** no embedding or slope count is proved
- **falsifier:** a scoped proper circuit with `Delta_J<delta_t`, defect charge
  below `(PC2)`, host complement below `(PC5)`, or a baseline-positive shell
  beyond `(PC6)`
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
