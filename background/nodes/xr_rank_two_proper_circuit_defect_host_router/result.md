# Result

Proper rank-two circuit records now have an exact sign/charge discriminator
from full-support cores. At nonpositive baseline, the full-core defect upper
range and proper-circuit lower range are adjacent and disjoint. Deep proper
four/five-block circuits must carry active-zero reuse, unused pair slack, or
an outside multiplicity-at-least-three event, and every circuit imposes an
explicit minimum number of additional host blocks. Counting the resulting
pointed embeddings remains open.
