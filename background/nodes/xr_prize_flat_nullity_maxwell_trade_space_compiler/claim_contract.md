# Claim contract

- **claim:** every over-budget prize P-A flat-nullity cell has a normalized
  minimal core with at least `(u+v)(t-2)+e+1` trades; each rank-one trade owns
  a canonical shared circuit satisfying the local-tangent conditions `(GC5)`
- **scope:** all prize P-A flat-nullity cells after exceptional-slope deletion
  and deterministic full-rank block truncation
- **output:** `(GC1)--(GC5)` and the specialization link to the stronger
  `u=0` common-support polynomial compiler
- **currency:** flat-nullity factorization, minimal dense core, stack-rank
  nullity, matroid circuits, and a two-slope quotient argument
- **nonclaim:** no circuit-owner count, no trade-rank-at-least-two payment, no
  P-B theorem, and no aggregate P-A close
- **falsifier:** an in-scope core violating `(GC3)`, or a rank-one trade whose
  canonical support circuit fails either local `W` explanation in `(GC5)`
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
