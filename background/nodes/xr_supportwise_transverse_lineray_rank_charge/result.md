# Result

The support-wise mismatch family can be charged globally as one LineRay
selector; it need not first be summed over canonical external-zero charts.
The exact paid ranks are `3,3,3,3,3,3` against an `8n^3` half-reserve and
`4,3,3,3,3,3` against the full `16n^3` mismatch reserve in the order

```text
RowC 1/4, RowC 1/8, RowC 1/16,
prize 1/4, prize 1/8, prize 1/16.
```

The remaining P-A2 support-mismatch contribution is a genuinely higher-rank
slope family, not a low-rank chart-aggregation problem.
