# Result

The support-only obstruction is now finite-geometric rather than an
unstructured matching problem. If no omitted pair has rank five, either the
actual smooth weight is opposite on every matched pair, or all but at most
148 exceptional pairs lie in fibers of one rational map of degree two,
three, or four.

The next exact target is to exclude or pay those four branches. Degree two
interfaces with the existing Mobius/dihedral machinery; degrees three and
four fit low-bidegree subgroup coincidence curves; the antiweight branch
must retain the full smooth formula for `H`.
