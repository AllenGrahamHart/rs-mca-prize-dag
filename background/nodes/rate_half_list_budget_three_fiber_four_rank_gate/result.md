# Result

The direct fiber-four residual is eliminated in the linear `K_4-e` and
triangle-plus-singleton chambers. In the linear `K_4` chamber it is reduced
to the exact rank-two locus of four reciprocal quadratics over `F(X^4)`.

Antipodal deleted pairs show that this algebraic locus is nonempty, but they
do not yet provide an official block construction. The next theorem question
is classification and payment of that rank-two exceptional class, not a
large search over degree-`2^39` locators.
