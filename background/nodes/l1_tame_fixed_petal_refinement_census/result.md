# Result

For every tame divisor `s|ell`, one fixed source petal determines at most one
degree-`s` refinement partition up to additive shift. All tame refinement-map
classes in the fixed source therefore number at most
`M_src tau(ell)<=n`. The remaining smaller-fiber obstruction is not quotient-
polynomial multiplicity: it is small-scale role/support payment, together
with genuinely wild and unanchored cells. An exact `F_9` additive fixture
shows why wild uniqueness needs extra multiplicative-domain geometry.
