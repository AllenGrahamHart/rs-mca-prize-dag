# Claim contract

- **Claim:** the descended external factors obey the exact product identity
  `(QEP3)`. Paired active-row sets are disjoint except at the zero-or-one
  printed identical-row orbit; accordingly all factors are squarefree, or
  exactly `e` factors have one common double root.
- **Scope:** either surviving rank-two dihedral branch with an exact external
  split design.
- **Dependencies:** dihedral subgroup-norm descent and external split-design
  saturation.
- **Consumer:** `rate_half_band_closure` and a quotient multiplicity or
  polynomial-abc attack.
- **Falsifier:** failure of `(QEP3)`, a root of multiplicity above two, two
  identical-row orbits, a nonexceptional orbit with positive codegree, or a
  violation of the exact totals `(QEP7)`.
- **Nonclaim:** the product and simple-root ledgers do not by themselves
  exclude `3e` external split factors.
