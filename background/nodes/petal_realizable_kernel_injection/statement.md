# petal_realizable_kernel_injection

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

In the L1 coset-chart full-petal residue bridge, every exact realizable
full-petal extra determines a squarefree locator point lying in the
residue-line kernel

```text
K_{I,d} = ker(pi_{>d} R_{I,d}),
```

and this assignment is injective.

## Falsifier

Two distinct realizable full-petal extras with the same locator point, or a
realizable extra whose locator point does not lie in the residue-line kernel.
