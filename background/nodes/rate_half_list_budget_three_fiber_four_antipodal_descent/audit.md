# Audit

The descent uses the full official factorization, not merely residue rank.
The equality `product H_i=Y^d-1` relies on the eight deleted roots exhausting
the exact degree-eight exceptional factor in the linear `K_4` chamber.

The signs in `(F4A4)` are load-bearing: deleting the antipodal pair
`X^2-a_i` leaves its complementary pair `X^2+a_i` in the block locator.

The two-dimensional conclusion is over the base field, not over `F(Y)`.
It follows from the two constant coefficient relations in `(F4A6)` and does
not classify all rank-two reciprocal-quadratic components.

The first nonconstant boundary is genuinely populated. For `d=8`, `s=2`
over `F_97`, exhaust all choices of four deleted quotient roots, both square
roots `a_i` of each, and all assignments of the four complementary quotient
roots to the linear `G_i`. Among `26,880` assignments, exactly `192` admit a
relation with all four coefficients nonzero. Their determinant condition is
the graph condition for a fractional-linear map between the four `a_i` and
the four roots of the `G_i`. This exact boundary census shows that scale-
sensitive subgroup rigidity is necessary; it is not evidence that the
official component exists.
