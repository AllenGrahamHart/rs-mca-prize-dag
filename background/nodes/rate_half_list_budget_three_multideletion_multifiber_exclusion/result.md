# Result

Every direct equal-multifiber completion with quotient-fiber size at least
eight is now excluded across all nine linear budget-three chambers. The proof
uses the rational generating functions of the top locator coefficients and
handles the mixed one-root/two-root deletion profiles exactly.

At the prize maximum, the direct one-map residual is reduced to fiber sizes
`1,2` in cycle/path and `1,2,4` in linear `K_4-e`, `K_4`, and
triangle-plus-singleton. This does not address mixed maps, partial fibers,
primitive blocks, or the four quadratic scrolls.
