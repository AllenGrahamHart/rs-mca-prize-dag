# Audit - L1 maximal background-anchor injection

## Checked axes

1. Exact background support, petal supports, touched set, and defect are fixed.
2. Petal labels are distinct and nonzero; both properties are used.
3. The threshold is `r+h>=ell+d`, including the retained-core contribution.
4. Monicity handles equality in the petal-injection degree comparison.
5. Maximality gives `r<ell`, hence the strict background-injection comparison.
6. The combined exponent is the minimum of two valid injection bounds, not a
   product of their target sizes.
7. The support-pattern factor in `(BA5)` is an upper bound and may include
   invalid patterns with an empty selected petal.
8. No polynomial conclusion is claimed when `E`, `u/t`, `r`, or the chart
   family ranges without a fixed bound.

## Remaining attack

Sum the joint anchor charge over the theorem-certified residual and over
non-intrinsic first-owner charts. A successful payment must beat the support
entropy in the growing-polarity and balanced sub-Johnson regimes.
