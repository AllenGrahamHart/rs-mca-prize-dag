# Claim contract - L1 maximal background-anchor injection

## Inputs

- the exact sunflower core-defect normal form;
- a maximal source chart, hence `|R|<ell`;
- pairwise disjoint petals with pairwise distinct nonzero labels;
- exact background and petal agreement supports;
- the list threshold `r+h>=ell+d`.

## Output

The exact fixed-pattern cap `(BA4)` and the aggregate `(t,u,r,E)` upper ledger
`(BA5)--(BA6)`.

## Consumer rule

For a fixed support pattern, charge the smaller of the largest-petal cofactor
space and the background quotient space. A finite census must apply `(BA4)`
before enumerating coefficient pairs, and may analytically remove any complete
stratum paid by `(BA5)`.

## Nonclaims

The theorem does not sum an unbounded stratum range, supply cross-chart
ownership, prove a polynomial number of charts, or prove L1 mixed-petal
amplification.

## Falsifier

Two distinct listed codewords in one exact layer with the same selected
largest-petal cofactor or the same exact background quotient; or a complete
`(t,u,r,E)` stratum exceeding `(BA5)`.
