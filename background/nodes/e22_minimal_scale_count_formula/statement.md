# e22_minimal_scale_count_formula

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

The dyadic quotient staircase pricing column, with any declared duplicate
subtraction or multiplicity factor, counts exactly the support classes whose
canonical tail data satisfy `e22_minimal_scale_tail_criterion`.

This is proved from the minimal-scale partition and the exact pricing-column
evaluation on that partition.

## Falsifier

A tail-minimal support class that is omitted, duplicated, or assigned the
wrong multiplicity by the refined pricing formula.
