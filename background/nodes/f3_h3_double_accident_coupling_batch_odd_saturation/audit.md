# Audit

The localization is load-bearing. A normalized shifted factor `c_a` is a
global cyclotomic unit only when `a` is odd; for even `a` its norm is a
nontrivial power of two. It is nevertheless a unit at every official odd
row-prime ideal, which is exactly the scope consumed by the prize row.

The sign in `(CBS4)` has no effect on generated ideals but is pinned for
replay. More importantly, the identity uses normalized factors `c_a`, not the
unnormalized shifted factors. With unnormalized factors an extra power of
`pi` obscures the odd-local equivalence.

The theorem removes a generator type, not the quotient information. The
complete coupling batch contains one generator per quotient lift and is
odd-locally equivalent to the anchored quotient-collision ledger.
