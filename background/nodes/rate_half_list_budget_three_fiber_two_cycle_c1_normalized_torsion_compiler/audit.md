# Audit

1. Normalize by the repeated square `A`, not by an arbitrary root.
2. The cycle order is `N=2^40`; the generic canonical covariance uses
   `h=2^37+1`.
3. Outer invariants scale by `lambda^(4h),lambda^(6h)`; source invariants
   scale independently by `A^(-2),A^(-3)`.
4. The norm therefore scales by `A^(-(24h+12))), a nonzero factor.
5. Pair torsion needs both `T_40=2` and `P_40=1`. The trace equation
   alone permits a reciprocal pair whose common product has the wrong
   order.
6. Splitting, square classes, distinctness, canonical span, and outer
   splitting remain explicit; the recurrence does not replace them.
