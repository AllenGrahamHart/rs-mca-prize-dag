# Claim contract

## Proved

- The mass-aware necessary condition `(RC1)` for the ordinary moment route.
- The four full-mass real-average moment floors `(RC2)`.
- The deployed Johnson/anticode Q ceiling is over 1.66 million bits too weak.
- One-pencil moving-root payment does not directly pay the four
  high-dimensional boundary-Q slices `(RC3)`.

## Not proved

- A moment route with explicitly small residual mass is impossible.
- Falling-factorial, off-diagonal, sparse-heavy, or non-moment methods fail.
- Q, BC, SP, or any deployed safe row is false or proved.
- The specialized `kb_rowsharp_route_d_*_no_go` packages map to a current
  local critical consumer.

## Falsifier

A numerical mismatch in the source-bound tables; an ordinary-moment proof
violating `(RC1)`; or a direct use of the one-pencil theorem that also proves
exhaustive boundary-Q coverage and pays the number of pencils.
