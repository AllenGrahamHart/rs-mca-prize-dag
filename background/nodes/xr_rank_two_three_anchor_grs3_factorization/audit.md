# Audit

1. The coefficient rank is `q=3`; no factorization is claimed for the
   four-anchor branch.
2. The Mobius determinant is nonzero, so `(P,Q)` really is a basis change.
3. Three moments, not two, are required: the slope-weighted trade equation
   contributes the quadratic moment.
4. The degree bound is strict, `deg H<t-3`. This gives dimension `t-3` and
   makes `(TG3)` unique.
5. Full support of the trade weights is recorded as `H(gamma_i)!=0` at every
   selected slope.
6. Formula `(TG4)` includes the pre-existing row scale `s_i`; omitting it
   would only be correct after an additional column normalization.
7. The minimum-face quotient is recovered at `t=4`, but larger-shell support
   embeddings are not declared paid.
8. No Modal or candidate-row computation is used.
