# Claim contract - L1 exact-shell complement Toeplitz normal form

## Inputs

- a multiplicative-coset domain with locator `Z^n-beta`;
- characteristic not dividing `n`;
- the exact-shell barycentric criterion.

## Outputs

- coefficient identity `(CT1)`;
- a fixed Toeplitz gap map invariant under degree-below-`k` shifts of `U`;
- an exact primitive split-divisor representation of every agreement shell.

## Consumer rule

The global L1 target may now be posed as primitive Toeplitz split-divisor
flatness. Do not replace "Toeplitz" by "locator prefix" without a theorem
that preserves split divisors, first-match ownership, and the exactness gcd.

## Nonclaims

The map need not have rank `w`. Its zero section need not be flat. The gcd
guard need not remove every rank collapse. No decomposition into upstream Q
atoms is proved.

## Falsifier

A support violating `(CT1)`, a monic divisor passing `(CT2)` whose remainder
has degree at least `k`, or a disagreement between the gcd guard and exact
agreement on a complement root.
