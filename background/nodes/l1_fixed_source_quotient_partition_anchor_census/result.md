# Result

Inside one fixed maximal source, at most `M_src` non-intrinsic degree-`ell`
quotient partitions can carry a whole source petal. Including every
complete-fiber core/petal/background role assignment gives at most
`M_src 3^(n/ell)` structural keys, polynomial at the L1 cutoff. Thus bounded
petal/core-polarity genuine quotient cells no longer have an unpaid quotient-
polynomial multiplier. This census does not count Forney coefficient keys;
the downstream anchored triple-polarity closure pays them at fixed triple
polarity. Smaller-fiber refinements, growing polarity, arbitrary-locator
cells, and general whole-petal anchor supply remain open.
