# Audit - L1 fixed-source quotient-partition anchor census

## Checked axes

1. The quotient polynomial is monic and has degree exactly `ell`.
2. The anchor is one whole `ell`-point source petal, not a partial support.
3. Additive shifts are one partition rather than separate charts.
4. Least-petal ownership is injective.
5. Complete-fiber role assignments are safely overcounted by `3^L`.
6. The cutoff turns both `M_src` and `L` into `O(log n)` quantities.
7. First matching can only decrease the aggregate.
8. This census does not count Forney keys; the downstream triple-polarity
   closure pays them in every fixed-polarity anchored box.
9. Dense support on a source petal does not supply the whole-fiber anchor.
10. Additive shifts retire only structural partitions; label-sensitive data
    is passed to the whole-strip CRT payment.

## Remaining attack

Handle growing triple polarity in the downstream anchored closure. Separately
handle internal quotient refinements that split a source petal into smaller
fibers, or prove an anchor-or-refinement dichotomy.
