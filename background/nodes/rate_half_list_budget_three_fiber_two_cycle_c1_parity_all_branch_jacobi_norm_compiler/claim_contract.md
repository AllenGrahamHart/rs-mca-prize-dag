# Claim contract

- **claim:** all six nonharmonic `c=1` parity source roles reduce exactly to
  seven degree-`2^36` Jacobi gcds per torsion sign, behind the existing
  CR-002 norm pair.
- **scope:** the official generic two-antipodal `c=1` parity chamber.
- **dependencies:** the exact nonharmonic scalar compiler, source-field
  routing, and the proved `R0` Jacobi/norm endpoint.
- **falsifier:** a source lift missed or spuriously added by either resultant,
  a wrong role collapse, an incorrect signed constant equation, or a packet
  requiring a different torsion norm.
- **nonclaims:** shared norms and fourteen scalar gcds remain unevaluated;
  nonparity and later Euclidean gates remain open.
