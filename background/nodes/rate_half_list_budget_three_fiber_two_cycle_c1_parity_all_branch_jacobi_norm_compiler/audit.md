# Audit

1. The signed equation is `h=sP` with `s^2=-epsilon`; both roots of `s` are
   required.
2. Denominator clearing is exact only under `r^4!=1`, which is retained.
3. The first resultant uses `r^4-2xr^2+1`; changing the sign of `2x` changes
   the trace orientation.
4. `R1/R2` collapse together and `P1/P2` collapse together, but `P0` remains
   separate.
5. Under `x -> -x`, the odd Legendre remainder also changes sign. Substituting
   `v=xq_s` before taking `A^2-zB^2` enforces this.
6. There are seven tests per `epsilon`, not seven total and not twelve per
   sign.
7. Shared torsion norms are only prefilters. Every compatible divisor must
   replay the relevant `F` and later Euclidean gates.
