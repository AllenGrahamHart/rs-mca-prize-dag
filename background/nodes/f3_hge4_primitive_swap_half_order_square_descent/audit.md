# Audit

1. `Y` has `h` elements because `P` is antipodal-free.
2. `N=n/2`; all elements of `mu_N` have square roots in `mu_n`.
3. The odd-degree sign in `c_Y=-L_Y(0)` is load-bearing.
4. The perfect-square test is on the exact polynomial `(L_Y+c_Y)/Z`, not on
   its values at selected points.
5. The square root must be monic and is unique in odd characteristic.
6. Trivial stabilizer is checked in `mu_N`, not in the ambient field.
7. Anchoring uses `1 in Y`, equivalently `{1,-1}` contained in `U`.
8. The factor `2^(h-1)` is removed work, not a claimed bound on survivors.
9. The divisor form is an algebraic classification, not permission to scan
   all `p^d` monic coefficient tuples.
