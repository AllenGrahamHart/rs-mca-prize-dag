# Audit

1. The sign in `P=TB^3-N` comes from `EQ=1-z^N`; the older antipodal
   quotient theorem uses the opposite binomial convention.
2. The derivative-free polynomial is `T_0`, with `T=8T_0`; omitting the
   `E_4b_0` boundary term changes the remainder.
3. The automatic valuation is `2H`, not merely `H`, because both primary-gap
   coefficients immediately after `B` vanish.
4. The useful gate is `C|P_0`; the large factor `z^H` in `V` must not be
   presented as new information.
5. Full support-polynomial degree is essential for `deg C=H-3` and the cube
   resultant.  Those conclusions do not hold for an arbitrary larger-support
   packet.
6. `C(0)=1` does not imply that `C` is monic.  The cube gate uses
   `C_sharp=C/lc(C)`; using the raw resultant would introduce a power of the
   leading coefficient.
7. No official-degree polynomial or resultant is materialized.
