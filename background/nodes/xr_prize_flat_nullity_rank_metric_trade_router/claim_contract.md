# Claim contract

- **claim:** the flat-nullity trade space obeys the rank-metric forcing rule
  `(RM2)`, hence the explicit rank bound `(RM3)` and low-rank exclusion
  inequalities `(RM4)--(RM5)`
- **scope:** every minimal prize P-A core produced by the general
  flat-nullity Maxwell compiler
- **output:** a guaranteed low-rank trade and official maximal-flat-nullity
  arity ranges for rank one/rank at most two
- **currency:** compression by the two slope parity checks and the elementary
  row-deletion Singleton bound
- **nonclaim:** no rank-two classification at `u>0`, no circuit-owner count,
  and no aggregate P-A payment
- **falsifier:** an in-scope core satisfying `(RM2)` whose nonzero trades all
  have rank greater than `r`
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
