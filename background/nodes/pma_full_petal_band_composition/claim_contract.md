# Claim contract - PMA full-petal band/root composition

## Inputs

- planted-receiver exclusion of non-planted one-petal words;
- the layout-anchored green full-petal top-band payment;
- the petal-pattern root-pinning aggregate at the L1 lower cutoff.

## Output

The correct relation between total chart petals and touched petals, an exact
union of the two paid full-petal regions, complete closure for `M<=3`, and the
deficit-coordinate description of the remaining full-petal family.

## Promotion evidence

1. direct agreement-count proof of the top-band relation;
2. exact composition inequality proving `e=0` below the band when
   `t>=2M-4`;
3. explicit small-`M` corollaries;
4. mutation-controlled exhaustive arithmetic verifier;
5. evidence-only consumer edge, with no promotion of PMA or `imgfib`.

## Nonclaims

This theorem does not prove the unbounded off-band count, pay partial petals,
or supply a complete profile envelope. It explicitly refutes, rather than
assumes, the proposed equality `m_chi=t`.
