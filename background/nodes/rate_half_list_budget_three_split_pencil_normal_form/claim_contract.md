# Claim contract

- **claim:** every B*=3 Johnson-predecessor witness induces the exact
  split-pencil system `(SP1)--(SP4)` with the printed six-row degree ledger;
- **scope:** `n=4d`, `k=2d`, four distinct codewords, exactly `3d-1` selected
  agreements per codeword, and arbitrary distinct evaluation points;
- **requirement:** the proved six-type intersection reduction;
- **consumer:** `rate_half_list_adjacent_crossing`, specifically its B*=3
  safe-predecessor question;
- **new attack object:** four disjoint large locators `A_i` whose product,
  together with at most nine exceptional linear factors, is `Lambda_D`, and
  whose triangle syzygies have edge coefficients of degree at most two;
- **nonclaim:** no split-pencil exclusion, official full-rank theorem,
  predecessor safety, or unsafe witness is proved;
- **falsifier:** a parent-compatible witness for which a pair locator does not
  factor as stated, a triangle identity fails after cancellation, or any
  `b_ij` exceeds its tabled degree cap.
