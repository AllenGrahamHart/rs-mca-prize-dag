# Result

After one explicit common-denominator clearing, each external fiber is the
known `4e+2`-root nonincidence locator times a residual quartic. The degree
four is sharp already on the exact `e=1` Hankel fixture, where the cofactor
is not base-field split. All official quartics are normalized fibers of one
biform of bidegree at most `(e-2,4)`. The remaining generic theorem must
therefore control this quartic-valued curve or prove a genuine pencil
decomposition; it cannot count the conic residues as a locator pencil
directly.
