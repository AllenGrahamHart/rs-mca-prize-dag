# Audit

1. The common denominator is `A B q_e`; clearing only `q_e` is not justified
   by the first-jet formulas.
2. The cleared fiber is `(A(x)B(x))^2H_x`, not merely a scalar-free copy of
   `H_x`.
3. `4e+2` counts nonincident active rows, whereas `2e+1` counts incident
   rows. Reversing them gives the wrong cofactor degree.
4. The exact `e=1` replay prevents promotion of `deg T_gamma<=4` to a cubic,
   split-quartic, or fixed-factor claim.
5. The factor `I` in the global quartic weld is exact at every order. The
   additional factor `z` uses `e>=3` through `4e+7<6e+3`; the `e=1`
   sharpness fixture is not evidence for that step.
6. The second factor of `I` uses all `2e+3` exceptional/triple support
   points and the quartic `X`-degree. It is not a multiplicity claim at an
   external slope.
7. The drop from `e-1` to `e-2` uses the omitted paired index in `(24)` and
   all `2e` exceptional roots. It does not imply that any external quartic
   drops below degree four in `X`.
