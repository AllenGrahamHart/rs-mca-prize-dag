# Audit

- The `A=1` matrix has one right singular block and no left singular block;
  the coefficient-independence proof uses the right kernel.
- Root incidence is counted for `Qbar` on `D\S`. The `s` identically zero
  rows of `Q` are not mistaken for capacity defects.
- `Delta=d-(1+s)e` is the contracted regular size, not the larger original
  regular size `rho-e`.
- The `s=2,3e=d` endpoint has quotient `T_max=4e+2` and remainder zero; it is
  not folded into the generic `4e+1` row.
- Exact matrix rank is asserted only under failure, where `T>=rho+2>e`.
- Component chambers are necessary conditions. Width below two does not mean
  uniqueness; uniqueness is asserted only in the printed subranges.
- The first-degree saturation bounds use the worst allowed slack separately
  for each core.
- No numerical experiment is used in the proof.
