# Claim contract

- **claim:** both residual harmonic `c=1` parity classes descend to
  `F_p` in exactly the positive quadratic field branch, and reduce to two
  fixed trace recurrences over the printed finite modulus interval.
- **scope:** `H_R,H_P` at the official maximal generic row.
- **dependencies:** parity Mobius routing, even factorization, and maximal
  field-degree collapse.
- **falsifier:** a scoped harmonic survivor in another field class, an
  anti-invariant residual lift, or a mismatch between source torsion and the
  fixed trace terminal.
- **nonclaims:** the finite recurrences are not evaluated and no harmonic or
  nonharmonic class is excluded here.
