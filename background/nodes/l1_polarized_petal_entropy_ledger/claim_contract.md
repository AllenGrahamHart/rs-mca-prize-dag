# Claim contract - L1 polarized petal-entropy ledger

## Input

`pma_petal_pattern_root_pinning_ledger`, specifically its proved statement
that one exact petal support pattern and defect has at most
`binom(k-1,e)` compatible listed words.

## Output

A field-independent polynomial aggregate for fixed
`p+e`, where `p` charges each petal by its smaller sparse/full description.

## Consumer Rule

Replace the old necessary condition `u+e->infinity` by the stronger
`p+e->infinity`. Retain `u` where another theorem genuinely uses total
deficit; `p` is an improved counting coordinate, not an identity with `u`.

## Falsifier

An exact support profile omitted by the sparse/dense orientation encoding, a
fixed-pattern multiplicity larger than the root-pinning theorem permits, or a
failure of `(PE3)` under the printed fixed-`E` hypotheses.
