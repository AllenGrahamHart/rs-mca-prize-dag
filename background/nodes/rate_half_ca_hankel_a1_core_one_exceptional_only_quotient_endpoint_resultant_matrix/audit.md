# Audit

The row factors are `A` and `H=P_X/A=A_1(0;X)`. The column factors are the
original clean locator `Q` and its first complement `V`, not the corrected
second-complement forms `A_1,B_1`.

The exceptional `z` exponents are asymmetric. `Q(0;X)=A`, so all `2e`
exceptional roots occur in `Res(A,Q)`. On the complementary roots `H`, the
identity `Q(0;X)V(0;X)=0` and `Q(0;X)!=0` force all `6e+6` roots into
`Res(H,V)`. The other two entries have no `z` factor.

In the swapped profile, the checkerboard factors are a notation for cleared
polynomial identities. No localization at an ordinary slope is used in the
theorem.
