# Claim contract

- **claim:** on the two-antipodal-denominator `c=1` chamber, the
  completion-root match is exactly one of six outer-trace equations from two
  role patterns; two of four harmonic symmetry classes are excluded.
- **scope:** the official generic floor at `M=2^36`.
- **dependencies:** the normalized `c=1` compiler and parameter-uniform
  even factorization.
- **falsifier:** a valid parity packet outside the two role patterns, a PGL
  match with none of the six trace equations, or failure of `R0/P0`
  exclusion.
- **nonclaims:** the two residual harmonic classes and every nonharmonic
  class remain open; no matched field descent is imported.
