# Audit

1. The degree-four target normalization enforces both `deg S=4` and
   `S(0)!=0`; neither is assumed from the original numerator.
2. The two boundary rows are printed. Their complete failure list is a
   reducible fourth-power difference or one of three monomial denominators.
3. The middle monomial denominator is not discarded. Its determinant-one
   torus substitution is printed and independently replayed.
4. The end monomials are equivalent under subgroup inversion and give the
   explicit Laurent map. The additive constant cancels, leaving the
   three-parameter curve `(Q4R2)`.
5. The `2592` and `960` estimates fit after the 148-pair tail charge. The
   best currently audited Laurent-end constant `5376` does not; the proof
   retains that branch rather than rounding the margin.
6. Geometric reducibility is separated before any absolutely irreducible
   subgroup estimate is invoked.
