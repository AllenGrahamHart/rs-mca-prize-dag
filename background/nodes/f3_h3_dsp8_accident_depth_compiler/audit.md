# Audit

`rho_L=(R-L)_+` is truncated at zero. The identity
`sum rho_1=sum R-|supp R|` is taken only over positive nonidentity fibers;
the higher-depth bounds use `rho_L<=rho_1`.

The support lower bound `n-2` fixes one denominator and varies the numerator.
It does not assume characteristic-zero quotient Sidonicity at the finite row.

The base payment is `L(n-1)^2`, not `(n-1)^2`, when `L>1`. The low-depth
payment `E(n-2)^2` is separate. Omitting either term makes `(ADC3)` unsound.

The direct uniform numerator is `76599-6800L`. The `L=1` value is `69799`;
the diagonal `L=11,E=6` value is `1799`. Mixing a depth with the wrong
allowance is unsound.

The selected fixed-order packet retains four distinct quotient lifts, not
merely three nonzero couplings, because the fourth lift records `R>=4`. At
most one coupling is zero, so a nonzero anchor always exists. The anchored
quotient-star ideal equals the full coupling-batch ideal only after inverting
two; that is enough because every official row prime is odd. A product-only
star does not certify `R>=4`, while unanchored or unrelated quotient
collisions do not certify the same target or prime-ideal alignment.

No numerical survival result is used in the proof.
