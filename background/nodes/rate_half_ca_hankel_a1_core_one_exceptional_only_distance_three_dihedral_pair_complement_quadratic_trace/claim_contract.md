# Claim contract

- **Claim:** at least `3e-3` distinct degree-`e` split divisors of `P_Z` lie
  in the four-dimensional complement space `(PCT3)` and satisfy the quadric
  equation `(PCT5)`.
- **Scope:** either surviving official dihedral external split design.
- **Dependencies:** quotient external-product ledger and pair-Lagrange normal
  form.
- **Consumer:** `rate_half_band_closure` and a finite split-divisor theorem.
- **Falsifier:** a failed interpolation value `(5)`, dependent
  `I,M_0,M_1,M_2`, two orbit coordinates producing proportional complements,
  or fewer divisors than `(PCT6)`.
- **Nonclaim:** no upper bound below `3e-3` for split divisors on the quadric
  is proved here.
