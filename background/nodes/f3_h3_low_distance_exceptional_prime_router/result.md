# Result

At each fixed dyadic order, every row prime with a cutoff-18 rich product
fiber belongs to a finite union of prime divisors of low-distance cyclotomic
collision norms. The union can be generated over Galois/exchange orbits.
Rows outside it have `X_18=Y_18=0`; surviving factors still require exact row
checks. At `n=8192`, even a restricted norm-three family already gives at
least `530,590,075` Galois/exchange orbits, so a complete implementation must
classify or merge norm templates before launching raw factor shards.
