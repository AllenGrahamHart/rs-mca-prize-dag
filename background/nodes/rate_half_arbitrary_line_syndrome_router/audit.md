# Audit

The router keeps three quantities separate:

1. the combined syndrome condition, which gives a line-codeword agreement;
2. the two individual syndrome conditions, which give a mutual extension on
   the same support;
3. the number of distinct finite slopes, which is the prize numerator.

The historical Modal launcher explicitly describes its fiber table as the
list count for words `X^k L_T0` and labels its zero-sum instrument as a
quotient-level construction. Neither instrument quantifies over arbitrary
syndrome pairs. Running that launcher cannot settle the non-polynomial flank.

The toy census is deliberately interpreted only as a lifting target. Small
MDS rows can have saturated bad-slope pencils for reasons that disappear at
the official parameters.
