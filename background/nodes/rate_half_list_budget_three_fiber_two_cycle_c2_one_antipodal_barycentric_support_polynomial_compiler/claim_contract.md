# Claim contract

- **claim:** the barycentric negation coloring is computed pointwise by the
  even polynomial `J` in `(BSP1)`, and minimum support is equivalent to the
  exact split-divisor gate `(BSP5)`.
- **scope:** complete canonical candidates in the official one-antipodal
  `c=2` chamber.
- **dependency:** the barycentric negation syndrome and its canonical-cell
  notation.
- **falsifier:** a covered candidate for which `(BSP2)--(BSP5)` fails.
- **nonclaims:** no split divisor is excluded, no larger-support case is
  classified, and no adjacent-crossing status changes.
