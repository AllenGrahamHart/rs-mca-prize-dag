# Claim contract

- **claim:** intersecting the c=2 pair-trace resolvent, outer invariant cubic,
  and dyadic trace recurrence gives an exact selector for actual denominator
  pairs satisfying both invariant coupling and quotient torsion.
- **scope:** the generic canonical `c=2` denominator-mismatch chamber.
- **dependencies:** the exact mismatch trace resolvent and the proved
  outer torsion-trace recurrence.
- **falsifier:** a zero of the triple gcd which is not an actual pair trace,
  a qualifying pair trace omitted by it, or failure of the terminal trace
  equation to be equivalent to `(A/B)^(2^40)=1`.
- **nonclaims:** the selector is not evaluated at the official parameters and
  does not enforce canonical span, either gap equation, or cycle closure.
