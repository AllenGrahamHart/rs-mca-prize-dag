# Attack plan

Audit the implication rather than either premise. A falsifier must exhibit a
row satisfying C1' and WCL-ZONE at all 34 levels for which

```text
product_(L=1)^34 E_U[rho_L] > 2^100,
```

or identify a defect in the schedule, field-size pin, level count, or exact
integer comparison. Counterexamples to C1' or WCL-ZONE attack their own red
nodes and do not refute this conditional theorem.
