# Result

The active-row resultant need not be formed as a product over `6e+3` rows.
After removing the explicit special-fiber resultants, it is the exact quotient
of the norm against the sparse binomial `X^N-1`. Under the external design,
that full norm is the `r`th power of the complete supported-slope locator,
times only the core and omitted-row forms.

This changes the preferred CR-003 implementation from a dense active-row
product to a sparse subgroup norm, exact division by known factors, and the
already-proved radical/splitting test.
