# Claim contract

- **claim:** quotient migration at threshold `A` splits into slopes of maximum
  agreement exactly `A` and the existing `B_(A+1)` carry; every paid exact
  quotient support is the witness codeword's full agreement set.
- **scope:** any RS code, received affine line, finite declared fiber family,
  and `A>=k+1` covered by the quotient boundary owner.
- **currency:** distinct finite quotient slopes, first-matched across
  thresholds, scales, and witness codewords.
- **consumer effect:** removes cross-threshold duplication and arbitrary
  support-subset multiplicity from the remaining XR quotient image.
- **nonclaims:** no bound on full-agreement quotient images, generic charts,
  or `B_(A+1)`, and no mismatch-bridge close.
- **falsifier:** a slope outside `B_(A+1)` with an `A`-support witness whose
  codeword has another agreement coordinate, or a threshold quotient slope
  outside both the maximum-agreement exact image and `B_(A+1)`.
