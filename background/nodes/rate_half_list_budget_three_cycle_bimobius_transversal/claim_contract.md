# Claim contract

- **claim:** every 4-cycle budget-three witness has two complete block fibers
  and two injective Mobius graph fibers as in `(CBM1)`; for `d>=8`, the
  `A_0,A_1` pencil has exactly two fully domain-split degree-`d-1` members.
- **scope:** every field and multiplicative-coset domain supporting the
  canonical 4-cycle normal form; exact-two conclusion at `d>=8`.
- **consumer effect:** the unique no-constant-pencil chamber is reduced to an
  explicit primitive bi-Mobius object; no status promotion.
- **nonclaim:** no 4-cycle exclusion, list upper bound, or adjacent crossing.
- **falsifier:** a cycle witness violating a graph identity, a noninjective
  graph map with distinct edge roots, or a third fully split pencil member at
  `d>=8`.
