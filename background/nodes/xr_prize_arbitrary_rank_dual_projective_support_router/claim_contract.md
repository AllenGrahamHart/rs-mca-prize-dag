# Claim contract

- **claim:** every support-minimal trade circuit of rank at least two is one
  dual projective map with hyperplane-complement supports, a coefficient-slope
  projective circuit, and interaction dimension `2r-t+1`
- **scope:** all rank-at-least-two circuits in normalized prize P-A
  flat-nullity first-core packets
- **output:** `(AR1)--(AR7)`, repeated-direction routing, maximal-arity
  orthogonality, and the trade-row converse
- **currency:** an `r`-dimensional dual space and the Segre embedding
  `P^(r-1) x P^1`
- **nonclaim:** no projective-map count, no full block reconstruction beyond
  parent certificates, no aggregate P-A payment
- **falsifier:** an in-scope circuit violating the projective support form,
  coefficient circuit rank, multiplicity-two bound, or interaction dimension
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
