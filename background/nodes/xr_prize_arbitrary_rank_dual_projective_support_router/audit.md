# Audit

- The theorem applies to support-minimal row-scaling circuits, not an
  arbitrary undecomposed trade.
- Projective coefficient multiplicity at most two is asserted only for
  `r>=2`; rank-one three-block circuits are handled separately.
- Hyperplane sections can overlap when `r>2`. Pairwise-disjoint fiber language
  from rank two is not imported.
- Maximal arity forces zero interaction because the Segre columns span all
  `2r` coordinates.
- The converse certifies trade rows and retains parent selected-block
  certificates.
- No Modal or large local computation is used.
