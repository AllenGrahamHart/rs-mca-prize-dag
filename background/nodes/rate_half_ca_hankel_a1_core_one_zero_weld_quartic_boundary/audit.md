# Audit

- `K=0` is an exact branch of the proved weld, not an assumption about the
  generic component.
- Unique factorization is applied to the squarefree product `B_XE_Z`; the
  two variable groups make all listed linear factors coprime.
- Map degrees are computed on the normalization of `Q_*=0`. Total extension
  degrees include inseparable degree, so the argument is characteristic-free.
- Coprimality `gcd(e_*,2e_*+1)=1` is load-bearing.
- The strict inequalities in `(7)` use both `b<=floor(e/5)` and official
  `e=3 mod 5`.
- When `b=0`, the full residual generator is irreducible; no residual
  component is silently retained.
- The three omitted domain factors are the factors assigned to `B`, not three
  root omissions of `Q_*`.
- The quartic boundary remains open.
