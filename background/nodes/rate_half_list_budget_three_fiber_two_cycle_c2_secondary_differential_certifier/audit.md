# Audit

1. `B` ends at index `2H-3`; the two missing primary coefficients are not
   included.
2. The top residual coefficient contains `E_4b`, and may vanish when `b=0`.
3. The factor multiplying `B` in `(SDC4)` is
   `4H-4(H-1)kappa z`; both the sign and factor four matter.
4. Differentiating a congruence loses one order, but every differentiated
   term is multiplied by `z`, restoring reduction modulo `z^H`.
5. Sufficiency uses the invertible diagonal `2n+4H`, not uniqueness of a
   guessed square root.
6. The criterion is compatible with nonparity gap-only packets; official
   torsion must still be used.
