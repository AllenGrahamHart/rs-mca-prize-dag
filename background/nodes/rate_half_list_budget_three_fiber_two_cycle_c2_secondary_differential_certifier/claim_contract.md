# Claim contract

- **claim:** after the primary double gap, the secondary two-window square is
  equivalent to one explicit differential divisibility involving only
  `E,B,C,b,c`.
- **scope:** the generic boundary in characteristic zero or greater than
  `8H-8`, including the official c2 row.
- **dependency:** the exact primary-tail and two-window square reduction.
- **falsifier:** a primary-gap packet for which `(SDC2)` fails, or a normalized
  `C` satisfying exactly one of `(SDC4)` and `(SDC5)`.
- **nonclaims:** the certifier does not prove parity, root torsion, canonical
  span, or c2 exclusion.
