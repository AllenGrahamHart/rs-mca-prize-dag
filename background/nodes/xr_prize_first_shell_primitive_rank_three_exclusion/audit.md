# Audit

- The zero sets record roots inside `X`. A singleton row polynomial may have
  another root outside `X`; it is not silently declared linear.
- Rank three equals the complete dual `GRS_a` dimension only on the first
  residual shell `|X|=a+3`.
- The graph intersection energy `I` is nonnegative and strengthens the
  deficit. Dropping it is safe for the prize exclusion.
- The block-extension argument counts pair-incidence supply, not distinct
  coordinates alone.
- `(P3E5)` excludes relations active on every primitive core block. It does
  not delete proper local edge circuits from an over-budget family.
- The RowC core-size caps exceed `h`; no RowC rank-three exclusion is claimed.
