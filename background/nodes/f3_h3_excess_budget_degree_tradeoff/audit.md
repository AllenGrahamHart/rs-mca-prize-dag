# Audit

The low-tail payment uses the total quotient mass once. It does not assign a
fresh `n^2` allowance to every product target.

The exact residual budget is `B_E(n)`, not merely `(300-17E)n^2`. The latter
is printed only to compare constants and is strictly more conservative for
`E>0`.

The cutoffs are sufficient alternatives, not simultaneous budget columns.
One must select one `E` and prove its complete high-tail estimate.

The degree threshold applies to every retained target, but it does not by
itself bound the number or norm of the resulting ideals. This node therefore
remains evidence into C36' rather than a close.
