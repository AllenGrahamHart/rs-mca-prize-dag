# Result

C36' may now be attacked through any of five explicit high-tail interfaces:

```text
center degree >=4   with numerator budget about 300n^2,
center degree >=6   with numerator budget about 266n^2,
center degree >=8   with numerator budget about 198n^2,
center degree >=10  with numerator budget about 130n^2,
center degree >=12  with numerator budget about  62n^2.
```

The exact budgets retain the positive lower-order correction
`51En-34E`. This isolates a concrete proof-design choice: count more weak
ideals, or demand more common generators from every surviving rich fiber.
