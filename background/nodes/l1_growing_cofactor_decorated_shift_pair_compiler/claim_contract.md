# Claim contract - L1 decorated shift-pair compiler

## Inputs

- two distinct members of one exact agreement shell;
- the forced common cofactor degree from fixed-cofactor transport;
- exactness of both locator/cofactor factorizations.

## Outputs

- an exact ordered second-moment record `(G,A,B,N,Q_1,Q_2,R)`;
- exact scalar-cofactor identification with locator-Q shift pairs;
- primitive decoration uniqueness for `e<=w`;
- a separate common-cofactor/tangency branch.

## Consumer rule

In the primitive `e<=w` range, count ordered split support pairs, not arbitrary
cofactor decorations. Route `gcd(Q_1,Q_2)>1` separately and retain its domain
core/off-domain factor data. Keep `e>w` open.

## Nonclaims

No support-pair count is proved. No common-cofactor payment is proved. No
uniqueness is asserted for `e>w`.

## Falsifier

An exact ordered pair violating `(DS3)` or `(DS4)`, two distinct primitive
decorations of one ordered `(A,B)` with `e<=w`, or a common cofactor factor
having a domain root outside `G`.
