# Result

Every oriented shift fiber of width at least `K` in a P-B support family has
multiplicity at most one. In particular, this pays the complete official
terminal-width interval more sharply than Plotkin. The more general terminal
bound remains `8H`, with aggregate contribution below `M^3/n^2` whenever
the family itself exceeds `8n^3`.

The unresolved additive structure is exactly the repeated
oriented-difference range `H<=t<=K-1`. This is a theorem-level Boolean-energy
reduction, not a proof of P-B or of CAP25's local SPI input.
