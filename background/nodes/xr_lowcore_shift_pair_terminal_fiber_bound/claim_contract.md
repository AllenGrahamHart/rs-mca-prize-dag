# Claim contract

- **claim id:** `xr_lowcore_shift_pair_terminal_fiber_bound`
- **status:** PROVED
- **input:** a constant-weight support family with pairwise intersection at
  most `a-H`; in P-B, global genericity proves distinct-support injectivity
- **currency:** multiplicity of one oriented indicator difference and its
  contribution to additive difference energy
- **proved range:** every shift width with `N-2t<=4H`
- **uniform fiber budget:** `8H`
- **aggregate terminal-energy budget:** `8H M(M-1)`
- **stronger wide-shift payment:** every `t>=K` fiber has multiplicity at
  most one and all such fibers contribute at most `M(M-1)`
- **consumer:** `xr_lowcore_spread_heart`
- **remaining repeated-fiber range:** `H<=t<=K-1`
- **nonclaim:** no bound on narrow shift fibers, no lower bound on total
  energy, no CAP25 local locator-SPI bound, and no P-B slope-count conclusion
- **falsifier:** a family satisfying the printed intersection cap with a
  terminal oriented difference fiber larger than `(TS4)` or `(TS5)`
