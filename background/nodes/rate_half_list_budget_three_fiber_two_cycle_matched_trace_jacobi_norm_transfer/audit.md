the even-Jacobi transform lowers this to `M`. The minus cyclotomic order is

## Field audit

is invertible. The congruence `p=1 mod 2^40` puts `iota` and `sqrt(2)` in
`F_p`. In the nonsplit class the field router forces

## Coverage audit

classifiers. PASS with no official-compatible divisor closes both six-branch
packets. A divisor or nontrivial torsion gcd must still pass the signed norm,
nonzero-next-coefficient, official field, distinctness, and corrected
`T/q_out=W^4` gates.
