# Result

The quotient-distance-three branch sits exactly on the residual MDS
minimum-distance boundary, rather than below it. This leaves only two clean
ordinary-fiber types: a forced support obtained by cancelling two exceptional
root coefficients, or a disjoint `2r+2`-point Vandermonde circuit whose
external locator has constant `G/A` value on the canonical triple.

The official incidence budget is tight enough to determine the whole
composition. Exactly `e` internal fibers partition the `2e` exceptional
roots into cancelled pairs, while exactly `3e` external fibers have total
outside-row deficit `e`. A finite census can now reject any packet that does
not have this exact structure before lower Hankel or reciprocal elimination.
