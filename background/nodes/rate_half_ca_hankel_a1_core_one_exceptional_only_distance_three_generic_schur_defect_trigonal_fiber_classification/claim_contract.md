# Claim contract

- **Claim:** on the rank-three pair-locator branch, the complete defect of
  the `3e+1` quadratic-product cap is exactly the linear solution space
  `D_i | R-y_iB`, and every nonzero defect recovers one cubic rational-map
  fiber structure.
- **Scope:** the generic side of the distance-three pair-locator dichotomy.
- **Dependencies:** pair-Lagrange quadratic-product gate and the rank-two /
  rank-three locator dichotomy.
- **Consumer:** `rate_half_band_closure` and `CR-003` packet classification.
- **Falsifier:** unequal dimensions in `(GSDFC4)`, a defect functional whose
  affine lines are not pairwise intersecting, or a rank-three packet whose
  pairwise-intersecting lines are not concurrent.
- **Nonclaim:** no external split design, boundary gate, resultant identity,
  or exclusion of the saturated or trigonal branch is supplied.
