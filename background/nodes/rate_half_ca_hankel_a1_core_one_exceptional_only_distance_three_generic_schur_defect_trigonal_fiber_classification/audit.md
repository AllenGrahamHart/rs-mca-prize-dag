# Audit

- The rank-three hypothesis is load-bearing. Pairwise-intersecting affine
  lines can be coplanar without being concurrent when the `D_i` directions
  span only two dimensions; that is the separately classified Mobius branch.
- The solution system is homogeneous and linear in the three coefficients of
  `R` and the `e` values `y_i`; no fiber level is guessed or enumerated.
- A zero `y_i` is retained rather than divided away. It means `D_i|R`, and
  degree bounds allow at most one such pair.
- The exact `F_101` fiber ladder verifies `(GSDFC4)` for every prefix
  `3<=e<=12`: ambient/product ranks are `(3e+1,3e)` and the recovery kernel
  has dimension one. A one-pair mutation at `e=12` gives `(37,37)` and zero
  recovery kernel.
- Two independent recovery solutions would place every `D_i` in the span of
  their two quadratics. This is the fail-closed reason that a generic packet
  can drop by exactly one rank, never more.
- The theorem classifies a product-space defect, not the official external
  incidence design. Both output branches remain live.
