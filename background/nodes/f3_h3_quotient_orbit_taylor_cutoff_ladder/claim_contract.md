# Claim contract

- **claim:** orbitwise Taylor-resultant coefficient content has exact odd
  prime support at every cutoff `c>=2`, and these supports descend as the
  cutoff increases.
- **scope:** every dyadic order and odd prime; the low-cutoff pre-screen is
  consumed only with exact filtering back to cutoff 35.
- **dependencies:** the cutoff-35 Taylor-content argument and the general
  quotient-algebra cutoff support compiler.
- **falsifier:** a mismatch between content divisibility and a common
  derivative root at any cutoff, or a prime in a higher-cutoff content but
  absent from a lower-cutoff content of the same block.
- **nonclaims:** no optimal cutoff, valuation, implementation, factorization,
  resource, moment, or C36' theorem is supplied.
