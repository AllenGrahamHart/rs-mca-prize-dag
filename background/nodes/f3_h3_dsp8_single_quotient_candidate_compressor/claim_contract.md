# Claim contract

- **claim:** the one-lift star-coupling ideal is exactly independent of the
  chosen star center and remains nonzero when the selected coupling vanishes;
  the cutoff-35 scalar annihilator has exactly the official-prime support of
  the `(36,1)` survivor rectangle and divides the cutoff-18 annihilator.
- **scope:** every official H3 row and every selected `(36,1)` product-star /
  quotient packet.
- **dependencies:** the proved product star, coupling-difference identity,
  shifted-product Sidonicity, and general quotient-algebra support theorem.
- **falsifier:** two center choices producing different ideals, a zero
  coupling that makes the star ideal zero, an official prime where
  `p|s_(n,35)^X` disagrees with `P>=36,R>=1`, or failure of
  `s_(n,35)^X|s_(n,18)^X`.
- **nonclaims:** no annihilator algorithm, factorization, survivor count,
  moment bound, DSP8 promotion, or C36' close is asserted.
