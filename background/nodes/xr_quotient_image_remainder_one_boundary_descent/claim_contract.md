# Claim contract

- **claim:** every quotient-remainder slope at any support size `B>=A`
  descends with the same slope to an exact-`A` quotient support, unless it is
  born at a one-point tail over a contained pure-fiber core.
- **scope:** Reed-Solomon rows, one fixed fiber size `c|n`, and `A>=k+1`.
- **currency:** distinct finite slopes / linear factors of the exact image
  lcm.
- **consumer effect:** removes every quotient remainder other than the
  remainder-one tangent boundary from agreement-induction migration.
- **downstream owner:** `xr_quotient_boundary_agreement_raise_owner` carries
  the complete one-point-tail union into the original `B_(A+1)` induction
  term.
- **nonclaims:** no boundary-image bound, profile coverage, generic payment,
  or XR close.
- **falsifier:** a noncontained quotient-remainder support with neither a
  profile-preserving noncontained deletion nor a contained pure-fiber
  predecessor at remainder one.
