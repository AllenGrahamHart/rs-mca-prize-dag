# Claim contract

- **claim:** the fixed selected-antipodal infinity quartet is exactly one
  subgroup scale times `1,y,A_a(y),B_a(y)`, where `a^2=-2`, with the printed
  product, scale-free quarter-order gate, and square-root involution.
- **scope:** complete one-antipodal `c=2` packets with minimum barycentric
  support whose selected denominator pair is the unique antipodal pair.
- **dependencies:** the selected-antipodal collision normal form and the
  infinity-cell torsion gate.
- **consumer:** the fixed selected-antipodal shard of
  `rate_half_list_adjacent_crossing`.
- **falsifier:** a covered packet for which `(SAI2)--(SAI8)` fail.
- **nonclaims:** the affine subgroup intersection is not proved empty or
  bounded, and its solutions need not extend to canonical packets.
