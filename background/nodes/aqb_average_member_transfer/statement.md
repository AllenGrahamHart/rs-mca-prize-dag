# aqb_average_member_transfer

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

For a finite AQB averaged quotient-box family, if the family-average list
contribution exceeds the MCA trigger after the certified shared entropy gain
is applied, then at least one member of the family individually exceeds the
trigger and gives an unsafe witness at `sigma*`.

## Falsifier

A finite family whose average exceeds a threshold while every member is at or
below that threshold.
