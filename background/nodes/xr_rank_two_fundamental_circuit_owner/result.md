# Result

A rank-two trade now has a canonical star atlas. Its lexicographically first
coefficient basis has three or four anchors, and each non-anchor block owns
one unique fundamental four/five-block circuit against those anchors. These
fundamental vectors form a basis of the full row-scaling kernel and reconstruct
the original all-one trade exactly.

This closes intratrade decomposition ambiguity. It does not yet select the
first trade or Maxwell core containing a block, count realized support
embeddings, or pay the cross-core slope aggregate.
