# Audit

1. The crossing equation has a plus sign after replacing `f_lk` by `G_l`.
   The sign comes from `D_k'(b_k)=-D_k'(a_k)` and the odd third power.
2. `G_l` contains `B^4`, `(A')^4`, and `D_l^2`. Each exponent is
   load-bearing in the derivative normalization.
3. The pair row is homogeneous in the quartic coefficients. Rank five
   forces the zero polynomial; rank four generally leaves one projective
   candidate and does not prove existence of compatible labels.
4. A valid `P_l` is nonzero at every root outside `D_l`, not merely nonzero
   as a polynomial.
5. Five rows first occur at `e=6`. The `e=4,5` controls cannot reach rank
   five for dimensional reasons.
6. The deterministic controls are support/matching route fences, not
   external split designs or a universal classification. The complete
   `10,395`-matching census fixes one exceptional/triple support partition;
   it does not range over support subsets.
7. The abstract antiweight construction proves that arbitrary nonzero pair
   weights are insufficient. Any universal rank argument must retain the
   exact smooth weight `X(X-s)(X-x_0)B^4(A')^4`.
