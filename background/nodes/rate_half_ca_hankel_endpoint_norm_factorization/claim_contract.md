# Claim contract

- **Claim:** every counterexample at the first strict `A=3` endpoint obeys
  the low-defect norm-power identity `(ENF2)`, the complementary split
  factorization `(ENF4)` over at least `15/16` of the evaluation domain, the
  clean-fiber lower bound `(ENF5)`, and the exact two-sided MDS-excess ledger
  `(ENF6)`.
- **Scope:** only the extremal profile already forced by
  `rate_half_ca_hankel_endpoint_saturation_rigidity`.
- **Dependencies:** endpoint saturation supplies `T=4m+1`,
  `O<=m-1`, the exact column deficit, and divisibility of every saturated
  column form by the supported-slope polynomial.
- **Consumer:** the terminal strict `A=3` part of
  `rate_half_band_closure`.
- **Nonclaims:** neither identity is proved impossible; `J` and `S` need not
  be coprime; `Vbar` is not asserted to be an apolar generator; no `e>m` or
  `A=1` profile is covered.
- **Next exact gate:** exclude `(ENF2)` or `(ENF4)` using the Hankel/apolar
  origin of `Q` and the order-`2^41` multiplicative-domain polynomial, or
  classify the full-row/full-column near-equality words in `(ENF6)` and show
  that no equality family is column-far.  A classification of globally
  minimum tensor-product words is not sufficient.
