No exact external split design exists on the official
`A=1,s=1,e=2m-1` distance-three face.

Every such design would supply, for every omitted exceptional pair, a
nonzero quartic in the corresponding support pair-crossing kernel and
nonvanishing on every retained pair. It would therefore lie in the
all-deficient quartic-support branch. The Pade residual discriminant theorem
excludes that entire branch. Hence the distance-three chart is empty.
