# Proof

Assume an exact external split design exists. For each omitted pair `l`, the
pair-crossing rank gate constructs an `(e-1) x 5` matrix `R_l`. The design
supplies a nonzero quartic coefficient vector `p_l` with

```text
R_l p_l=0
```

whose polynomial is nonzero on both roots of every retained pair. In
particular, every `R_l` is deficient and the packet belongs to the complete
all-deficient quartic-support branch.

The Pade residual discriminant exclusion consumes exactly that branch,
including its antiweight, bounded-tail dihedral, Laurent, and quartic
pullback alternatives, and proves that no official packet in it exists.
This contradicts the assumed design. QED.
