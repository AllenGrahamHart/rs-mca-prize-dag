# Claim contract

- **claim:** W3 row scope is parametric, not a named finite row slate.
- **inputs:** an admissible exact row descriptor and the W3 cell/crossing data.
- **proved output:** exact row constants are available for every input, and a
  universal close must be uniform or produced by a proved-total parameterized
  certificate procedure.
- **exhibit rule:** finitely many named rows are calibration only unless a
  transfer or totality theorem covers the remaining family.
- **nonclaim:** no upper bound on the actual non-planted list count is proved
  here.
- **consumer disposition:** `ww_row_envelope_clause` remains an open but
  retired `TARGET`; this router supplied the exact row used by its unsafe-cell
  scope counterexample.
