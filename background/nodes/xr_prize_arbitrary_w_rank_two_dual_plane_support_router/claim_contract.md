# Claim contract

- **claim:** every arbitrary-`W` rank-two four/five circuit is exactly one
  dual plane with projective zero fibers, with repeated coefficient pairs
  routed to the flat/basis owner
- **scope:** genuine rank-two circuits from the arbitrary-`W` Segre router
- **output:** `(DP1)--(DP4)`, fiber disjointness/multiplicity, repeated-pair
  ownership, and the converse reconstruction
- **currency:** a two-dimensional dual plane, projective fibers, and exact
  support-locator interpolation
- **nonclaim:** no dual-plane count, no cross-core first match, and no
  aggregate P-A payment
- **falsifier:** an in-scope circuit whose supports do not arise from `(DP2)`,
  a five-circuit direction of multiplicity at least three, or a repeated pair
  violating `(DP3)`
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
