# Audit

1. The two singular minors use complementary `e`-sets from the proved
   weighted self-dual minor law; arbitrary unrelated dependencies would not
   imply a zero product.
2. Left-kernel vectors of the column submatrices are row combinations, hence
   elements of `U_q` rather than dual-code words.
3. Evaluation is injective on `U_q` because it is injective on all of `R_A`.
4. Squarefreeness of `A` is load-bearing: it converts pointwise zero into
   zero in the quotient and excludes nonzero nilpotents.
5. The router replaces an exponential minor search with an existential
   zero-divisor pair; it does not yet supply an efficient official-scale
   construction or exclusion algorithm.
6. The gcds may overlap only in the excess-zero branch. At exact degree `e`,
   the lcm condition forces coprimality and the factorization `A=D_uD_v`.
7. Multiplication by the unit `q_1^(-1)` changes values but not exceptional
   zero sets, so `(HNA5)` needs no modular inverse.
8. Equal half-ranks use weighted self-duality, not only the vanishing of one
   complementary determinant. The nonzero diagonal weights do not alter
   puncturing/shortening dimensions.
