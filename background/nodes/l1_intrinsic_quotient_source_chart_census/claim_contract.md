# Claim contract - L1 intrinsic quotient source-chart census

## Inputs

- one received word and intrinsic complete-fiber partition;
- source cores and petals that are unions/members of that partition;
- canonical charts identified by member codewords;
- the L1 cutoff when polynomiality is invoked.

## Output

At most `3^(n/ell)` source charts per intrinsic scale and a polynomial
first-match aggregate across all dyadic intrinsic scales.

## Consumer rule

Consumers may remove chart multiplicity from fixed-box counts inside this
intrinsic complete-fiber scope. They must retain partial cores, non-intrinsic
or contributor-dependent quotient maps, and arbitrary petal locators.

## Falsifier

Two distinct source member polynomials for one `(C,T,U)`, more than `3^L`
canonical charts at one partition, or a contributor omitted by the printed
first-match order within the stated scope.
