# Published input

One finite-field subgroup bound is used.

I. Vyugin and S. Makarychev, *Solutions of polynomial equation over
F_p and new bounds of additive energy*, arXiv:1504.01354, Theorem 2: for an
admissible absolutely irreducible bidegree-`(m,n)` curve and a multiplicative
subgroup `G`,

```text
#solutions on G x G <=16 m n^2 (m+n) |G|^(2/3),
```

under `100(mn)^(3/2)<|G|<p^(3/4)/3`. For `m=n=1` this is
`32|G|^(2/3)`.

Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/1504.01354

Both nonspecial cases in the proof satisfy the theorem after at most
inverting the two subgroup coordinates. No asymptotic suppression or hidden
constant is used.
