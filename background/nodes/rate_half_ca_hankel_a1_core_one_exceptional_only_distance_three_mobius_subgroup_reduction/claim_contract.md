# Claim contract

- **Claim:** on the official prime-field row, a common Mobius involution
  pairing all `2e` exceptional subgroup roots is either antipodal or
  constant-product.
- **Scope:** the rank-two pair-locator branch with `N=2^41`,
  `e=2^38-1`, and `p>2^167`.
- **Dependencies:** pair-locator Mobius dichotomy and official prime-field
  collapse.
- **Published input:** the explicit `32N^(2/3)` subgroup-curve bound in
  `source.md`.
- **Consumer:** `rate_half_band_closure` and the CR-003 rank-two classifier.
- **Falsifier:** a non-dihedral projective involution with at least
  `2^39-2` ordered graph points on `mu_(2^41) x mu_(2^41)` in an official
  prime field.
- **Nonclaim:** antipodal and constant-product packets are not excluded.
