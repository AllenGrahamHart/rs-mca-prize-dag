# Audit

- Counted roots in the direct record convention: the sole bad row has no
  clean or exceptional root, while every saturated row has exactly `e`
  supported roots.
- Counted the exceptional fiber with `r-1`, not `r`, saturated roots.
- Used squarefree split fibers to exclude hidden roots and multiplicities.
- Kept both nonzero normalization constants and derived their inverse partners
  from the two complement equations.
- Resultant identities are necessary but are not treated as Hankel,
  coefficient-chain, or irreducibility certificates.
