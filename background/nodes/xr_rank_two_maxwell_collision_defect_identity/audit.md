# Audit

1. The union of all supports is `X`; no active coordinate is lost when the
   block union is written as `X union union O_i`.
2. The higher-multiplicity correction is `C(m_x-1,2)`, not `C(m_x,3)`.
3. Inside reuse contributes `(t-2)I` after combining extension size and pair
   collision charges.
4. `sigma` is the sum of individual nonnegative unused pair budgets. Hence
   `sigma=0` means every pair saturates, not only aggregate equality by
   cancellation.
5. Formula `(MD4)` is an identity for every compatible family. The condition
   `Delta<=0` is used only for the full-core consequences.
6. At `D_0=0`, `I=0` uses `t>=4`; no division by a possibly zero coefficient
   occurs.
7. `H=0` permits outside multiplicity two and excludes multiplicity at least
   three.
8. No count of bounded-defect configurations is claimed.
9. No Modal or candidate-row computation is used.
