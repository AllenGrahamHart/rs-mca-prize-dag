# Claim contract - L1 background-overlap singleton payment

## Inputs

- one maximal-sunflower source chart and its strict background remainder;
- one defect degree and exact labelled petal support;
- degree-`<k` codewords at the L1 list threshold.

## Output

Every cell with `a+s<ell+g` is a singleton. At fixed petal polarity these
cells are polynomial per chart, and the sub-Johnson residue lies in `(BO4)`.

## Consumer rule

Consumers may impose `a+s>=ell+g`, equivalently
`c<=2a-ell-g`, on the bounded-polarity sub-Johnson branch. Equality must be
retained.

## Falsifier

Two distinct compatible codewords in a cell satisfying `(BO2)`.
