most `v` exceptional slopes are removed, it is a coherent `GRS_a` Maxwell
core with at least `v(t-2)+e+1` independent trades. Its trade space splits
into:

1. exact rank-one common-support records `(S,Q,alpha)` with
   `a+1<=|S|<=a+v`; and

Any future proof or contributor registry must count these two strata
separately over the official `v` intervals in `(LC7)`.
