# Claim contract

- **claim:** the prize `u=0,v>=1` endpoint has a Maxwell core with a printed
  trade-space dimension, and all rank-one trades are exact common-support
  polynomial defects
- **scope:** first-open prize P-A flat-nullity cells after exceptional-slope
  deletion and global-root puncturing
- **output:** `(LC1)--(LC7)`, including the rank-one support/polynomial normal
  form and the surviving official `v` intervals
- **currency:** coherent `GRS_a` extension, minimal dense core, stack-rank
  nullity, and dual-GRS locator cancellation
- **nonclaim:** no count of rank-one records, no rank-at-least-two payment, no
  P-B theorem, and no cross-cell aggregate
- **falsifier:** an in-scope core violating `(LC3)`, or a rank-one trade not
  admitting `(LC4)--(LC6)`
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
