# Result

Constructing the global product and quotient polynomials no longer requires
enumerating their shifted-product or quotient factors. Each derivative packet
is obtained from the binomial polynomial `F_n` by a resultant truncated
modulo `U^19`. The packets are still indexed by individual quotient lifts.
The remaining blocker is an official-scale recurrence, modular resultant, or
norm-template algorithm that also aggregates that index with a proved
coverage and cost bound; raw orbit and dense resultant campaigns remain out
of scope.
