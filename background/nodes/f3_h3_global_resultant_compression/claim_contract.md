# Claim contract

- **claim:** the product polynomial, quotient polynomial, and each cleared
  first-nineteen derivative packet are exact resultants of the one explicit
  shifted-root polynomial `F_n`;
- **scope:** dyadic orders `n>=4` over `Z[zeta_n]`;
- **consumer:** the derivative-ideal and fixed-order C36' routes supporting
  `f3_h3_mobius_excess_half`;
- **dependency:** the definitions and integral packet in
  `f3_h3_global_derivative_ideal_valuation`;
- **falsifier:** any dyadic order for which `(GRC1)`--`(GRC4)` fail, including
  a missing diagonal scalar or an incorrect clearing exponent;
- **nonclaims:** no bound or factorization of `e_n` or `f_n`, no official-row
  closure, no elimination of the `(u,v)` packet index, and no claim that a
  dense degree-`n` resultant is affordable;
- **upstream mapping:** a constants-first algebraic compiler for the
  primitive shift-pair/residual-ray exceptional-prime route.
