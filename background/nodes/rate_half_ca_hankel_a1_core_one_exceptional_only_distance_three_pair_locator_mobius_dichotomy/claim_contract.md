# Claim contract

- **Claim:** the exceptional pair locators span dimension two or three, and
  dimension two is equivalent to one common Mobius involution pairing all
  exceptional roots.
- **Scope:** disjoint pair partitions with at least two pairs over the odd
  characteristic fields of the official distance-three chart.
- **Dependency:** pair-Lagrange normal form.
- **Consumer:** the complement-residue rank gate and
  `rate_half_band_closure`.
- **Falsifier:** disjoint pair locators of span two that are not the orbits of
  one projective involution, or a common involution whose pair locators span
  three.
- **Nonclaim:** the theorem does not bound how many points of the official
  multiplicative subgroup a non-dihedral Mobius involution can pair.
