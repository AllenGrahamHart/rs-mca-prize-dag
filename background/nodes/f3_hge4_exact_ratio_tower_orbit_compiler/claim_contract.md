# Claim contract

## Proved

- Primitive shift-pair scaling orbits split bijectively by their exact ratio
  subgroup, as in `(ERT2)`.
- Every exact-level orbit has exactly `h` left-anchored presentations.
- The levelwise constant `(21/2)m^2` geometrically implies the existing
  `14n^2` HGE4 orbit budget.
- The near-square conditions in `(ERT7)` project exactly to the left-anchored,
  non-full, exact-level base locus.

## Consumer

`f3_hge4_norm_gate_count`, through its proved primitive orbit interface.

## Falsifier

Two distinct exact levels representing one ambient orbit, an ambient
primitive orbit omitted by all exact levels, a wrong anchor multiplicity, or
a near-square base pair misclassified by `(ERT7)` falsifies this theorem.

## Nonclaims

- The levelwise estimates `(ERT4)` and `(ERT6)` remain unproved.
- No exact-level census, characteristic certificate, or uniform count is
  supplied.
- Counting selector witnesses is invalid; only projected base pairs count.
- Failure of the sufficient constant `21/2` would not itself falsify HGE4.
