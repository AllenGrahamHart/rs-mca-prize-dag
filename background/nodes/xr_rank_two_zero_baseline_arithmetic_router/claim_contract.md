# Claim contract

- **claim:** a zero-baseline rank-two full Maxwell core at an official row has
  even divisor arity, even `D=d+1`, a multiplicity-at-most-two outside
  collision design, and the exact rank floors in the statement.
- **scope:** active rows of the rank-two relation are all blocks of one
  minimal uniform Maxwell core; `D_0=0`; official odd reserves.
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`.
- **effect:** excludes every odd active-row arity, removes five-row
  zero-baseline full cores, collapses arity four to four disjoint zero fibers,
  and reduces all remaining arities to divisors of `h` above a row-sharp rank
  floor.
- **nonclaims:** no statement for `D_0<0`, proper local circuits that are not
  full cores, higher trade rank, nonuniform cells, or the final slope count.
- **falsifier:** an official zero-baseline full core with odd `t`, odd `D`, a
  nonzero defect charge, an arity/rank outside the exact table, or a four-row
  example violating `(ZB7)`.
