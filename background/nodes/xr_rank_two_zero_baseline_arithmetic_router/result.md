# Result

`PROVED`. Zero-baseline rank-two full Maxwell cores at every official row
have even divisor arity and odd shell depth. Five-row cores are impossible;
four-row cores have no inactive extensions and are complements of four
disjoint `h`-point zero fibers. The arithmetic rank floors are
`10,10,6,8601474050,8601474050,4302648690` on the six rows.

The surviving problem is the strictly negative-baseline and proper-circuit
record count, together with higher-rank and nonuniform branches.
