# xr_profile_minor_record_inventory_payload

- **status:** TARGET
- **closure:** proof or certificate

## Statement

Construct the actual inventory of XR minor-certificate records covering every
budget-meeting unpaid non-boundary light-triangle profile, with each record:

- triangular with nonzero diagonal;
- monomial/noncancellation-certified; or
- part of a complete remote nonzero-minor certificate table.

## Falsifier

A live XR profile missing from the inventory, an inadmissible record, or a
listed record whose determinant evidence is zero.
