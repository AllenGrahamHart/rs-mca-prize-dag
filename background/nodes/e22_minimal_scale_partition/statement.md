# e22_minimal_scale_partition

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

For dyadic E22 support-scale data, the support classes with at least one
admissible quotient modulus decompose disjointly by their unique minimal
admissible modulus.

Equivalently, the tail-minimal predicates from
`e22_minimal_scale_tail_criterion`,

```text
|B_M(R)| < M
and
|B_{M'}(R)| >= M' for every smaller admissible M' < M,
```

form a disjoint partition of the selected canonical representatives.

## Falsifier

A dyadic support class with two distinct minimal admissible moduli, or with at
least one admissible modulus but no tail-minimal partition cell.
