# Result

The final WCL leaf also has a fixed-dimensional endpoint. Weight-six
relations at the order-512 terminal level are exactly the five-variable norm
divisors

```text
E(Y)^2-YB(Y)^2 divides Y^256-1,
deg E=3 monic,       deg B<=1.
```

Its remainder ideal is the unit ideal over `Q`, so a single checked integer
certificate replaces a blind weight-six support or norm fleet.
