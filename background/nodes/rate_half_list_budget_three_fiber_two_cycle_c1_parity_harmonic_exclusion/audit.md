# Audit

1. The campaign covers all integer characteristic candidates, not only
   probable primes.
2. Multiples of five are explicitly counted as composite rejections.
3. Both `H_R` and `H_P` terminals are checked.
4. A no-hit result is used only after the field router proves the finite
   interval and trace equivalence.
5. The verdict closes harmonic `c=1` parity only; matched, nonharmonic, and
   nonparity scopes are not conflated.
