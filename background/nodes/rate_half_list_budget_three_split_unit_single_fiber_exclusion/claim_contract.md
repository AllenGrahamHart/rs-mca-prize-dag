# Claim contract

- **claim:** the one-degree-`d`-quotient-fiber completion `(SFE2)` is
  incompatible with every one of the nine proved split-unit chamber
  relations whenever the total supported deficit is at most `d`.
- **scope:** the nine linear Grassmann chambers in odd characteristic at
  `d>=8`; all official rows included.
- **requirement:** `rate_half_list_budget_three_linear_grassmann_atlas`.
- **consumer effect:** removes the direct one-fiber quotient-periodic
  construction from the split-unit side; no status promotion.
- **nonclaim:** primitive, multi-fiber, or quadratic-scroll configurations are
  not excluded, and no list safe point or adjacent crossing is proved.
- **falsifier:** a nonzero relation satisfying `(SFE1)--(SFE3)`, or a chamber
  relation-support deficit row different from the displayed table.
