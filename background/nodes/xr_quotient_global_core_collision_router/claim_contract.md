# Claim contract

- **claim:** maximum-agreement quotient slopes split globally by their
  `(scale,k-point fiber core)` key; singleton keys fit `B_quot_ub(A_0)`, while
  nonsingleton keys are common-core collision slopes.
- **scope:** rate-preserving quotient scales on cyclic RS rows of rate at most
  `1/4`, support-wise noncontained quotient witnesses, initial reserve
  `t_0>=1`, and the proved maximum-agreement owner.
- **currency:** distinct quotient slopes across every raised threshold,
  first-matched across scales and full agreement witnesses.
- **consumer effect:** removes a separate quotient-image/lcm debit. Generic
  multiplicity enters P-A; nongeneric multiplicity stays in the mismatch
  bridge's existing common-core aggregation problem.
- **nonclaims:** no P-A bound, nongeneric aggregate, generic-chart payment, or
  XR close.
- **falsifier:** a maximum-agreement active quotient support whose complete
  fibers do not contain exactly `k` points, a singleton-core sum above the
  conservative quotient envelope, or two slopes with one key that do not
  supply a shared noncontained size-`k` core at the initial threshold.
