# Claim contract

- **claim:** two finite quotient-algebra modules, their Fitting orders, and
  their smaller scalar annihilator integers detect exactly the official row
  primes supporting positive `X_c` and positive `Y_c`;
- **scope:** dyadic `n>=4`, cutoffs `2<=c<n-1`, and support equivalence at
  primes `p=1 mod n`; the critical specialization is `c=18`;
- **consumer:** fixed-order candidate-prime generation for
  `f3_h3_mobius_excess_half`;
- **dependencies:** exact global resultants, the layered-gcd multiplicity
  compiler, and characteristic-zero shifted-product/quotient Sidonicity;
- **falsifier:** an official row prime where the relevant Fitting integer is
  divisible by `p` but the corresponding moment vanishes, or conversely;
- **nonclaims:** no full `X_c`/`Y_c` valuation, no bound or factorization of
  the annihilator/Fitting integers, and no affordable official-scale
  Smith/subresultant algorithm;
- **upstream mapping:** an exact finite exceptional-prime support compiler
  for the residual ray/primitive shift-pair correlation.
