# Result

Every HGE4 exact-level width in the upper-quarter band `m/4<h<m/3` has the
uniform orbit bound

```text
E_h^prim(m,p)<=2N(m-2h,m-3h).
```

This sharpens the closest-boundary debits to `2` and `(m+4)/3` and pays five
additional exact-level cells at `m=32,64,128,256,1024`. The remaining HGE4
problem is a necklace/cyclotomic sieve outside those paid cells together with
all lower-quarter widths; the node itself remains open.
