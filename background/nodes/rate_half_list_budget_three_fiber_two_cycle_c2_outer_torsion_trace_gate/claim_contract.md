# Claim contract

- **claim:** every official generic `c=2` mismatch survivor passes the
  forty-step outer cubic torsion-trace gcd in `(CTT5)`.
- **scope:** the canonical minimum-degree fiber-two cycle `c=2`
  denominator-mismatch stratum at quotient order `2^40`.
- **dependency:** the radical-free mismatch trace-resolvent elimination.
- **falsifier:** a valid `c=2` packet whose selected ratio has order dividing
  `2^40` but whose printed cubic is coprime to `Q_40-2`.
- **nonclaims:** the gate is not sufficient for a denominator pair or cycle,
  does not cover `c=1`, and does not authorize denominator enumeration or a
  large computation.
