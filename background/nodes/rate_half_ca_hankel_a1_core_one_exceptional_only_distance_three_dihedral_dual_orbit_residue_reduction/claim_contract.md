# Claim contract

- **Claim:** on either surviving rank-two dihedral branch, all dual
  row-product residues have the orbit-coordinate form `(DOR2)` with a fixed
  degree-seven boundary polynomial, equivalently the split-algebra equation
  `(DOR4)`.
- **Scope:** the official distance-three pair-Lagrange chart.
- **Dependencies:** dihedral boundary-order router and dual row-product power
  router.
- **Consumer:** `rate_half_band_closure` and the CR-003 rank-two classifier.
- **Falsifier:** a dihedral support packet for which direct resultant residues
  differ from `(DOR2)`, or for which `(DOR4)` is not equivalent to the
  coordinatewise gates.
- **Nonclaim:** the orbit-residue equation is not excluded in either retained
  high-order field stratum.
