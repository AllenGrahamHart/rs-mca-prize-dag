# Claim contract

- **claim:** the residual `R0` primary/torsion/constant packet is exactly two
  degree-`M` Jacobi gcds, and its torsion-only prefilters are the existing
  CR-002 norm pair.
- **scope:** the official generic two-antipodal nonharmonic `c=1` parity
  chamber, source role `R0`.
- **dependencies:** the lift-free `R0` constant gate and the universal
  torsion cyclotomic-norm decomposition.
- **falsifier:** a mismatch in the Legendre exponent, torsion sign, quadratic
  transform, gcd equivalence, or identification of the norm pair.
- **nonclaims:** neither norm nor signed gcd is evaluated; the Euclidean
  fourth-power and gcd filters remain.
