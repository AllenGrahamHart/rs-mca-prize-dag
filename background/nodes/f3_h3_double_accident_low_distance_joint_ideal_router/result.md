`Y_18` summand produces a four-generator normalized cyclotomic ideal that
simultaneously records a low-distance product star, a second quotient
representation, and equality of the product and quotient targets at one
degree-one row-prime ideal.

Primes outside the resulting finite candidate set have `Y_18=0`, so C36'
holds there by the proved cutoff-18 reduction. The critical node remains
`TARGET`: an efficient official-order template generator and a survivor
bound are still missing.

`(n,p)=(32,1153)` only to check the normalized congruences and principal-norm
divisibilities.
