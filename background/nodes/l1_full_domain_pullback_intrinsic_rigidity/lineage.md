# Lineage - L1 full-domain pullback intrinsic rigidity

The general pullback descent exposed full partitions as arbitrary quotient
label domains, and the Johnson closure paid only their positive gate. The
composition identity with the multiplicative-domain locator is stronger: it
places `K(P)` inside the cyclic extension `K(X)/K(X^n)`, whose intermediate
fields are unique at each divisor. This identifies the map itself and retires
the whole fully fiberwise full-partition residual.
