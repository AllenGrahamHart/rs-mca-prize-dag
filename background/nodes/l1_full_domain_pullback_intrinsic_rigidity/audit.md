# Audit - L1 full-domain pullback intrinsic rigidity

1. Every domain point is required to lie in a complete degree-`s` fiber.
2. Fiber labels are distinct, giving the exact composition identity `(1)`.
3. A multiplicative coset has locator `X^n-alpha^n`.
4. The field contains `mu_n`, so the function-field extension is cyclic
   Galois over the base field itself.
5. The characteristic is coprime to `n` and `s`.
6. The intermediate-field index is the polynomial degree `s`.
7. Cyclicity gives a unique subgroup of order `s`.
8. Equality of rational function fields yields a Mobius relation.
9. Polynomial behavior at infinity kills the denominator; monicity fixes the
   leading scalar.
10. Exact-periodic ownership is claimed only for supports that are unions of
    the resulting fibers.

Incomplete-domain and partial-fiber cells remain the exact attack surface.
