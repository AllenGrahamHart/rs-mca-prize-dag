# Audit

The rows deliberately fail source torsion. They falsify only the tempting
two-condition strategy "primary gap plus Legendre gate is impossible in good
characteristic." They do not falsify the deleted-pair node, whose hypotheses
include `t^(8M)=1` and hence `r^(32M)=1`.

The next primary coefficient is checked nonzero in every row. The examples
therefore do not exploit the higher-contact stratum excluded by `(DPP9)`.

The branch equations are evaluated in the reduced form `(LCC6)`. The verifier
also checks the equivalent cleared integer polynomials from CR-002-L, which
guards both the `r^6` prefactor in branch zero and the `r^4` prefactors in
branches one and two.

No broad search underlies the claim. The proof consists of three fixed rows,
recursive primality certificates, and direct modular arithmetic.
