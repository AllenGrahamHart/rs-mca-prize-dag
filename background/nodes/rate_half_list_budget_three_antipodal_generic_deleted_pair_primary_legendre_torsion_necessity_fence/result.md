# Result

The primary gap and Legendre scalar equation do not suffice by themselves:
all three branches have exact good-characteristic solutions already at
`M=1`. Every printed solution fails `r^32=1`.

CR-002 must therefore retain source torsion inside its algebraic elimination
or fast batch evaluator. Computing only the pairwise primary/Legendre
resultant can produce genuine large-prime hits and cannot certify the official
sublane.

The live theorem target is the triple system consisting of the primary gap,
source torsion, and one Legendre equation. The secondary, trace, gcd, and
fourth-power gates remain valid later rejection stages.
