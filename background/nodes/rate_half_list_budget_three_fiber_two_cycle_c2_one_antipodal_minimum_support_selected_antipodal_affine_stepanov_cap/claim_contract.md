  subgroup intersection has at most `355106851` values of `y` for either
  square-root orientation.
- **scope:** the maximal rate-half row with `N=2^40` and the exact affine
  forms from the minimum-support selected-antipodal compiler.
- **dependencies:** the affine compiler, in-house Stepanov nonvanishing
  construction, and maximal-row field-degree/characteristic bounds.
- **consumer:** the selected-antipodal minimum-support branch of
  `rate_half_list_adjacent_crossing`.
- **falsifier:** an official field and more than `355106851` distinct values
  satisfying `(SAC1)`.
- **nonclaims:** no candidate is excluded individually, no canonical gaps are
  evaluated, and the cap does not authorize local or unpriced enumeration.
