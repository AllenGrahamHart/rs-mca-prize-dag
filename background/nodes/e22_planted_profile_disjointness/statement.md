# e22_planted_profile_disjointness

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

In the E22 planted sunflower receiver with distinct nonzero petal scalars,
every planted codeword agrees on the core and exactly one full petal, with no
background agreements and no second touched petal. Hence any nondegenerate
mixed/full-petal staircase challenger is disjoint from the planted family.

## Falsifier

A planted codeword with a mixed/full-petal nonplanted profile, a second petal
agreement caused by repeated scalars, or a background agreement.
