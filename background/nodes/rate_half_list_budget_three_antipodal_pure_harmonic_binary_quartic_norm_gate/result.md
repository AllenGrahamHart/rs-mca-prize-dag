# Result

Harmonicity of some square-root lift of a deleted quartic is now one exact
support condition. The condition is symmetric, independent of all lift and
ordering choices, and evaluable either by three quadratic norms or by three
explicit radical-free pairing norms rather than by cross-ratio relabeling or
a large expanded polynomial.

The remaining pure task is unchanged in substance but has a smaller input:
exclude the zero locus of this support norm after imposing the coprime
fourth-power decomposition and the proved Euler/passport constraints.
