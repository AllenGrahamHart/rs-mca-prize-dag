# Audit

- The rank is that of the actual pairing map `L->F^2`, not the trade rank.
- In pairing rank one there is exactly one, not merely at most one,
  interactive row because the active rows span `L`.
- The sharpened `t<=2r-1` uses independence of every proper subset of the
  Segre projective circuit.
- The rank-two conic describes the quotient coefficients. It does not assert
  that the full support packet has rank two or only four/five rows.
- Augmented-orthogonal rows have `tau_i=0` and are retained, not discarded.
- No Modal or large local computation is used.
