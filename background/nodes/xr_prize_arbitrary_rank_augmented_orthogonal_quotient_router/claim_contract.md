# Claim contract

- **claim:** every arbitrary-rank trade circuit is an augmented-orthogonal
  lift of an interaction quotient of rank at most two
- **scope:** support-minimal rank-`r` circuits, `r>=2`, in normalized prize
  P-A flat-nullity first-core packets
- **output:** `(OQ1)--(OQ5)`, the exact rank-one row count and arity
  sharpening, and the rank-two slope conic
- **currency:** the pairing map from the dual row space to `<b,q>`
- **nonclaim:** no count of augmented-orthogonal subspaces, projective maps,
  supports, or cross-core first matches
- **falsifier:** an in-scope row outside the stated kernel violating `(OQ2)`,
  two interactive rows in rank one, or a rank-two quotient direction not
  proportional to `(-gamma,1)`
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
