# e22_cross_scale_rootset_recovery

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

If two E22 staircase locators at possibly different quotient moduli are equal,
then they have the same root set. Applying fixed-scale recovery at each
modulus recovers the corresponding tail and selected fibers at that scale.
Thus any hidden multiplicity is exactly a cross-scale equality of recovered
root-set data.

## Falsifier

Equal staircase locators with different root sets, or a fixed-scale
tail/fiber recovery failure.
