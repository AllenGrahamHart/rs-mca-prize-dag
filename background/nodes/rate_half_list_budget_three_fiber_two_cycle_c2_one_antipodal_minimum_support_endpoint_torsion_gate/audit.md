# Audit

1. `J_supp` is the support compiler polynomial; it is distinct from the
   binary-quartic invariant also denoted `J` elsewhere in the c=2 lane.
2. The top coefficient of `Theta` is
   `b_(r-1)c_m-b_rc_(m-1)`.  Reversing this determinant changes `Xi`.
3. Both relevant degrees are even, so the two negated top terms add rather
   than cancel and the root product has no extra minus sign.
4. The conclusion is `N/2`-torsion, not merely `N`-torsion, because every
   negation-pair product is a square in `mu_N`.
5. No official-degree polynomial or subgroup-root list is constructed.
