# Result

The exceptional first-order direction is no longer an unstructured weighted
moment vector. After quotienting the `r-1` exceptional locator columns, it
needs at least three residual-domain columns. The sharp three-column case is
one unique Vandermonde circuit whose coefficients are reconstructed from
`Theta_2`; every other packet has quotient support at least four.

For exact classification this gives a responsible split: enumerate one
canonical unordered triple and recover its weights, or use a diffuse-support
argument starting at four. No dense `M_1` matrix or arbitrary source-weight
allocation is needed in the first branch.
