# Claim contract

- **claim:** one squarefree lcm polynomial has as its roots exactly the
  distinct noncontained affine-line slopes contributed by any finite support
  family.
- **scope:** arbitrary linear codes and finite support families; the XR use is
  the quotient-remainder family over every raised agreement.
- **currency:** distinct finite slopes, not supports or witness
  multiplicities.
- **consumer effect:** replaces the quotient part of agreement induction by
  the exact target `deg R_quot`.
- **remaining theorem:** construct or bound `R_quot` in compressed form and
  combine its degree with the generic-chart image inside the official slot.
- **nonclaims:** no degree bound, coverage theorem, generic-chart aggregate,
  official-row algorithm, or XR close.
- **falsifier:** one fixed support whose noncontained slope set differs from
  the roots of `g_S`, a basis change altering `g_S`, or an lcm whose roots are
  not the union of the fixed-support roots.
