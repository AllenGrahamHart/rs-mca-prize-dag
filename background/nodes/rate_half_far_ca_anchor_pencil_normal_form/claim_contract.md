# Claim contract

- **Claim:** exact anchor-pencil parametrization of every non-anchor far-CA
  bad slope, including off-anchor discrepancy and anchor-cancellation budgets.
- **Quantifiers:** every linear code, every integer radius, every column-far
  received pair with at least one bad slope; uniqueness when `2r<d_min`.
- **Dependencies:** linearity, column farness, and minimum distance for the
  uniqueness clause.
- **Consumer:** the CA-only transition isolated in `rate_half_band_closure`.
- **Nonclaims:** no upper bound on the number of admitted `lambda` values and
  no official adjacent certificate.
- **Falsifier:** a non-anchor bad slope without an `(AP1)` presentation, or a
  presentation violating `(AP2)` or `(AP3)`.
