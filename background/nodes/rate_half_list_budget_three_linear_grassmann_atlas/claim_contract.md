# Claim contract

- **claim:** exactly nine B*=3 edge-degree chambers are Plucker lines; their
  block locators satisfy the base-field split-unit relations and exact
  exceptional-degree ledger `(LGA1)--(LGA5)`.
- **scope:** canonical chambers in odd characteristic with `d>=3`; all
  official B*=3 rows are included.
- **requirements:** `rate_half_list_budget_three_split_fiber_atlas` and
  `rate_half_list_budget_three_k4_grassmann_line`.
- **consumer effect:** partitions the thirteen-chamber edge frontier into
  nine linear split-unit chambers and four genuinely quadratic
  Plucker-conic chambers; no status promotion.
- **nonclaim:** no split-unit or Plucker-conic exclusion, predecessor safety,
  or adjacent crossing is proved.
- **falsifier:** a tabled linear chamber whose edge bivector is not a
  Grassmann line, whose relation support differs from the table, whose
  relation has a proper vanishing subsum, or whose exceptional degree is
  wrong.
