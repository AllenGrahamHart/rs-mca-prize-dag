# Claim contract

- **claim:** every official five-row rank-two relation that is itself a full
  minimal Maxwell core has quantized negative baseline, the exact arithmetic
  rank floor `(F5N3)`, and defect window `(F5N4)`.
- **scope:** uniform P-A branch, rank-two relation with exactly five active
  rows equal to the complete minimal-core block family.
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`.
- **effect:** excludes full-core five-row rank-two relations below affine
  ranks `9,9,6,9544371773,9544371773,4772185889`.
- **nonclaims:** no proper-circuit exclusion, surviving-record count, other
  arity, higher trade rank, nonuniform-cell result, or aggregate payment.
- **falsifier:** an in-scope full core with nonnegative baseline, wrong
  baseline residue, rank below the printed floor, or defect charge outside
  `(F5N4)`.
