# Audit

## Verdict

The floor is an exact necessary consequence of the full-core five-row scope.
It is not an existence theorem or a count of surviving records.

## Load-bearing checks

- The active five rows must equal the entire minimal Maxwell core. A proper
  five-block circuit inside a larger core need not have `Delta<=0`.
- Official odd reserve plus the zero-baseline router is what makes `b>0`.
- The congruence is modulo six, not merely a parity condition.
- The degree capacity is `Z<=5(D-1)`; replacing it by `Z<=5D` loses the
  printed floor.
- The rank bound separately uses disjointness `Z<=a+D`.
- The surplus range `e<=h-1` makes the defect budget a two-sided window.

## Remaining frontier

Proper five-block circuits, full-core arities at least six, surviving
high-rank five-row records, trade rank at least three, nonuniform cells, and
cross-core aggregation remain open. No compute request is promoted.
