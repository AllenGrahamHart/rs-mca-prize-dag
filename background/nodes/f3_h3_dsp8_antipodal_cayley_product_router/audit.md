# Audit

- `C` is undefined at `1` and vanishes at `-1`. These are not discarded
  accidents: they encode exactly the two ordered antipodal representations.
- The normalization is by the canonical antipodal root `a`, not by the cubic
  scale `q` from the unit-product trace normal form.
- `M_a` counts every ordered nonantipodal representation. The small-generic
  and signed-disjoint predicates enter only when defining `V_a,E_a`.
- `E_a` is ordered. This absorbs the factor two between an unordered disjoint
  edge and a decorated ordered primitive-SP record.
- Choosing both `a` and `-a` would double count every target. The canonical
  sign set `A_H` contains exactly one of them.
- The quotient weight `L_a` remains correlated with the multiplicative fiber.
  Dropping it changes the consumer and cannot close DSP8.
