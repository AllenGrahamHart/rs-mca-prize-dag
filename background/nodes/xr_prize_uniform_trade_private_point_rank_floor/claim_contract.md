# Claim contract

- **claim:** every prize-row uniform Maxwell trade, independent of trade
  rank, requires a printed minimum affine kernel rank
- **scope:** the uniform `u=v=0` split-pencil branch at prize rates
  `1/4,1/8,1/16`
- **output:** affine floors `11243370,9629972,2241633`, paying the same
  selector ranks in the uniform branch
- **currency:** points private to the active trade-block subfamily versus the
  bounded number that the remaining host blocks can absorb
- **falsifier:** a scoped nonzero trade below `(UF4)`, failure of `(UF2)` or
  `(UF3)`, or an over-budget uniform selector below `(UF5)`
- **nonclaim:** no high-rank uniform payment, nonuniform payment, or aggregate
  slope count
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
