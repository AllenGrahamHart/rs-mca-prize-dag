# Claim contract

- **claim:** five-block dual planes annihilate both received directions, while
  four-block planes are either likewise orthogonal or perfectly paired with
  them through the Mobius hyperplane
- **scope:** arbitrary-`W` rank-two dual-plane packets with certified selected
  blocks
- **output:** `(IR1)--(IR6)` and the trade-row parity converses
- **currency:** received-pair pairing against the rank-three/four Segre column
  spans
- **nonclaim:** no full block realization, no plane count, no cross-core
  aggregate, and no P-A close
- **falsifier:** a certified five-block packet with nonzero interaction, or a
  four-block packet whose interaction is outside its Mobius annihilator line
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
