# Claim contract

- **claim:** every dyadic quotient Galois block has one canonical
  `(j,sign,w)` identifier, and its monic orbit polynomial is given by the
  printed cyclotomic resultant and reciprocal normalization.
- **scope:** every dyadic order `n=2^s`.
- **dependency:** the proved quotient-orbit decomposition, including
  characteristic-zero quotient Sidonicity.
- **falsifier:** a quotient orbit with zero or multiple listed
  representatives, a wrong norm exponent in `(QRM4)`, or a listed resultant
  whose roots differ from the corresponding quotient orbit.
- **nonclaims:** distinct blocks need not have equal candidate-prime support;
  no official-scale resultant, scalar annihilator, factorization, valuation,
  moment, complexity estimate, or C36' close is supplied.
