# Audit

- Used the `X^(D_0-1)` coefficient, whose right side is one because `P_X`
  is monic of exact degree `D_0-1`.
- Included both contributions from `QA_1` and the contribution from
  `P_clEB_1`; omitting the next coefficient of either biform breaks the
  Bezout identity.
- Derived coprimality with the complete form `E q_bar`, strengthening but
  not assuming the preceding `gcd(q_bar,Delta_inf)=1` gate.
- Called `Delta_inf` a specified modular inverse, not a unique polynomial
  representative: uniqueness would require an additional degree bound.
- No profile exclusion is claimed.
