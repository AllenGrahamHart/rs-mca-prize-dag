# Claim contract

- **claim:** deterministic first-core peeling assigns every nonterminal block
  in any prize P-A flat-nullity cell to exactly one pointed trade certificate,
  and exceptional plus terminal slopes total less than `n`
- **scope:** all normalized prize P-A flat-nullity cells
- **output:** the peeling algorithm, terminal bounds `(PO1)--(PO2)`, and the
  complete pointed certificate schema
- **currency:** minimal dense cores, canonical left-kernel trades, and strict
  terminal density failure
- **nonclaim:** no count of nonterminal owner packets and no cross-cell
  aggregate payment
- **falsifier:** an in-scope current family satisfying the density condition
  but admitting no trade, a block receiving two certificates, or an official
  terminal bound violating `(PO2)`
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
