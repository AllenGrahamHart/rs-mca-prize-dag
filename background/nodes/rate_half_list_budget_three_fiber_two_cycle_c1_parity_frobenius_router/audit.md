# Audit

1. Only odd `k` are screened; even `k` already put every `4L`th root in
   `F_p`.
2. The equality tested is source-trace Frobenius invariance, not the Mobius
   implication itself.
3. `R0` is exactly invariant under `r -> -r` and is not claimed to descend.
4. The `R1,R2` exceptional trace is `-8`; the `P1,P2` trace is `6/5`.
5. Both traces require 40 updates because their source elements lie in
   `mu_(2^40)`.
6. The campaign covers candidate moduli, not only primes. Multiples of five
   are safely skipped as composites before inversion.
7. No absence-of-hit claim is made beyond the exact printed interval and
   parity shard.
