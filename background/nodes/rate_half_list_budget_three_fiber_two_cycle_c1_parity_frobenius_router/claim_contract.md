# Claim contract

- **claim:** every accepted nonharmonic `R1,R2,P0,P1,P2` source lift descends
  to `F_p`; `R0` is the only branch on which `r^p=-r` remains compatible with
  the unordered trace.
- **scope:** the official generic two-antipodal `c=1` parity chamber.
- **dependencies:** the six exact Mobius traces and the nonharmonic scalar
  compiler's positive-quadratic field line.
- **falsifier:** an accepted anti-invariant lift on one of the five named
  branches, an omitted zero factor in the Frobenius comparison, or an
  official characteristic supporting either fixed exceptional trace.
- **nonclaims:** `R0`, invariant source lifts, and all official-degree
  polynomial gates remain unresolved.
