# sov_forced_root_recursion_algebra

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

For every `h` over odd characteristic, the monic degree-`h` square-root
recursion uniquely recovers the degree-`h` polynomial `S` forced by the top
`h+1` coefficients of a monic degree-`2h` locator `L`, and the obstruction
vector is exactly the list of coefficients of `S^2 - L` in degrees
`1, ..., h-1`.

In the X79 shifted-constant trade model the recursion returns the midpoint
and all obstructions vanish.

## Falsifier

A degree-`2h` locator whose top coefficients do not determine the forced root
by the printed recursion, or a shifted-constant X79 trade with a nonzero
obstruction.
