# Result

The three possible nodal trace fibers are reduced to at most nine explicit
affine coset-pair branches. Their subgroup points satisfy a uniform
`<12n^(2/3)+1` cap, and the pair target is the four-factor rational function
`(NTP4)`. Marginal Stepanov bounds already give the correct `O(n^2)` exponent
for their quotient-weighted raw records, but the leading weighted constant
`29376` is more than thirty times the complete DSP8 allowance `892`.

The next theorem must exploit the retained joint structure; additional
marginal point-count computation cannot close this branch.
