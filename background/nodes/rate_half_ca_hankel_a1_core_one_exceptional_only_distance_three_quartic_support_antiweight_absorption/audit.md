# Audit

1. The theorem does not refute the abstract antiweight construction. The
   new input is the exact internal-slice value of each actual `P_l`.
2. The symbol `r_k^(0)` in the proof is the lambda-cube polynomial, not the
   comparison function `r_l=P_l/D_l^2`.
3. Three proportional quartics are impossible because a squared Mobius
   ratio has at most two preimages. Distinctness and nonvanishing of all
   internal slopes and lambda-cube factors are inherited explicitly.
4. Birational generator tuples may identify normalization points. The proof
   charges 18 ordered points in the degree-four ambient argument and four
   unordered lifted pairs in the degree-two common-field argument.
5. Degree three is cited only after producing at least `e-1` actual fibers,
   stronger than the dependency's `e-85` threshold.
6. The final alternatives are necessary conditions, not constructions or
   closure of the degree-four branch.
