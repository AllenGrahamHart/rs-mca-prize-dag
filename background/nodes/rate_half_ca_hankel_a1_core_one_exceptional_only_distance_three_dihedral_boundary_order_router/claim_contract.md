# Claim contract

- **Claim:** every antipodal or constant-product rank-two packet forces
  `gcd(e,p-1)` to be `e/3` or `e`. The apparent reciprocal alternative has
  orbit normal form `c=sx_0`, `T={u,t,c/t}`, `u^2=c`, but the triple
  boundary-power equality excludes it.
- **Scope:** the two dihedral branches on the official distance-three row.
- **Dependencies:** Mobius subgroup reduction and matching-free boundary
  power router.
- **Consumer:** `rate_half_band_closure` and the rank-two CR-003 classifier.
- **Falsifier:** a retained dihedral packet with smaller `gcd(e,p-1)`, or a
  reciprocal packet satisfying all three boundary-power equalities.
- **Nonclaim:** neither high-order field stratum is excluded.
