# Claim contract

- **claim:** every official full-support rank-two minimal Maxwell core has an
  explicit affine-rank floor
- **scope:** uniform residual shells `d>=2`, with every active row equal to a
  block of the same minimal Maxwell core
- **input currency:** shell zero-fiber mass, full-core Maxwell sign, official
  arity caps, and the zero-baseline router
- **output:** per-arity formulas `(GF2)--(GF4)` and official all-arity floors
  `8,8,5,8590051854,8590020865,4294977670`
- **nonclaim:** the arithmetic minimizers are not asserted to be realizable
- **excluded:** proper circuits inside larger cores, higher trade rank,
  nonuniform cells, and cross-core slope aggregation
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
- **falsifier:** one scoped full-core rank-two realization below the printed
  floor, or an official arity omitted from the finite minimization
