# Claim contract

- **claim:** every B*=3 split-pencil witness lies in one of thirteen exact
  edge-degree chambers, with a genuine quadratic in the pendant type, and
  contains exactly the deficit-free constant split pencils printed in the
  atlas;
- **scope:** the complete six incidence types over any field and distinct
  evaluation set, with the vote cap specialized to `|D|=4d`;
- **requirement:** `rate_half_list_budget_three_split_pencil_normal_form`;
- **consumer:** the B*=3 branch of `rate_half_list_adjacent_crossing`;
- **new attack order:** treat the cycle separately, and for every other type
  classify the coupling of its one-member-short constant split pencils;
- **nonclaim:** no degree chamber or incidence type is excluded except the
  impossible pendant subcase with two constant deficit quotients; no official
  adjacent crossing is promoted;
- **falsifier:** a valid normal-form witness outside the thirteen chambers, a
  pendant witness with both deficit quotients constant, a missing or spurious
  deficit-free triangle in the atlas, or more than `floor(4d/h)` pairwise
  disjoint degree-`h` split members in one gcd-trivial constant pencil.
