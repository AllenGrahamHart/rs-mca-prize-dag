# Result

The nonzero weld is now routed into exact fiber factorizations. Every bad
domain trace factors as `K_x=-H_xJ_x` with a quotient degree paid by its row
deficit; the exceptional slope has a saturated gcd of degree at least
`e+3b` and a complementary quotient of degree at most `c+1`.

If all defect traces vanish identically, the weld loses the full split factor
`B_XE_Z` and descends to `W_1B_1-1=QK_1` in a smaller degree box. Both
branches remain open. No Modal computation was used.
