# Claim contract

## Outputs

1. The field-generic fixed-multipartial source bound `(MP1)--(MP2)`.
2. The independently replayed deployed floor `(MP3)`.
3. The exact same-remainder, different-prefix `F_17` obstruction `(MP4)`.
4. The arbitrary-word pair `(L_S,H)` and attained-target sum `(MP5)`.

## Consumer rule

For arbitrary received words, do not replace the fixed-syndrome sum over
attained prefixes by one maximum locator-prefix fiber. A closing theorem must
coalesce or pay attained targets and preserve first-match ownership. Route
fixed-template polynomial-fold sources through their quotient owner; do not
infer that every large frame has one fixed template.

## Nonclaims

No L1 upper estimate, carrier owner, attained-image bound, non-support-
preserving impossibility, deployed row closure, or prize endpoint is proved.

## Falsifier

A failure of the source degree bound or exactness, a mismatch in `(MP3)`,
equality of either locator-prefix pair in `(MP4)`, or a common translation
that changes `(L_S,H)`.
