# Claim contract

- **Claim:** recursive nongeneric mismatch descent preserves `A-K` and has
  the printed finite depth cap.
- **Input:** the transition supplied by the proved full-zero and
  alternative-joint-support theorems.
- **Potential:** ambient length, decreasing by at least `h+1` per transition.
- **Official output:** depth caps `169,169,255,254,254,510`.
- **Consumer:** the XR tangent/support-mismatch bridge and future compute
  sharding.
- **Nonclaim:** no branch-width, slope-fiber, or per-level aggregate bound.
- **Falsifier:** a live transition changing `A-K`, or a chain exceeding the
  integer cap.
