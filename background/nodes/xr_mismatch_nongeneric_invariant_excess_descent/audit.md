# Audit

The depth statement is not inferred from experiments. `verify.py` exhausts
the complete abstract transition system for every live `(N,K,h)` with
`N<=30`, allowing every `d` and every next length permitted by the theorem.
It also evaluates all six official integer caps directly.

The theorem intentionally leaves branching open. A bounded depth does not
authorize a large search unless a width or aggregate compiler is also proved.
