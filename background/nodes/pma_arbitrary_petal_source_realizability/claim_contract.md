# Claim contract - PMA arbitrary-petal maximal-source realizability

## Inputs

1. An evaluation domain partitioned into a `(k-1)`-point core, `M` arbitrary
   disjoint `ell`-point petals, and a background of size less than `ell`.
2. `M` distinct nonzero field labels.

## Output

An explicit received word and `M` degree-`<k` codewords with exact agreement
sets `C union T_i`; at the packing value of `M`, this is a maximal sunflower
source.

## Consumer Rule

Do not infer a common constant-shift locator pencil from source maximality.
Any use of `pma_coset_subtwoell_saturation_exclusion` on a below-band residual
must cite a contributor-dependent source/rechart theorem.

## Nonclaims

- no construction of an unpaid `M=4,t=3` residual contributor;
- no counterexample to the reserve-conditioned PMA or `imgfib` theorem;
- no claim that arbitrary-locator pencils are numerous;
- no obstruction to a bounded-to-one rechart into common pencils.

## Falsifier

An error in the exact agreement-set identity or a proof that every printed
arbitrary partition nevertheless has common-shift locator polynomials.
