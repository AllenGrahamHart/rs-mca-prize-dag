# Claim contract

- **claim:** the coefficient matrix of every quadratic B*=3 balanced scroll
  has determinant `(QFR1)` and is invertible; hence every quadratic chamber
  has the standard full-rank module `(QFR4)`.
- **scope:** all three pendant and the quadratic `K_4-e` chamber in odd
  characteristic; all official rows included.
- **requirement:** `rate_half_list_budget_three_quadratic_scroll_atlas`.
- **consumer effect:** removes the rank-deficient quadratic split-unit
  subbranch; no status promotion.
- **nonclaim:** the full-rank module is not excluded and no predecessor safety
  or adjacent crossing is proved.
- **falsifier:** a quadratic chamber violating `(QFR1)`, a pendant chamber
  with zero determinant, or a quadratic `K_4-e` chamber with zero determinant.
