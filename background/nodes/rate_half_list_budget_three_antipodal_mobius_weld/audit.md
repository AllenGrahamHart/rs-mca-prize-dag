degree at most `r-1`, so the four polynomials lie on one affine chart of their
projective pencil.

The Möbius conclusion needs both relations in `(AMW1)`. One relation only
places the four `G_i` in a pencil and does not weld its parameters to the
deleted-root parameters `a_i`.

The exact `d=8` positives from the parent audit satisfy the determinant in
`(1)` and therefore lie on Möbius graphs. This checks posedness of the weld
but does not imply an official-scale solution of `(AMW5)`.
