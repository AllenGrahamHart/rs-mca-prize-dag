# Claim contract

- **claim:** terminal genuine-tangent mismatch slopes are owned globally by
  distinct nonroot common-support codeword pairs and cost at most
  `(n-A)(E-1)`; the existing interleaving theorem bounds `E` by the ordinary
  worst list through `(GEO4)` and `(GEO5)`.
- **scope:** every deterministic canonical mismatch forest for one received
  pair at an official XR instance.
- **currency:** distinct support-wise bad slopes whose first terminal event is
  a genuine recovered-line tangent.
- **consumer effect:** remove recursive depth, repeated flags, and repeated
  appearances of one explanation from the tangent charge.
- **nonclaims:** no ordinary-list bound, no generic-chart slope aggregation,
  no payment for quotient or structured list profiles, and no bridge closure.
- **falsifier:** one lifted pair outside the original common-support pair
  list, more than `n-A` retained tangent slopes owned by one pair, or a
  canonical forest violating `(GEO2)` after deduplicating global pairs.
