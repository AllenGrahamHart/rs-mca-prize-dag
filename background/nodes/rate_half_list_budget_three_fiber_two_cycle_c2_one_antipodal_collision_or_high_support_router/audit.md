# Audit

1. The ordinary zero count excludes `+1,-1`; those two automatic zeros are
   restored in `|supp u|=N-2-Z`.
2. `deg D<=4H-7` alone would give the odd lower bound `4H-3`.  Negation
   parity sharpens the even zero count to `4H-8` and the support floor to
   `4H-2`.
3. `D=0` is excluded by the exact odd rational degree `2H-3`, not by a root
   count.
4. The triple/two-pair/fourfold collision exclusions use only barycentric
   moments and `alpha!=0`; they do not use minimum support.
5. The `L/Q` factorization and outer discriminant calculation likewise use
   only the one-pair normal form and completion coupling.
6. The conclusion does not extend the minimum-support-only facts
   `deg C=H-3`, the Euler/resultant gate, the support-divisor endpoint, or the
   infinity quartet.
7. The threshold is exhaustive as a dichotomy but neither side is declared
   empty or paid by the adjacent consumer.
