# Audit

1. The coefficient comparison requires `h>e`. Otherwise the constant shift
   `dP` enters the same top coefficients of `PQ`; the small `h=1,2` fixtures
   are not valid checks of `(BTP1)--(BTP2)`.
2. The factors `d^2` and the exact fractions in `m` are load-bearing. The
   formulas live in characteristics greater than `m`, inherited from the
   complement-third gate.
3. The scalar gates come from the full differential identity at `X=0`, not
   merely from matching its leading coefficients.
4. Root scaling changes `a`, `p_0`, and `d` with different weights. Formula
   `(BTP3)` is covariant because `m=3h+1`; normalization must transform the
   whole locator before applying `(BTP5)`.
5. The `e=2` scaling map has `gcd(h,m)=2`, so the one-ratio normalization is
   not asserted there.
6. A bounded exact census found no `e=1` pair at the first twenty compatible
   primes `p>=257` for `m=16,h=5`. This is heuristic route evidence only and
   is not used in the proof or status.
