# Audit

1. `R_D` uses unordered denominator-root pairs and has leading coefficient
   `e_4^3`; no division by a possibly zero symmetric coefficient occurs.
2. The six resolvent roots are `(A+B)^2/(AB)`, including coincidences.
3. `K_O` has exact degree three because `4I^3-J^2` is nonzero for a
   separable outer quartic. Harmonic and equianharmonic classes are retained.
4. The `c=1` norm is taken only over the two signs of `u^2=BD`; the sign of
   the remaining square root does not create another PGL class.
5. A zero quadratic norm is equivalent to one zero sign factor because all
   factors lie in the base field.
6. The packet is an exact algebraic rewrite, not an emptiness result or a
   reason to launch an official-order resultant computation.
