# Audit

The constant six counts unordered generic--generic edges, not oriented
support certificates. Each overlapping edge has exactly one positive and one
negative common signed-support coordinate, so its normalization is unique.

The antipodal allowance two is additional to the six generic edges. It is not
a degree bound on arbitrary distance-six graphs; it uses the norm-one
antipodal vector and the common product target.

The moment payment uses only the exact quotient mass
`sum_(t!=1)R(t)=(n-1)(n-2)`. It does not assume a pointwise quotient-fiber
bound. The conservative residual `(223/20)n^2` is smaller than the exact
residual `(DSO6)` and therefore is a genuine sufficient target.

The finite audit at orders `32` and `64` checks the complete support taxonomy
on every exact distance-six edge in every product fiber. It is an audit of the
algebraic classification, not the reason the cubic root bound is uniform.
