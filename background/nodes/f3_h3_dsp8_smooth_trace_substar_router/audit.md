# Audit

- The parameter `r=xy` is injective on a fixed product fiber because `t,r`
  determine `x+y` and hence the unordered quadratic root pair.
- The nodal equation uses the unnormalized trace. The normalizing cube has
  value `rs`, so the correct equation is `(1+r+s-t)^3=27rs`; omitting `rs`
  would be false.
- The cubic has leading coefficient one in `s` in every characteristic. All
  official characteristics exceed three, as required by the trace-curve
  smoothness theorem.
- Degree three is sharp: the exact `F_97`, order-32 and `F_193`, order-64
  controls both contain vertices with three nodal neighbors. No degree-two
  claim is made.
- The smooth substar is at the same center as the pre-existing Pareto star;
  no cross-center bridge or additional multiplicity is silently introduced.
- A smooth discriminant is an avoided element, not a vanishing ideal
  generator. It is used only for saturation.
