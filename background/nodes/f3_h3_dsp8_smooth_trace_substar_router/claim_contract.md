  most three; the Pareto product stars therefore retain the smooth substars
  in `(SSR2)`, including degrees five and three at `(35,2)`.

  the proved joint-star/depth compiler.

  criterion.
- **consumer effect:** strengthen the CR-001 candidate packet and permit
  saturation by explicit trace discriminants.
- **nonclaims:** no DSP8 moment estimate, no survivor count, no candidate
  prime exclusion, and no status change for the critical leaf.
- **falsifier:** a fixed target and vertex with four distinct nodal neighbors,
  or a `(35,2)` Pareto star with fewer than five/three smooth same-center
  leaves in its respective class.
