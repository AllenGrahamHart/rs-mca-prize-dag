# Result

The rank-five `GRS_4` split-pencil mechanism now has an arbitrary-rank
uniform-cell form. Every over-budget `u=v=0` selector contains a bounded
Maxwell core with an anomalous dual-product-code trade. Rank one is absent;
rank two is confined to at most `2(s-1)` active coordinates and a polynomial
pencil of degree at most `s-2`.

This reaches the first open prize selector ranks without assuming they are
small. The remaining high-core work is now split cleanly between the
nonuniform flat-nullity cells and the actual payment/classification of
primitive trades, especially trade rank at least three.
