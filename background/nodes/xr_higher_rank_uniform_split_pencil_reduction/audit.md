# Audit

The theorem applies only to the uniform flat-nullity cell `u=v=0`. It does
not silently discard the many nonuniform cells left by the exact rank-five
ledger.

The extra right-kernel direction on a Maxwell core is justified by the
generic tangent-strip exclusion. Without that hypothesis, the extension
direction can restrict to a `GRS_a` word on a block and `(HR4)` need not
follow from dimension counting.

The rank-two support bound uses the actual post-strip pair cap `a`; a raw
dual-product-code trade without that cap need not be supported on `2a`
coordinates.

For `t>4`, the polynomial-pencil fiber labels need not be projectively equal
to the slopes. The equality is asserted only at `t=4`, where the coefficient
space is the full dual `RS_2` code.

The Maxwell core and trade are obstruction certificates. Their existence
does not count selected slopes and therefore does not promote P-A.
