# Claim contract

- **claim:** the first three product-derivative resultants of each quotient
  orbit block are not all zero, and the exact cutoff-35 scalar annihilator
  divides their positive odd gcd.
- **scope:** every dyadic order; the C36 candidate interpretation is for odd
  official split primes.
- **dependencies:** the canonical irreducible orbit polynomials and the exact
  quotient-algebra scalar-support compiler, including characteristic-zero
  shifted-product multiplicity at most two.
- **falsifier:** one block for which all three characteristic-zero resultants
  vanish, or one odd prime dividing `s_O,35` but not `g_O`.
- **nonclaims:** `p|g_O` need not imply `p|s_O,35`; no official-scale
  resultant, factorization, cost, false-positive, moment, or C36' bound is
  supplied.
