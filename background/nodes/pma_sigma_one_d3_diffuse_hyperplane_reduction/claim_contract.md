# Claim contract - PMA sigma-one diffuse hyperplane reduction

## Inputs

1. Exact `sigma=1`, `(d,r)=(3,0)` source objects after the full-petal payment.
2. Five canonical singleton agreements in distinct petals.
3. Distinct nonzero planted petal scalars.

## Output

Every object has a nonzero divided-difference hyperplane certificate, every
fixed certificate admits fewer than `(k-1)^2/2` split cubic core locators, and
the aggregate class is below `16(k-1)^2 binom((n-k)/2,5)`.

## Nonclaims

- no `n^6` payment;
- no average or generic-position hypothesis;
- no use of the small-field census as proof;
- no disposition of `d>=4` or the asymptotic residual.
