# Audit

The computation scans every modulus in the exact congruence interval, not
only numbers accepted by a probabilistic primality test. A zero-hit result is
therefore stronger than the required prime-characteristic assertion.

The trace index range is complete. An admissible lift ratio has order
`2^s`, `3<=s<=40`, and its trace first reaches zero at index `s-2`, which lies
in `1,...,38`.

The first harmonic equation is excluded algebraically by incompatible orders;
the second is the already-forbidden `r^4=1` case. Only the third equation uses
the arithmetic packet.

This theorem is maximal-row-specific. The printed characteristic interval
cannot be reused for smaller smooth domains or other list budgets.
