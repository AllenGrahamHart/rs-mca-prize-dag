1. Band membership gives `s>=1`.
2. Vector dimension is `s+1`; projective dimension is `s`.
3. The first-coordinate map is injective under the shifted degree cap.
4. Complete agreement sets, not arbitrary support witnesses, are used.
5. Distinct codewords agree together on at most `k-1` points.
6. Complement intersections are at most `s-1`.
7. Packing uses `s`-subsets of complements.
8. The exact floor in `(CP2)` is retained.
9. Fixed `s` is polynomial and sublinear `s` is subexponential at linear
   complement density.
10. Reserve absorption retains `s=o(R log |B|)`.
11. Linear `s` is not declared paid.
12. Deep levels are not charged again.
