## Primary route

Exploit the source coupling that arbitrary RS lists lack: on every petal the
target is a scalar multiple of the same locator `L_D`. The background quotient
in `pma_source_paving_bridge` reduces the list to degree-`<kappa`
interpolation against a correlated target and realizes every object in the
weighted-RS chart of upstream PR `#764`. Its pinned-basis bound is exact but
can be exponential, so the next theorem must use the source coupling to bound
the non-quotient `GROW union RES` cell rather than merely reapply the ambient
basis count.

The exact-periodic part of `QOWN` is proved and runs globally before chart
selection. `pma_quotient_closure_scope` settles the next routing decision: the
precise folded-source theorem is already inside `QOWN`, while low-defect error
supports have only a structural boundary-stripping reduction. Its raw
word-free first-scale route exceeds the official profile allowance. Therefore
`QCLOSE` is empty and the direct `GROW union RES` theorem must retain every
low-defect survivor. A future source-coupled boundary theorem may improve this
route, but is not a premise of the current attack.

The finite dyadic growing-defect near-coset owner now runs between `QOWN_per`
and B11. Writing `n=2^j` and `s_n=floor(n/(32j))`, it uses the full source
agreement set to charge every codeword missing at most `s_n` points of any
dyadic coset of size at least `k-s_n`. Its global count
is below `Q_2(k+2)`, and the combined periodic/near-coset class uses less than
`8` of the available `719` first-scale units. This is a separate owner, not a
revival of `QCLOSE`.

The scale-two odd-lift boundary owner runs next. The parity decomposition of

killed the raw one-boundary `QCLOSE` count. Exact levels `k+1` and `k+3`

The common finite ledger is now proved by

The source-layout multiplier is now canonical rather than schematic.

Do not return to a weighted paired-core census or multiply a per-layout bound
by the number of paired cores.

## Descent route

The auxiliary degree is `d`, below the original `k`. A recursive attack is
valid only after proving a strict progress measure and preserving:

- the generated field and smooth-domain dictionary;
- the agreement/reserve inequality;
- canonical strip ownership;
- the exact exponent allowance.

Parameter decrease alone is not a proof of termination.

## Route cuts

- Ordinary Johnson bounds cannot enter the wide regime by definition.
- A theorem for arbitrary received words is likely false or too weak; the
  common-locator source relation is load-bearing.
- The ambient paving-basis numerator from PR `#764` is a valid charge, not by
  itself a polynomial payment.
- Applying PR `#771` through the current one-added-locator bridge gives the
  per-core cap `binom(L+1,kappa+3)`. Its exact ratio to the unfloored pinned
  paving cap `binom(L,kappa)/(kappa+1)` is
  `(L+1)(L-kappa)(L-kappa-1)/((kappa+2)(kappa+3))>1` in every official
  low-defect cell. It is therefore both weaker locally and still subject to
  the paired-core sum; only a different genuinely global LineRay bridge could
  alter this conclusion.
- A paired-core weighted census is no longer needed for the finite target:
  universal first-layout domination has removed that multiplier. Any attack
  that still sums the paid source bounds over cores is solving a stronger,
  unnecessary problem.
- Boundary stripping plus a raw quotient candidate count is not a finite
  low-defect payment; the `d=2,beta=1` line already loses more than `719`.
- At `sigma=1`, the B11 threshold partition closes exactly but its ambient
  `q^4` cofactor certificate loses the `n^6` primitive line at the two lower
  rates even for `q=n+1`.
- `petal_fixed_excess` is full-petal only and cannot pay mixed petals.
- `petal_growth` is layout-anchored top-band only and cannot pay below-floor
  full petals; no off-band coefficient theorem is currently wired.
- The top-band `(121/128)n^6` certificate does not create a
  `(7/128)n^6` post-top-band allowance. The only valid subtraction uses the
  proved carried-layout partition and exact `N_top` census.
- The defect-three full-petal certificate does not extend to the diffuse
  five-singleton class; that class has no two-point interpolation block.
- The complete diffuse auxiliary/source class is not `O(n^6)` per sunflower:
  the reciprocal-quadratic multiplicative-core family is a proved actual-source
  `Omega(n^7)` obstruction. Any replacement must contain a named prior owner.
- Fixed-parameter enumerations do not prove uniform amplification.
- A polynomial bound without the composed exponent ledger does not close the
  consumer.
