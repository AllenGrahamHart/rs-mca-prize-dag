# Audit - L1 coarse p-free Wronskian distance packing

1. Frobenius recovers the omitted power sums, not the elementary checkpoints.
2. Common roots are removed before the Wronskian; the two tail locators are
   coprime and squarefree.
3. The Laurent cancellation is through `Z^(-d-1)`, leaving
   `O(Z^(-d-2))` and degree `2t-d-2`.
4. Zero derivative is not treated as constant in characteristic `p`; the
   proof uses `F(Z^p)` and excludes it by simple zero/pole valuations.
5. The `F_4` fixture attains the half-depth boundary and rejects a full-depth
   distance claim for coarse fibers.
6. The packing cap is exact but may be exponentially above the finite prize
   numerator. No row-sharp claim is hidden in the word "packing."
7. No computation or probabilistic evidence is load-bearing.
8. A lower bound on the numerical packing ceiling is not called a lower
   bound on any actual fiber.
9. Exact mixed locator-prefix collisions already have the stronger tail
   distance `d+1`; the half-depth result is used only for the coarse p-free
   superset.
