# Lineage

The entropy-reserve theorem showed that coarse p-free flatness had enough
finite average budget but supplied no maximum. The present theorem takes the
first target-uniform step: equality of p-free moments makes the logarithmic
derivatives agree deeply, and squarefreeness converts that cancellation into
an exact symmetric-difference distance and packing cap.

The follow-on comparison keeps this coarse route separate from the exact
mixed/Pade lane, whose codeword-distance tail bound is already stronger.
