# Audit

- The arity proof is repeated without an MDS support assumption.
- Rank-one circuits are retained and have arity three; they are not silently
  discarded as in the uniform specialization.
- The four/five-block conclusion is coefficient-level. No dual-GRS support
  factorization is imported for `u>0`.
- Four-block row classes are distinct because the Mobius map is nonconstant,
  not because of the uniform block intersection cap.
- The theorem decomposes a fixed trade but does not deduplicate circuits from
  different trades or Maxwell cores.
- No Modal or large local computation is used.
