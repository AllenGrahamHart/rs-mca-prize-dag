# Claim contract

- **claim:** arbitrary-`W` rank-two trades decompose into rank-one three-block
  circuits and genuine rank-two four/five-block Segre circuits
- **scope:** every rank-two trade produced by a normalized prize P-A
  flat-nullity core
- **output:** `(AW1)--(AW2)`, the rank-one routing, and the complete
  coefficient-level four/five-block atlas
- **currency:** row-scaling kernel dimension and the Segre quadric
- **nonclaim:** no arbitrary-`W` support embedding count, no cross-core owner,
  and no aggregate P-A payment
- **falsifier:** an in-scope support-minimal rank-two scaling trade outside
  arity four/five, or a rank-two four/five circuit violating the Segre atlas
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
