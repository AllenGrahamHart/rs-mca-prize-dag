# Claim contract

- **claim:** a saturated coefficient ideal of the first twenty-five
  subresultants of two sparse degree-`n-1` polynomials has exactly the odd
  characteristic support of nonidentity product fibers with `P>=25`.
- **scope:** every dyadic `n>=4` and prime `p=1 mod n`.
- **dependencies:** the integral global product resultant and
  characteristic-zero shifted-product Sidonicity.
- **falsifier:** disagreement between product multiplicity and specialized
  gcd degree, failure of the subresultant cutoff, or a prime-support mismatch
  in `(PSC6)`.
- **certificate:** either the global scalar annihilator plus complete prime
  factorization, or a fixed-row saturated Bezout/unit-ideal certificate.
- **nonclaims:** no efficient subresultant implementation, coefficient bound,
  factorization, resource estimate, official PASS certificate, DSP8
  promotion, or C36' close is asserted.
