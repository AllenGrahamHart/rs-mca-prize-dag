# Claim contract - L1 intrinsic partial-core triple-polarity closure

## Inputs

- intrinsic complete-fiber quotient partitions;
- arbitrary `(k-1)`-point source cores and full-fiber petals;
- fixed layout, core-defect, and petal-support polarity caps;
- the L1 cutoff and whole-strip numerator bounds.

## Output

The complete fixed triple-polarity box is globally polynomial across source
charts and intrinsic dyadic scales, with explicit bound `(TP4)`.

## Consumer rule

Consumers may restrict the intrinsic quotient common-pencil residual to
unbounded layout, defect, or petal polarity. They must retain non-intrinsic
quotient maps and arbitrary petal locators.

## Falsifier

A core, defect, or support pattern missing from its orientation-plus-
exceptions encoding; two source members for one `(C,T,U)`; or a fixed
support/locator fiber exceeding the applicable CRT bound.
