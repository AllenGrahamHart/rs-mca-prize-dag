# Claim contract - L1 joint core/background Johnson bound

## Inputs

- one maximal-sunflower source chart and exact labelled petal support;
- exact saturated core-defect pairs and the L1 list threshold;
- the fixed-support cross-determinant root argument;
- the generated-field, petal-polarity, and lower-cutoff bounds.

## Output

Core-defect intersections and common background agreements share the single
pairwise budget `(CJ2)`. Positive joint denominator gives the
field-independent cell bound `(CJ4)` and the aggregate payment `(CJ5)`.

## Consumer rule

Consumers may impose `(CJ7)`. For `b>0` it supersedes, but does not invalidate,
the two separate nonpositive-Johnson conditions. Consumers must retain the
zero-denominator boundary.

## Falsifier

Two distinct compatible contributors violating `(CJ2)`, a
positive-denominator family violating `(CJ4)`, or failure of either exact
fixture.
