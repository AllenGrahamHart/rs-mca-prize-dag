# Result

Every uniform rank-two trade decomposes into four- and five-block circuits.
The four-block class is the Mobius/Plucker graph on the Segre quadric; the
five-block class is one circuit in projective three-space with every four
points independent.

The proper local rank-two frontier is therefore constant in block arity.
A downstream fundamental-circuit theorem supplies canonical ownership inside
each fixed trade. First-core selection, cross-core ownership, and counting of
realized support embeddings remain, not an unbounded rank-two template
generator.
