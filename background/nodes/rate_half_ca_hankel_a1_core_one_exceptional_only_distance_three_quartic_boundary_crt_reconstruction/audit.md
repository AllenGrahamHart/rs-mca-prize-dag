   division by a globally defined polynomial `z-delta(X)` before reduction.

   resulting five coordinates remains diagnostic only.

   `deg(AC)>6e+7`, which starts at `e=3`. It does not apply to the `e=1`
   sharpness fixture.
5. The `F_97,e=3` verifier packet deliberately lacks an external split
   design and is rejected at degree five. It proves nonvacuity of the gate,
   not existence or nonexistence of an official packet.

   reconstruction; they do not provide three extra free coordinates for
   `Omega`.
