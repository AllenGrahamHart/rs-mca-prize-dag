# Result

An explicit `F_17` fixture shows that the split-pencil census, exact
external design, affine Hankel kernel, first-order exceptional crossing, and
pinned adjugate are jointly consistent at `e=1`. The fixture is column-far
and its complete projective supported-slope set is known exactly.

The low-order escape is transparent: quotient distance is three but has four
supporting triples, whereas the official branch has a unique triple once
`r>=4`. Future exclusion arguments must use that official-scale uniqueness,
the lower reciprocal square, or another genuinely stronger input.
