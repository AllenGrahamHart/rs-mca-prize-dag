# Audit

- The number `(n-2)/2` counts target values, not signs: `a` and `-a` give
  the same target, and these are the only collisions because the field has
  odd characteristic.
- `S_A` is restricted by the same `P>=25` predicate as the distance-six
  moments. Enlarging it to all antipodal targets is used only for `(AQM1)`.
- In `(AQM2)`, the coefficient `38/5` is the incremental antipodal cost:
  `(17/10)*8-6=38/5`. Charging `(68/5)Q_n` discards this distinction and is
  the superseded payment.
- The adapter has `K_25^c=2D_c`, not `K_25^c=4D_c`; the other factor two
  belongs to `J_25^c=4K_25^c`.
- `(AQM3)` is a sufficient correlation target, not an identity for C36'. A
  violation of it does not by itself refute C36'.
- `(AQM4)` is deliberately target-independent and slightly stronger than
  `(AQM3)`. The exact target-sensitive right side should be retained by a
  contributor computation.
