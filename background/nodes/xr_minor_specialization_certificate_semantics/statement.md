# xr_minor_specialization_certificate_semantics

- **status:** PROVED
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

In the light-triangle eliminant normal form supplied by
`xr_triangle_eliminant_form`, a certificate consisting of:

- a maximal minor of the normal-form matrix; and
- one admissible specialization of the profile chart at which that minor
  evaluates nonzero

proves that the corresponding maximal minor is not the zero polynomial on the
profile chart. Consequently the profile eliminant is not identically zero on
that chart.

## Falsifier

A maximal-minor polynomial that is identically zero on the admissible profile
chart but evaluates nonzero at an admissible chart point.
