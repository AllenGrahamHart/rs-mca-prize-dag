- **claim:** an official generic `c=2` survivor cannot have a denominator
  whose four roots are a scaled fourth-root quartet.
- **scope:** the maximal-row normalized `c=2` denominator-mismatch chamber.
- **dependencies:** official characteristic control and the exact normalized
  primary gap.
- **falsifier:** vanishing of the displayed Pochhammer coefficient in an
  official characteristic, or a scaling that changes zero/nonzero status.

  to the pure geometry is asserted.
