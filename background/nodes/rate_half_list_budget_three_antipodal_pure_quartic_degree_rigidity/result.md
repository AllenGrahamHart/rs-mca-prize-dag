# Result

The centered pure-quartic antipodal stratum is now degree-rigid. Its
lower-leading pencil direction has the maximum possible degree `r-1`; at the
prize maximum this is `2^37-2`. Its second-derivative Wronskian is exactly
`Y^(d-2)U^2V^2` times one nonzero linear factor. Both directions are
squarefree, and at most one of their roots lies in `Z(D) union {0}`.

Future work on this special outer-parameter locus should impose that exact
degree and the linear differential residual from the outset. The generic and
intermediate centered-quartic strata remain open. No Modal computation was
used.
