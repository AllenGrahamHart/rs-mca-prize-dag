# Audit

1. `(QBR1)` is the exact polynomial Bezout identity, not only a congruence.
2. The canonical remainder has degree below `e`; no squarefreeness of
   `f_0=E q_bar` is used.
3. The sign in `s_k=d_k+a_minus r_k` follows from
   `rho_k=-r_k` and `l_0P_cl=1-f_0a_minus`.
4. The theorem removes per-stage inversion and exact-division work, but not
   the final full unit and inherited degree checks.
