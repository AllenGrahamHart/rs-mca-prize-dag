# Claim contract - L1 fixed-source anchored triple-polarity closure

## Inputs

- one fixed maximal source and its whole-petal anchor census;
- internal genuine common-pencil charts with degree-`ell` complete-fiber
  petals and arbitrary source cores;
- fixed layout, core-defect, and petal-support polarity caps;
- the strict-window and thin-edge numerator bounds;
- the L1 cutoff and generated-field bound.

## Output

The complete fixed triple-polarity box is polynomial across all whole-petal-
anchored non-intrinsic partitions, canonical source charts, and degree strips,
with explicit bound `(AT6)`.

## Consumer rule

Consumers may remove both source-chart supply and numerator/Forney
multiplicity in the printed anchored scope. They must retain growing triple
polarity, unanchored or smaller-fiber quotient maps, and arbitrary petal
locators.

## Falsifier

An anchored partition omitted by least-petal ownership; a core, defect, or
support missing from its orientation-plus-exceptions encoding; two source
members for one `(C,T,U)`; or a fixed support/locator numerator fiber larger
than the applicable CRT bound.
