# Audit

1. The outer polynomial is `product(W+w_i)`, whereas the derivative-weight
   polynomial is `Phi(T)=product(T-w_i)`; this accounts for the sign
   `s^3=-beta`.
2. The degree argument uses all `5H-11` split-divisor roots.  The deleted
   non-antipodal points are not mismatch zeros because every barycentric
   weight is nonzero.
3. The rational map has exact odd degree `r`; cancellation in one displayed
   numerator does not change that degree because `B` and `V` are coprime.
4. Equation `(BCR3)` alone is necessary, not sufficient, for a repeated
   barycentric weight over the official base field.  The pair parameter `s`
   and factorization `(BCR4)` must be retained.
5. Triple collisions are excluded by `alpha=4c!=0`, not by a speculative
   torsion-trace computation.  Two-pair and fourfold collisions are excluded
   separately from the barycentric identities.
6. Division in `(BCR5)` uses both `alpha!=0` and `3y+2!=0`; the latter follows
   from separability through the binary-quartic discriminant.
7. No dense `B`, `C`, `J`, or order-`2^40` subgroup object is constructed.
