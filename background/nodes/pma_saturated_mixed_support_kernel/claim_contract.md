# Claim contract - pma saturated mixed-support kernel

## Inputs

- the exact PMA core-defect normal form;
- an exact noncore support partition into background/petal label classes;
- distinct class labels and the proved per-class caps;
- the reserve rich-fiber lower bound for the final corollary.

## Output

An exact linear and weighted-Hankel kernel parametrization, the coarse
rich-class rank bound, maximal rank `min(d,s-d-1)` for actual saturated pairs,
the reduced/basepoint-free saturation rules, and the fixed-pattern split-
locator bound `binom(k-1,max(0,d-w))`.

## Promotion evidence

1. interpolation equivalence in both directions;
2. a dimension proof including the unique exceptional case;
3. exact-defect gcd and pairwise-fiber coprimality;
4. proof that common split factors are duplicate lower-defect
   representations;
5. dual Reed-Solomon moment criterion and rank-defect interpretation;
6. determinant/coprimality proof of maximal rank in both degree regimes;
7. canonical core-root injection for the fixed-pattern locator bound;
8. finite-field verifier with sharp and hostile controls;
9. evidence edges only, with no promotion of `petal_mixed_amplification`.

## Nonclaims

This theorem does not make the fixed-pattern binomial charge polynomial, sum
support patterns, provide a profile owner, or prove `imgfib`.
