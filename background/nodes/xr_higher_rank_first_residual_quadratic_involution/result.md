# Result

After the minimum-face quotient, the first possible rank-two union is
`a+3`. That whole shell is now one quadratic-pullback class: row zero sets
are disjoint pairs, with at most one singleton, and all pairs are cycles of
one Mobius involution over the base field.

The remaining first-shell problem is ownership and counting of these
quadratic charts, not further generic rank optimization. Higher support
shells, trade rank at least three, nonuniform cells, and the aggregate XR
slope count remain open. No computation is required for this reduction.
