# Audit

- The zero sets are taken inside the active-coordinate union. Their
  disjointness uses the nonzero evaluation functional on the rank-two row
  space; it is stronger than pairwise block intersection alone.
- The pair-cap calculation uses actual row supports, which are contained in
  the selected blocks. Equality between a row support and its block is not
  assumed.
- Coprime reduction of `F,G` is necessary before calling the projective map
  degree two. A common factor cannot vanish on `X`, so this reduction preserves
  every displayed fiber.
- Odd characteristic is load-bearing for the separable quadratic deck
  involution. It holds on the official even-order multiplicative domains.
- The involution pairs zero fibers, not necessarily every coordinate of `X`;
  coordinates outside the displayed `Z_i` may be nonzero in every active row.
- This result generalizes only the seven-coordinate profile at `a=4`. The
  eight-coordinate cubic possibility belongs to the next shell `a+4`.
