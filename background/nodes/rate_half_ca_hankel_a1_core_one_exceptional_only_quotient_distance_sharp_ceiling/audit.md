# Audit

The exact sum is over the `4e` ordinary supported slopes. The exceptional
slope has already been removed when
`sum_z a_z=2e(e-1)` is formed.

Minimum complement means `|G_z\R_A|=h`; it is stronger than merely having a
large intersection with `R_A`. Such a fiber is internal even when several
abstract minimal leaders exist, because the two source representations fit
inside the Vandermonde independence range.

The former ceiling is not rounded: `e` is odd, so `3(e+1)/2` is an integer.
The theorem removes exactly that integer and retains the next one with two
fully printed profiles.
