# Claim contract

- **claim:** every canonical path-plus-singleton B*=3 witness has the two
  exact coupled pencils `(PM1)--(PM5)`; the degree-`d` pencil has exactly three
  fully domain-split members for `d>=3`, and the degree-`d-1` pencil has
  exactly three for `d>=6`;
- **scope:** the path-plus-singleton incidence type over any field and
  distinct `4d`-point evaluation set;
- **requirement:** `rate_half_list_budget_three_split_fiber_atlas`;
- **consumer:** the B*=3 branch of `rate_half_list_adjacent_crossing`;
- **new attack object:** existence of a degree-`d-1` rational function with
  three complete split fibers and the coupled Mobius graph fiber `(PM5)`;
- **nonclaim:** the path type is not excluded, no count of all such rational
  functions is proved, and no adjacent crossing is promoted;
- **falsifier:** a canonical path witness violating either pencil relation,
  partition `(PM3)`, either Mobius fiber, injectivity, or the exact
  three-member conclusions at the stated `d` thresholds.
