# Claim contract - PMA constant-shift-pencil sub-two-ell saturation exclusion

## Inputs

1. A field and at least three distinct quotient labels.
2. Full-petal locators exactly of the form `P-a_i` for one common monic
   degree-`ell` polynomial `P`.
3. A numerator/denominator pair of degrees at most `d`, with denominator
   degree exactly `d` in the strict strip `ell<d<2ell`.
4. Exact-defect saturation `gcd(F,W)=1`.

## Output

The corresponding full-petal coset-chart stratum is empty.

## Consumer Rule

This output may be used as a PMA payment only after a proved source predicate
places the carried contributor in one common constant-shift locator pencil.
Without that bridge it is evidence and a classified subcase; arbitrary-petal
layouts stay inside `petal_mixed_amplification`.

## Nonclaims

- no theorem for `t=2`;
- no theorem at `d=2ell` or above;
- no partial-petal theorem;
- no theorem for locator families outside one constant-shift pencil;
- no row-sharp Q bound or complete BC chart decomposition;
- no change to the status of `petal_mixed_amplification` or `imgfib`.

## Falsifier

A field, `t>=3`, distinct `a_i`, and a coprime pair `(F,W)` satisfying the
printed strict degree and petal-divisibility conditions.
