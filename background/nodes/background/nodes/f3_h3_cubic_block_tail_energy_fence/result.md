# Result

An explicit cubic rich-block tail is not, by itself, the missing C36' theorem.
Even with tail constant four and the exact quotient mass, abstract profiles on
the official orders `2^22,...,2^41` can exceed the `145/4` energy endpoint.

Therefore extracting constants from Mit'kin's block-count tail without adding
source-specific coupling cannot close C36'. The selected analytic work should
return to the truncated product/quotient moment, oriented character phases, or
an energy argument retaining more than marginal block sizes.
