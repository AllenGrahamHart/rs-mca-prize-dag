# Audit

The determinant uses one fixed full-size minor. Its nonvanishing is sufficient
for full column rank; no converse is inferred from a rational prime dividing
its norm. In particular, the theorem says "except possibly characteristic
17" before invoking the separately verified `F_17`, `zeta=3` witness.

The support pattern is fixed in exponent coordinates. A different partition
of the order-sixteen subgroup is outside the claim, even if it has the same
abstract path-plus-singleton incidence type.

The official exclusion uses both the order-`2^41` requirement and the field
cap. It does not claim that characteristic `17` is unavailable at every
smaller smooth domain.

No lifting construction is asserted. Multiplying a composed small witness by
a common locator does not automatically add its full degree to every
agreement count when the original supports cover the base domain.
