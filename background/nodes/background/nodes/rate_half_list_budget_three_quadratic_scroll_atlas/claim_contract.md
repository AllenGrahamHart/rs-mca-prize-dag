# Claim contract

- **claim:** every one of the four quadratic B*=3 chambers has the balanced
  scroll normal form `(QSA1)--(QSA7)` and splits exactly into a nondegenerate
  constant-relation subbranch or the full-rank standard module
  `(alpha,Xalpha,beta,Xbeta)`.
- **scope:** the three pendant and one quadratic `K_4-e` chambers in odd
  characteristic with `d>=3`; all official rows included.
- **requirements:** `rate_half_list_budget_three_plucker_edge_gate` and
  `rate_half_list_budget_three_linear_grassmann_atlas`.
- **consumer effect:** completes the bounded edge-geometry classification of
  all thirteen chambers; no status promotion.
- **nonclaim:** no full-rank balanced scroll or split-unit equation is
  excluded, and no predecessor safety or adjacent crossing is proved.
- **falsifier:** a quadratic chamber for which no affine-linear row basis
  exists, `U_1 wedge V_1=0`, the rank dichotomy fails, or the exceptional
  degree is not four or six.
