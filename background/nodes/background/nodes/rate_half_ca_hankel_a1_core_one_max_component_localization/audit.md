# Audit

- The component ledger is formed for `Qbar` on `(D\S) x Z`; the fixed core
  row is not counted as a zero-capacity residual row.
- The load-bearing identity is `N-1=8e+7`, not the approximate ratio eight.
- `E` counts overlap of distinct component incidence sets. It is retained in
  both exact deficit sums and satisfies `E<=O<=1`.
- The unique component follows from nonnegative integers
  `z_i=r_i-2e_i` summing to one. No irreducibility or genericity heuristic is
  substituted for that arithmetic.
- The factor-five residual charge uses the column deficits `C_i`; it is not a
  slope-count or a moment bound.
- Rank amplification uses only exact full-generator separation rank and the
  elementary rank bound `sr(G)<=b+1`.
- Geometric squarefreeness is checked at a clean slope outside the leading-
  coefficient zero set of a hypothetical repeated component.
- Rank at least five excludes models, not the stratum.
