# Audit

`N_4(t)` and `N_6(t)` count all eligible low-distance edges in the product
fiber. They are not the edges of one selected seven-vector subgraph and not a
chosen multistar only.

The factor two on `N_4` is the centroid deficit weight. It is not an ordering
factor or quotient multiplicity.

The ratio `16/83` is optimized over the integer lower bound on the number of
small vectors. It is not asserted to be attained by an actual subgroup row.

The moment retains `R(t)` pointwise. Replacing it by an average or multiplying
the unweighted global edge count by an average quotient size would not prove
`(HLM3)`.

The conservative constant `2573/136` discards a favorable lower-order term.
The exact certificate target is `(HLM3)`.
