# Result

The product-energy route to C36' has a larger exact allowance than the
roadmap previously recorded. Product and quotient multiplicities have equal
`L2` norms, but they are forced far apart at the identity parameter:
`R(1)=n-1` while `P(1)<4n^(2/3)`. Keeping that compulsory distance rather
than applying bare Cauchy proves

```text
E_x((1-H)\{0}) <= (145/4)(n-1)^2  =>  C36'.
```

This permits approximately `36.25n^2`, rather than the old convenient
`35n^2` target. It does not settle the needed energy estimate, but it
reopens a constants-explicit shifted-energy proof with about `3.5%` more
headroom and explains why the marginal cutoff-18 profile fence is not a
fence against direct C36' energy payment.

No Modal resource is used and no critical status is promoted.
