# Result

The `B*=3` Johnson predecessor is now reduced to six explicit incidence types
and their 4-wise RS intersection matrices. A complete safe proof at
`3n/4-1` may check full column rank only on those six fixed-subgroup types,
not arbitrary agreement hypergraphs.

An exact order-eight witness realizes one type, proving that pairwise MDS and
Johnson incidence arithmetic alone cannot close the branch. No official
safe/unsafe pair is claimed.
