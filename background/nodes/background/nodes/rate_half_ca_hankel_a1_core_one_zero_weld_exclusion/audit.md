# Audit

- The exact identity `C_*=e` is imported from the proved component norm
  ledger after substituting `b=0,D_*=1`; it is not an upper-bound heuristic.
- At a root of `G_0`, the nonzero homogeneous specialization `Q_*(U,V;x)`
  has degree `e`. Divisibility into squarefree `F` gives `e` distinct clean
  roots, not merely `e` roots counted with multiplicity.
- At an omitted domain point, `(4)` excludes every clean supported root.
  There is exactly one other supported slope, so `v_x<=1` even if the
  exceptional specialization behaves degenerately.
- The three omitted factors are distinct because `G_0` and the full domain
  polynomial are squarefree.
- All omitted-row contributions are nonnegative by definition. Ignoring the
  other `8e+4` rows therefore preserves the lower bound on `C_*`.
- The contradiction is strict precisely for `e>=2`; at `e=1` the lower bound
  is below the exact capacity, which the verifier retains as a mutation fence.
- The theorem says only `K!=0`. No property of the nonzero-weld branch is
  inferred.
