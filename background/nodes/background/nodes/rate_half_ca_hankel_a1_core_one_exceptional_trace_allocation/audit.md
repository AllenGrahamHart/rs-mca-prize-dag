  base-field root is assumed.
- `P_cl(gamma_0)` is nonzero because `gamma_0` is the exceptional, not a clean,
  supported slope.

  pointwise polynomial argument.

  this is the load-bearing part of the divisibility contradiction.

  `Z_B` branch.

  and zero exceptional traces.
