# sov_forced_root_correctness

- **status:** CONDITIONAL
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

The generalized forced square-root recursion correctly produces the
obstruction vector for every `h` in the intended band, and every active
`h`-core forces the relevant obstruction coordinates to vanish.

## Falsifier

An active core in the intended `h` band whose forced-root obstruction vector
does not vanish, or a mismatch with the banked h=5/X79 construction under the
generalized recursion.
