# Claim contract

- **claim:** every odd-characteristic canonical `K_4` witness has the
  Grassmann-line form `(KGL2)--(KGL3)` and a nondegenerate constant relation
  `(KGL4)` with all four coefficients nonzero; its four split locators leave
  exactly eight exceptional domain roots.
- **scope:** the `K_4` incidence chamber of the B*=3 split-pencil normal form
  in odd characteristic with `d>=3`; in particular every official row.
- **requirements:** `rate_half_list_budget_three_plucker_edge_gate` and
  `rate_half_list_budget_three_residual_transversal_atlas`.
- **consumer effect:** replaces the six coupled linear edge factors in the
  `K_4` branch by one projective Grassmann line and one nondegenerate
  four-term base-field split-unit equation; no status promotion.
- **nonclaim:** no four-term split-unit exclusion, official predecessor
  safety, or adjacent crossing is proved.
- **falsifier:** an official-compatible `K_4` system whose Plucker line has no
  common vector, whose locator relation has a zero coefficient or proper
  vanishing subsum, or whose exceptional factor has degree other than eight.
