# Audit

- The theorem uses only tight edges `02,03,12,13`; it does not assume that the
  two slack factors `b_01,b_23` are constant.
- The four edge roots are distinct because the incidence blocks partition the
  evaluation domain.
- The ratios in `(CBM1)` are evaluated only on `T_2` or `T_3`, disjoint from
  their denominator edge points.
- "Exactly two" counts degree-`d-1` fully domain-split projective members of
  the fixed pencil. It is not a count of all split locators or all pencils.
- The threshold is `d>=8`: one vote from each Mobius block plus four
  exceptional edge votes gives the elementary cap six.
- The official value `d=2^39` lies in scope.
- No claim is made that a two-fiber/two-graph rational map is absent. The
  theorem replaces the formerly unstructured cycle chamber by that exact
  residual object.
