# Claim contract

- **Claim:** supported exceptional slopes consume both residual domain-root
  incidence and Kronecker multiplicity, giving `(ERC1)`, `(ERC2)`, and the
  contracted-core strengthening `(ERC1R)`.
- **Scope:** syndrome Hankel pencils in the first-transition range
  `r<=R/2`, with generic rank `rho>=1`, moving apolar generator degree
  `e>=1`, and the fixed-factor notation of
  `rate_half_ca_hankel_minimal_index_budget`.
- **Dependencies:** the split-locator equivalence supplies the supported
  degree-`r` locator; the minimal-index theorem supplies the common apolar
  generator, residual root count, and regular size `delta=rho-Ae`.
- **Consumer:** the two terminal moving-kernel profiles in
  `rate_half_band_closure`.
- **Nonclaims:** this does not classify the remaining core-free high-degree
  `A=3` pencils, prove their dyadic split-fiber cap, close the residual
  `A=1` profiles in `(ERC6)`, or determine the rate-half crossing above the
  first CA-only transition.
- **Finite/projective convention:** projective root incidence is used only as
  an upper resource.  The conclusion counts finite slopes, so a boundary
  root cannot invalidate the bound.
