# Result

Maxwell core. Their active block family is always proper and lies at least
two incidence dimensions below the Maxwell density threshold. Conversely,
every printed deficit bound is sharp for abstract pair-cap-four block set
systems, so pairwise overlap accounting cannot exclude the proper local
branch.

The projected-rank census therefore splits cleanly into proper rank-two
local circuits and full-core anomalies of trade rank at least three. Paying
the former still requires source-specific first-match ownership or
divided-difference incompatibility.
