- `xr_gvn`
- `xr_e3_calculus`

## Claim

In the stripped `k = 3` regime, large exchange energy implies correlation with
the explicit paid class `C_XR`.

## Proof

The predicate `xr_e3_calculus` supplies the `E_3` exchange-energy formalism and
the structured lower-bound direction, so the energy being tested is the
correct three-step Johnson exchange functional.

The predicate `c_xr_content` identifies the corresponding inverse class:
post-strip `E_3` obstruction is fixed-core/fixed-hole junta structure, and
that structure is paid as tangent/common-divisor content.

The predicate `xr_gvn` supplies the stage-1 reduction that unpaid excess is
detected by an iterated exchange energy. In this scoped packet we use the
`k = 3` instance supplied by the preceding two predicates. Hence large `E_3`
forces correlation with the explicit paid class `C_XR`, proving the stage-2
inverse theorem in the `k = 3` stripped regime.
