# Claim contract

- **Claim:** a column-far Hankel pencil with a constant generic right kernel
  has at most `rho` split-supported finite slopes, where `rho` is its generic
  rank.
- **Quantifiers:** every RS syndrome Hankel pencil satisfying `(HK1)` with
  `rho>=1`, over every field and evaluation domain covered by the Hankel
  equivalence.
- **Dependency:** `rate_half_ca_hankel_split_pencil_equivalence` for the CA
  interpretation; the constant-kernel and minor argument is elementary.
- **Consumer:** the generically rank-deficient branch of the first
  post-quadratic rate-half CA-only range, including its half-distance endpoint.
- **Nonclaims:** no assertion for a genuinely moving generic kernel, and no
  classification asserting that every rank-deficient Hankel pencil has a
  constant kernel.
- **Falsifier:** a column-far pencil satisfying `(HK1)` with more than `rho`
  finite slopes carrying a domain-split degree-`r` locator.
