# Claim contract

- **claim:** every quadratic B*=3 chamber at `d>=4` has the coprime,
  base-field-direct module `(QPM1)--(QPM4)` with the exact generator and
  exceptional degrees printed in `(QPM2)` and `(QPM4)`.
- **scope:** all three pendant and the quadratic `K_4-e` chamber in odd
  characteristic; all official rows included.
- **requirement:**
  `rate_half_list_budget_three_quadratic_scroll_full_rank`.
- **consumer effect:** proves the four quadratic chambers are genuinely
  four-dimensional and reduces them to primitive two-generator
  four-moving-target subgroup factorizations; no status promotion.
- **nonclaim:** no such primitive factorization is excluded and no ordinary
  list safe point or adjacent crossing is proved.
- **falsifier:** a common factor of `alpha,beta`, a different generator
  degree, a constant dependence among the four module entries, or failure of
  `(QPM4)`.
