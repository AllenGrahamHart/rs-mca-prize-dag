# Result

The four quadratic B*=3 chambers are genuine coprime direct-sum modules.
Pendant generators have degrees `(d-2,d-1)` and the quadratic `K_4-e`
generators have degrees `(d-2,d-2)`. Their four locator factors are
independent affine-linear combinations of those generators and multiply,
up to four or six exceptional roots, to the official subgroup polynomial.
