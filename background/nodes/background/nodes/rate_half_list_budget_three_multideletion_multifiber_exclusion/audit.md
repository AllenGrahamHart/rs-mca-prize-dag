# Audit

The locators can have different degrees. The factor
`z^(ell_i-ell_0)` in `(3)` is load-bearing; omitting it would prove a false
same-degree statement.

The threshold is strict: `m>L-ell_0`. At equality below the printed threshold,
the truncated series need not determine the rational numerator. No small-fiber
case is inferred by continuity or by power-of-two rounding.

The deleted-root sets are pairwise disjoint in the consumer because the
exceptional coordinates are partitioned among completed omitted blocks. The
abstract theorem states this hypothesis explicitly.

The application uses only the nine chambers where the Grassmann atlas already
proves a nondegenerate constant locator relation. The four full-rank quadratic
scrolls have no such relation and are not included.
