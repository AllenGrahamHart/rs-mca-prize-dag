# Audit

The common factor in `(4)` is the load-bearing use of the official completed
block degree. Rank three does not by itself contradict a relation of small
arbitrary polynomials; it contradicts four huge equal-degree completed blocks
whose quotients are pairwise coprime.

The theorem deliberately stops at residue rank two. An exact census over
`F_13` finds `50,856` rank-four, `1,104` rank-three, and `15` rank-two
pairings of eight distinct nonzero roots into four quadratics. All `15`
rank-two pairings are antipodal. This is evidence for an antipodal
classification, not a proof over arbitrary official fields.

No exhaustive official-field computation is needed for the proved gate. The
larger symbolic component-classification task is recorded separately in
`notes/PRIZE_COMPUTE_REQUESTS.md`.
