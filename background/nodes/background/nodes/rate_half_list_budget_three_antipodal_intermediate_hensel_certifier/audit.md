# Audit

The terminal equation is linear in `u` only because it is extracted below
degree `2h`. Terms quadratic in the shifted block begin exactly at `2h` and
must be retained in the complete Hensel reconstruction.

The branch `Delta=kappa=0` is not declared impossible or unique. It retains
one scalar until later polynomiality, full-identity, splitting, and Möbius
checks.

The cube root and every Hensel solution are normalized by constant term one.
The removed scalar is precisely the allowed scaling of the degree-drop pencil
direction.

An implementation must verify polynomiality by an exact multiplied identity;
observing a long zero prefix in a truncated formal solution is not sufficient.
