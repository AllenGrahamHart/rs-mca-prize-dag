# Claim contract - PMA sigma-one variable-defect exact-hit floor

## Claim

The collision-free exact-hit construction has a rigorous variable-defect
first moment and yields superpolynomial post-owner growth at sigma one on an
infinite polynomial-field family.

## Serves

- `petal_mixed_amplification`: forbids a uniform polynomial claim without its
  reserve hypothesis and supplies a mandatory finite lower floor;
- `imgfib`: proves only a scope guard, not a falsifier under its lower cutoff;
- finite adjacent ledgers: rules out any return to a fixed `n^6`-style
  allocation for all sigma-one rows.

## Nonclaims

No reserve-scale counterexample, quotient-profile classification, safe/unsafe
adjacent crossing, or complete upper ledger is claimed.
