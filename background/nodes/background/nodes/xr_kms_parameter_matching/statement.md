# xr_kms_parameter_matching

- **status:** CONDITIONAL
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

The XR inverse route is parameter-compatible at FM-scale densities conditional
on `xr_globalness_from_ledger` and `xr_small_set_engine`: the post-strip ledger
gives polynomial globalness, and the KLLM engine loses only polynomially in
that globalness parameter, so the result remains `n^B * FM`.
