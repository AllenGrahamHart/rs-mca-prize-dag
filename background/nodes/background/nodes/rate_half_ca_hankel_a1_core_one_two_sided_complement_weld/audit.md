# Audit

- `c` is the number of nonsaturated dominant-component domain rows, not the
  total deficit `C_*`; only `c<=C_*` is claimed.
- Since `D_*<=1`, the number of nonclean supported slopes equals `D_*`; no
  analogous equality is asserted between `c` and `C_*`.
- The slope-side quotient `A_gamma=G_X/Q_gamma` is interpolated only on clean
  fibers where divisibility and squarefreeness are proved.
- Coprimality of `Q` and `P_X` follows from positive parameter degree and
  irreducibility; it is not inferred from pointwise nonvanishing.
- Signs in `(TSW7),(TSW8)` are fixed by the chosen quotient sign in `(TSW4)`.
- `K` may have large bidegree within `(TSW9)`. Calling the weld bounded does
  not mean it is computationally small at the official row.
- The low-defect factor is `B_XE_Z`, not either interpolated quotient.
- No stratum exclusion is claimed.
