# Audit

The quotient and remainder are taken after the constant ODE has generated the
unique possible monic `U_0`. Treating coefficients of `U_0` or `V_0` as free
variables would undo the proved compression.

The nonharmonic criterion checks both the exact remainder identity and that
`S/(1+q)` is a polynomial square. A fourth-power remainder alone does not
identify the correct square-pencil direction.

The harmonic branch is kept separate because division by `1+q` is invalid.
There the cross term vanishes and the residual must be a fourth power, not
merely a square.

The equivalence concerns the complete factorization after the selected
Möbius-ratio branch. It does not assert that a bounded prefix or modular
factor sample certifies the official polynomial identity.
