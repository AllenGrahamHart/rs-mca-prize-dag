# Audit

- Used the exact pure degree `deg V=r-1` and the exact residual degree
  `deg T=r`; neither was inferred from a generic upper bound.
- Checked the leading coefficient of `C`: it is `-4` times the leading
  coefficient of `V`, so `deg C=r+3` in the official characteristic.
- Proved the cubic Euler identity in both directions. The converse uses
  monicity, `deg D(U^4+e_4V^4)=d`, and invertibility of every
  `1,...,d` in the field.
- Derived `L` from exact divisibility and degree, not from a generic
  ramification assertion.
- Kept internal overlaps `T cap U` and `C cap V` possible, but proved
  that every such defect is confined to the sole root of `L`.
- Did not infer emptiness from having at most three finite critical values;
  low-critical-value polynomials exist and still require classification.
