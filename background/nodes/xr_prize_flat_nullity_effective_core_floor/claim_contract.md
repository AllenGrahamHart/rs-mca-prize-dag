# Claim contract

- **claim:** every over-budget prize P-A flat-nullity cell has effective core
  `a+u+v` above a printed million-scale floor
- **scope:** post-strip high-core P-A selected errors at prize rates, after
  deleting their persistent common-root set
- **output:** effective-core floors `11243370,9629972,2241633`; at the first
  open ranks this forces `u+v>=11243354,9629956,2241619`
- **currency:** an exact-size residual zero-set family with pairwise
  intersection cap, followed by a minimal dense-core private-point argument
- **nonclaim:** no count above the floor, no P-B theorem, and no aggregate
  cross-cell payment
- **falsifier:** one scoped over-budget cell below `(EF2)`, or a failure of
  the persistent-root size/intersection dictionary used in the proof
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
