# Claim contract

- **claim:** every complete canonical one-antipodal `c=2` candidate has four
  equal-sized root cells with the exact Fourier annihilation ladder
  `(CFL4)--(CFL7)` and the negation dichotomy `(CFL9)--(CFL10)`.
- **scope:** the official maximal `c=2` boundary after primary, secondary,
  canonical-span, split-outer, and one-antipodal normalization gates.
- **dependencies:** the transferred canonical factorization, the normalized
  `c=2` compiler, and the exact one-antipodal normalization.
- **falsifier:** a passing canonical factorization whose cell degrees,
  partition, printed moments, or Vandermonde support bounds fail.
- **nonclaims:** no canonical coloring is classified or excluded; no
  primary-only, antipodal-free, or full adjacent-crossing closure is claimed.
