# Audit

- Nonproportional nonconstant affine forms have distinct roots; proportional
  forms are deliberately out of scope because their intersection can have
  size `n`.
- The two fixed powers `alpha,beta` in `(1)` need not agree. The Stepanov
  coefficient count only uses that each is fixed on the selected fiber.
- The strict bound uses `p>=n^2+1`, which follows from `p>=n^2` and
  `p=1 mod n`.
- Constant `4` is a one-fiber optimization. Replacing `66` by `4` in the
  existing multi-coset or energy theorem would be invalid.
