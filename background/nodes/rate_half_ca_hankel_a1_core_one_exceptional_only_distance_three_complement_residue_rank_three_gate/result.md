# Result

Every surviving distance-three external design has a three-dimensional
complement image in `F[z]/(I)`. This replaces a generic family of `6e+3`
degree-`2e` complement locators by a rank-three residue packet. Exact
biregularity alone does not imply the restriction. Even before the internal
slopes are supplied, the full complement locators must span dimension at
most `e+4`.
