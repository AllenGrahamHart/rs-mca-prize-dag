# Audit

- The half-distance matrix has right nullity two and left nullity one. The
  coefficient independence proof uses the unique left block; it does not
  reuse the strict branch's unique-right-block statement without checking the
  transposed dimensions.
- A clean `Q_gamma` has degree `rho`, while the supported locator has degree
  `rho+1`. The theorem calls `Q_gamma` a split factor, not the full locator.
- `d_x` counts distinct finite supported slopes. Boundary roots consume
  degree but only increase the printed capacity deficit.
- Exact matrix rank is asserted under failure, where `T>=rho+3>e`.
- The `h=0` complementary form `Vbar` is interpolated and is not claimed to
  be a second apolar generator.
- Component chambers are necessary conditions, not existence certificates.
- The `e=m+1` corollary uses the worst allowed `h=3`; all four strata retain
  a positive saturation guarantee.
- No numerical experiment is used in the proof.
