# Claim contract

- **claim:** prize-row proper rank-two circuits, and hence all prize-row
  rank-two trades, are absent below explicit affine-rank floors
- **scope:** uniform split-pencil minimal Maxwell cores at the three prize
  rates; proper circuits have four or five active rows
- **output:** proper-circuit floors
  `11265488,9646193,2243389` and the same low-rank exclusion for every
  rank-two trade after composition with the full-core floor
- **currency:** multiplicity-one outside extension points and the number of
  host blocks available to absorb their privacy
- **nonclaim:** no existence or count above the floor; no higher trade-rank or
  nonuniform payment
- **falsifier:** a scoped proper circuit below `(PF4)`, a rank-two prize trade
  below `(PF5)`, or an extension family violating `(PF2)`
- **consumer:** `xr_highcore_collision_count`
