# Result

Every prize-row rank-two trade is absent below affine ranks

```text
11,265,488;       9,646,193;       2,243,389
```

at rates `1/4,1/8,1/16`. The new ingredient is a private-point lower bound
for arbitrary proper-circuit extension multiplicities, composed with the
minimal host-core private-point cap. Below these floors the uniform XR
compiler may discard rank two entirely and retain only trade rank at least
three. Higher ranks and the aggregate slope count remain open.
