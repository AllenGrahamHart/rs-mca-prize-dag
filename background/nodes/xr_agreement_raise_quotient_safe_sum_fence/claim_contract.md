# Claim contract

- **claim:** the standard quotient-remainder support safe sum over active cells
  `B-k<c` is valid across every raised agreement, but one active summand already
  exceeds `B*` on each clean row.
- **scope:** the six `(RowC, prize) x (1/4,1/8,1/16)` XR candidates.
- **currency:** support-union upper bounds for distinct support-wise bad
  slopes; this is not an attained slope count.
- **consumer effect:** rules out generic support enumeration as the quotient
  payment inside the agreement-raising induction.
- **required successor:** distinct-slope image/lcm coalescing across support
  sizes, threshold-coherent first-match ownership, or the static global
  pair-list route.
- **nonclaims:** no quotient-union lower bound, no counterexample, no generic
  chart payment, and no mismatch-bridge close.
- **falsifier:** a displayed witness fails `A<=B<=n` or `B-k<c`, its summand is
  at most `B*`, or one fixed support owns two distinct noncontained line
  slopes.
