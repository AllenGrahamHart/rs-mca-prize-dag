# Claim contract

- **claim:** nonidentity `P>=25` is equivalent to solvability of the printed
  ordered-root quadratic system with exact size `50s+328` by `50s+352`.
- **scope:** every dyadic split row `p=1 mod 2^s`.
- **dependency:** the proved degree-25 divisor-tower equivalence.
- **falsifier:** a row where solvability and direct `P>=25` disagree, a
  missing inverse/distinctness condition, or a wrong system-size count.
- **certificate:** a modular solution falsifies P24; a checked integer
  unit-ideal identity gives a complete characteristic outer set.
- **nonclaims:** no symmetry-breaking theorem, efficient solver, certificate,
  resource estimate, official P24 theorem, DSP8 promotion, or C36' close is
  asserted.
