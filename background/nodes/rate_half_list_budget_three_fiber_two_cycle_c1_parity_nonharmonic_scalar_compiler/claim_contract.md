# Claim contract

- **claim:** every generic two-antipodal `c=1` parity survivor, and only such
  a survivor, passes one of six outer-trace scalar and twisted-fourth-power
  branches; every accepted branch also passes the printed constant and gcd
  gates.
- **scope:** the official maximal-row, generic, normalized `c=1` parity
  chamber with two antipodal denominator pairs.
- **dependencies:** parity Mobius routing, harmonic exclusion, signed
  even-factorization and field collapse, the constant ODE, and the
  constant-coefficient Legendre formula.
- **falsifier:** a scoped complete square pencil missed by all six packets, a
  packet accepted without a reconstructible square pencil and Mobius match,
  dependence of the twisted fourth-power verdict on the reciprocal root, or
  failure of either necessary first-rejection gate.
- **nonclaims:** no branch is uniformly excluded, no official-degree
  compressed implementation is supplied, and neither nonparity `c=1` nor
  `c=2` is covered.
