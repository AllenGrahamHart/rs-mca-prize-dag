# Audit

1. Branch values are labelled. Swapping `0` and `1` is not part of the
   equivalence relation.
2. Plane embeddings are oriented. Burnside divides by rotations, not by
   reflections.
3. There is no factor `2^h`: the two white leaves at a trivalent black branch
   are indistinguishable, so reversing them does not create another cyclic
   datum.
4. The positive-characteristic use is tame and prime-to-`p`. The hypothesis
   `p>m` also makes the monodromy group order, which divides `m!`, prime to
   `p`.
5. The theorem counts geometric source-scaling classes. A consumer must still
   prove that its arithmetic orbit relation is no finer.
6. `(CSN1)` counts all compatible trees, including unrealizable ones; this
   only enlarges the upper bound.
