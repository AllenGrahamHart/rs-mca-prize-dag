# Result

Every official exact-shell locator prefix, at arbitrary depth, is exactly a
mixed Q prefix made of p-free power sums and at most 23 elementary Frobenius
checkpoints. Small characteristic no longer creates an untyped L1 tail; the
remaining theorem may use direct flatness of the larger coarse p-free fiber,
which transfers with no checkpoint loss, or uniform conditional flatness of
the mixed prefix together with collective Pade-graph coalescing. A raw
checkpoint union costs less than `n^453` qualitatively but can never pay the
finite `q/2^128` threshold.
