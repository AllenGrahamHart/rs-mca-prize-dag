# Audit

1. The torsion assertion uses the leading coefficients of the split cell
   factors, not the outer roots `w_i` themselves.
2. The sign in `ell_i=-product_(a in A_i)a` uses odd `r`; it does not affect
   membership in `mu_N` because `-1 in mu_N`.
3. `O_inf(0)=P_src^(-1)` follows from `lc(E)=-P_src`; the two minus signs in
   the quotient leading coefficient cancel.
4. The repeated-squaring endpoint is `R_40=1`, not `R_40=X`.
5. Affine scaling preserves equality of derivative weights but scales all
   reciprocal weights by the same nonzero scalar.
6. `J_inf=0` applies only to the selected-antipodal shard.  The checked
   order-32 subgroup of `F_97` contains an explicit non-antipodal `J=0`
   quartet with exactly one derivative collision; no universal antipodal
   classification is claimed.
7. No dense canonical polynomial, subgroup list, or paid computation is
   authorized.
