# Claim contract

- **claim:** the leading coefficients of the four canonical cells form a
  squarefree four-root divisor of `X^N-1`; the outer derivative collision
  and selected-antipodal `J=0` condition persist under this affine map.
- **scope:** complete canonical one-antipodal `c=2` packets with minimum
  barycentric support.
- **dependencies:** canonical cell splitting, the minimum-support degree
  conclusion, and the proved one-pair collision branch compiler.
- **consumer:** the minimum-support branch of
  `rate_half_list_adjacent_crossing`.
- **falsifier:** a covered packet whose infinity quartic fails `(ICT2)`,
  `(ICT4)`, `(ICT5)`, or the applicable specialization `(ICT6)`.
- **nonclaims:** no subgroup-quartet classification or official-degree
  coefficient evaluator is supplied, and passage of the gate is not a
  complete candidate certificate.
