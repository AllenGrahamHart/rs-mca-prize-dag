# Audit - L1 affine split-pencil cross-determinant uniqueness

## Checked axes

1. Equal support equations kill the cross determinant on every `S_i`.
2. Equal missing syndromes do not kill it on `V_i`; the mark factor `J` is
   required there.
3. Pairwise disjoint petals make their full locators coprime.
4. The strict degree inequality, not a weak inequality, forces vanishing.
5. Saturation and equal monic degree turn cross-ratio equality into equality
   of pairs.
6. The exact cell threshold uses `v`; replacing it by `p` is only the safe
   aggregate sufficient condition.
7. The result is per source chart and does not pay chart multiplicity.

## Remaining attack

Count split/saturated points inside the width window `(CD7)`, preferably by a
base-field-normalized split-pencil or signed-profile owner, and separately
bound canonical non-intrinsic first-match source charts.
