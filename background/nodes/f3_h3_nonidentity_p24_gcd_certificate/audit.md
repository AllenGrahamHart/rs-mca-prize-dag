# Audit

1. The complete `T=1` valuation is removed from `G_12`; merely dividing once
   is unsound when the identity fiber has multiplicity above 13.
2. The diagonal gcd is not optional. `U=13,D=2` gives `P=24`, whereas
   `U=13,D=1` gives `P=25`.
3. Hasse derivatives, not factorial-normalized ordinary derivatives, are
   used. The bound `U(t)<p` makes their local multiplicities exact.
4. Divisibility includes multiplicity. A root-set inclusion would miss
   `U>=14`, for which `G_12` has multiplicity at least two while `H_D` has
   multiplicity at most one.
5. The criterion concerns all nonidentity product targets. This is stronger
   than checking only targets with positive quotient weight and is exactly
   the vacuous DSP8 satellite.
6. The theorem is a checker contract, not evidence that an official row
   satisfies the divisibility.
