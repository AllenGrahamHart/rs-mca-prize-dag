   the non-strict mutation.

   load-bearing.

   elementary-prefix equality is assumed.
5. The compact bound uses `min(d+1,p)` and is then maximized with the original
   half-depth floor.
6. The official `p>n/24` conclusion is used only when `d>=p`.
7. The improved packing cap remains an upper bound, not an existence claim.
