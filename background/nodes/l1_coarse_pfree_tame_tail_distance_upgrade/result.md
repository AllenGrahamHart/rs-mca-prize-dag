# Result

The apparent half-depth collisions below the characteristic do not exist.
Every coarse p-free collision has width at least
`max(ceil((d+2)/2),min(d+1,p))`. Hence the ordinary Newton window has the
full `d+1` distance, while every official checkpoint-depth collision
exchanges at least `p>n/24` roots. The `F_5` fixture attains the tame bound;
the `F_4` fixture pins the strict endpoint.
