# aqb_box_charge_amortization

- **status:** CONDITIONAL
- **closure:** proof

## Statement

For the averaged `c=2` quotient-box family, the shared box charge amortizes by
at least `429,645,547` bits relative to the single-word floor at `sigma*`.

## Falsifier

A certified upper bound showing that the proposed averaged family saves less
than `429,645,547` bits after all multiplicities, overlaps, and quotient/fiber
normalizations are charged.
