# Audit

- `N_x` removes one copy of every distinct supported root. Repeated supported
  roots remain in `N_x` and are correctly charged to `delta_x`.
- The norm comparison is only up to a nonzero scalar, matching the
  normalization ambiguity of the component factor.
- If both `H_x,J_x` vanish, the factor is handled through `H_x=0`; no second
  domain allocation is needed.
- The apparent `J_x=0` branch is inconsistent: `Qhat_x|E_Z` itself forces
  `epsilon_x=1`, raising `deg Qhat_x` to at least two.
- `Qhat_0|B_X` is asserted only in the `H_0!=0,J_0=0` exceptional branch.
- Squarefreeness and degree `r-1` of `q_0` follow from divisibility into
  squarefree `G_X` plus the already-proved count of `r-1` distinct roots.
- Every cancellation in `(TFA7)` is by a nonzero polynomial factor already
  proved to divide both sides.
- The reduced complement square remains open.
