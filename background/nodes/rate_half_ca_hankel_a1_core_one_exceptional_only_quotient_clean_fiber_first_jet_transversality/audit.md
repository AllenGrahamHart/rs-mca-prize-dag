# Audit

1. The derivative is taken before specialization; terms containing `F` or
   `P_cl` then vanish at a clean selected incidence.
2. `E(gamma)` is nonzero because clean and exceptional slope factors are
   coprime.
3. `U(gamma,y)` is nonzero because the clean fiber is a squarefree divisor
   of squarefree `R_X` and `U` is its complementary divisor.
4. Multiplicative-domain roots are nonzero, so the right side of `(QJT4)`
   cannot vanish.
5. The endpoint resultant matrix records only aggregate incidences; it does
   not contain the root velocities in `(QJT5)`.
6. Reversal removes exactly `s` and `x_0`; no additional domain point is
   deleted in `(QJT6)`.
7. `M` is nonzero in the field because the multiplicative domain order
   divides `|F|-1`.
8. The corrected-square exponent is `N_sq=M+r-3`, not the domain order;
   reducing `y^(N_sq+1)` modulo `y^M=1` gives `y^(r-2)`.
