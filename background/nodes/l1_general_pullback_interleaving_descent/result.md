# Result

Every arbitrary monic polynomial pullback has a canonical interleaved
quotient representation. Fully fiberwise agreement reduces to a common-
support quotient list with exact multiplier `q^kappa`; for a complete domain
partition, `kappa=0`, and sub-square-root interleaving collapses to the
ordinary quotient list. The remaining L1 work is an ordinary-list/payment
theorem on realized label domains plus partial-fiber and sparse-coverage
control, not raw enumeration of fiber roles.
