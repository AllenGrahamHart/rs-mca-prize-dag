# Lineage - L1 general polynomial-pullback interleaving descent

`cyclic_fiber_interleaving_descent` handles intrinsic monomial quotient
fibers. The tame refinement census exposed non-intrinsic polynomial maps, and
the periodic-owner obstruction showed that their exact supports need not be
multiplicatively stable. This node replaces cyclic symmetry by the general
free-module basis `1,X,...,X^(s-1)` over `K[P]` and records the exact kernel
cost when only part of the domain lies in complete fibers.
